# Awọn ohun-ini Zcash Shielded

Zcash Shielded Assets (ZSA) jẹ atunṣe ti a dabaa si ilana Zcash ti yoo jẹ ki ẹda, gbigbe, ati sisun awọn ohun-ini aṣa lori pq Zcash.

Bí o bá mọ̀ nípa [ERC-20](https://ethereum.org/en/developers/docs/standards/tokens/erc-20/) Àkọsílẹ̀ ìpamọ́ lórí ẹ̀ka-ìpínlẹ̀ Ethereum, ZSAs jẹ́ fún Zcash gẹ́gẹ́ bí àwọn àmì ERC-20 ṣe jẹ́ láti Ethereum.

Zcash Shielded Assets yoo jẹ ki ẹda ti awọn ami aṣa lori blockchain Zcash, nitorinaa gbigba awọn ami miiran ju [ZEC](https://wiki.zechub.xyz/using-zec-privately) láti jàǹfààní nínú àìdánimọ̀ àti ìpamọ́ àwọn ìsòwò tí a fi ààbò bo lórí ẹ̀rọ-ìdánilẹ́kọ̀ọ́ Zcash.

A major potential use of ZSAs would be for the issuing of stablecoins on the Zcash protocol. Stablecoins are cryptocurrencies that peg their value to a fiat currency, such as the US Dollar or Euro. Currently, some of the most widely circulated stablecoins are ERC-20 tokens such as [USDC](https://www.circle.com/en/usdc) àti [Dai](https://docs.makerdao.com/).

Another potential use of ZSAs would be for the issuing of governance tokens. For example, Zechub (the publisher of this wiki) is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) and could create and issue to its members a ZSA for the purpose of voting on proposals and governance decisions.

ZSAs ti wa ni idagbasoke nipa [QEDIT](https://qed-it.com/), labẹ kan pataki iranlowo lati awọn [Zcash Foundation](https://wiki.zechub.xyz/zcash-foundation) ni ifọwọsowọpọ pẹlu [Electric Coin Company](https://wiki.zechub.xyz/electric-coin-company)Bi ise agbese yi ti wa ni ṣi actively ni idagbasoke, awọn imudojuiwọn ti o wa ni atejade lori [yi asọ](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/grant-update-zcash-shielded-assets-monthly-updates/41153) of the Zcash forum. The [ZSA grant application](https://zcashgrants.org/gallery/25215916-53ea-4041-a3b2-6d00c487917d/33106640/) nipasẹ QEDIT wa lati oju opo wẹẹbu awọn ẹbun Zcash Foundation.

## Àmọ̀ràn Ìtìlẹ́yìn ZSA

Àbá ZSA fún Àwọn Ohun Ìní Ààbò (ZSA/UDA) ni [QEDIT] gbé kalẹ̀](https://qed-it.com/) team to build generic shielded assets on the Zcash blockchain. These are usually referred to as User Defined Assets (UDA) or as Zcash Shielded Assets (ZSA).

pẹ̀lú àbá yìí, ẹgbẹ́ [QEDIT]](https://qed-it.com/) plans to bring DeFi to the Zcash ecosystem, and at the same time enable the use of the best privacy technology within the existing DeFi ecosystem with regards to a pool survey, where the team asked, and the community answered: [generic shielded assets (ZSA/UDA) are the most requested feature at the moment](https://twitter.com/BenarrochDaniel/status/1428327864034791429)

Àwọn àbá wọ̀nyí bá àwọn àbá tó wà nínú [Zcash Improvement Proposal (ZIP) ] mu.](https://zips.z.cash/zip-0000) Àkọsílẹ̀ àti a ṣe àlàyé wọn nínú ZIP 226 & ZIP227.

1. [ZIP 226](https://qed-it.github.io/zips/zip-0226): gbigbe ati sisun awọn ohun-ini ti o ni aabo Zcash
2. [ZIP 227](https://qed-it.github.io/zips/zip-0227): Ifisilẹ Awọn ohun-ini Ideri Zcash
