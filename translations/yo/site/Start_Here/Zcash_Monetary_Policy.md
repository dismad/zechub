# Àwọn Ìlànà Zcash

## Kí ni ètò ọrọ̀ ajé ti Zcash?

Ìdásílẹ̀ owó ti Zcash jẹ́ bákan náà pẹ̀lú ìpèsè Bitcoin tí ó dúró digbí ti 21 mílíọ̀nù ZEC currency units.](https://zcash.readthedocs.io/en/latest/rtd_pages/glossary.html#:~:text=Block,mempool%20in%20an%20unconfirmed%20state.) ti wa ni mined si awọn Zcash blockchain ati ki o kan block èrè ti 3.125 ZEC ba wa sinu kaakiri.](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1014).

The amount of the block reward cuts in half about every four years until all 21 million ZEC are in circulation. Zcash inflation almost precisely mimics that of Bitcoin. It is important to note that as new coins are created inflation goes down, and at each halvening the rate drops significantly.
