# Àwọn Ìlànà Zcash

## Awọn Ọran Lilo ZEC

#### Àwọn ìsanwó

People use ZEC to transact efficiently and safely with low fees. Shielded Zcash ensures transactions remain confidential, while also giving them the option to share address and transaction information should they choose to. Private, "Z-Z" transactions appear on the public blockchain so it is known that they occurred and that fees were paid. But, the addresses, transaction amount and memo field are all encrypted and not publicly visible. 

#### Ìpamọ́ Iyebíye

Awọn eniyan tun le lo ZEC lati fi ọrọ pamọ sinu ohun-ini lile, ti o daabobo aṣiri. Awọn ẹya ara ẹrọ ZEC miliọnu 21 nikan ni yoo wa lailai, itumo pe dukia naa ni ipese titilai. Ni kete ti a ba wa 21 milionuth ZEC [ti mined sinu iyipo](https://z.cash/mining-zcash/), the asset will become anti-inflationary. Anti-inflationary assets are a good hedge against inflation in the event that centralized gatekeepers inflate national money supplies.

#### Ìsọfúnni

Awọn eniyan le lo aaye akọsilẹ ni iṣowo Z-Z ti o ni aabo lati firanṣẹ awọn ifiranṣẹ ti a fi pamọ patapata. Lati firanšẹ akọsilẹ kan, awọn olumulo gbọdọ ṣiṣe iṣowo kan ki o san owo-owo iṣowo kan, eyiti o kere ju $ 0.01.
