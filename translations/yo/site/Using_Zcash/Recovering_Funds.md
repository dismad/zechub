# Gbigba pada Owo apo apamọwọ Zcash

** Kí nìdí tó fi yẹ kó o ní kókó ìkọ̀kọ̀ rẹ? ** 

Awọn bọtini ikọkọ ni aṣiri si aabo awọn ohun-ini oni-nọmba rẹ. Pa wọn mọ ki o ma ṣe pin wọn pẹlu awọn ẹgbẹ kẹta jẹ pataki. 

> Ni idi eyi a le wo ** Seed Phrase ** bi deede ti bọtini ikọkọ kan.

By maintaining control over your private keys, the recovery process is always possible. There are 2 types of Zcash private keys (transparent and shielded), you can easily import them into your wallet, whether by using the Sweep Funds function or importing them as a new account. By keeping control over your private keys, you maintain total control over your assets, ensuring ownership, security and peace of mind.

# Ààbò àti Ojúṣe

It is crucial for users to understand the risks involved in dealing with private keys and to keep these keys protected from unauthorized access. The security of funds depends on the user's responsibility to safeguard their private keys.

## Gbigba owo pada pẹlu Ywallet

A mọ YWallet gẹgẹbi ọkan ninu awọn aṣayan ti o dara julọ fun gbigba awọn owo ti ko ni iraye si, mejeeji lati *transparent nikan* ati awọn bọtini ikọkọ ti o ni aabo.

### 1) Àkọsílẹ̀ Àkọlé Ìmúlò 

1. Ṣe igbasilẹ Ywallet[](https://ywallet.app)

2. Lọgan ti ṣii, ni isalẹ ọtun tẹ 'More'

3. Yan 'Àwọn Àkáǹtì'

4. Ni igun apa ọtun oke tẹ ami afikun 

[Àmì ìdìbò àfikún]](https://i.postimg.cc/xJbVz7gB/plus.png)

5. Tẹ 'Tún àkáǹtì kan ṣe' 

6. Tẹ gbólóhùn Àkọlé tàbí kókó Ìkọ̀kọ̀

> **Àkíyèsí**: Bí o bá ní owó nínú àpamọ́ tí kì í ṣe ìtìlẹ́yìn fún àwọn àdírẹ́sì tí a fi ààbò bo (Trust, Coinomi, Guarda àti bẹ́ẹ̀bẹ́è) o ní láti lo iṣẹ́ 'Sweep Funds'.

### 2) Àwọn Owó Ìdókòwò

1. Ṣe igbasilẹ Ywallet[](https://ywallet.app)

2. Lọgan ti ṣii, ni isalẹ ọtun tẹ 'More'

3. Yi lọ si isalẹ si awọn irinṣẹ apakan, tẹ 'Sweep'

4. Tẹ gbolohun ọrọ irugbin rẹ (Awọn iyasọtọ aaye fun awọn adirẹsi afikun ti a ṣẹda nipasẹ irugbine)

[Yọ́jú Àwòrán Ìṣúná!](https://i.postimg.cc/3055CBcN/sweep.png)

5. Tẹ Àkójọ Iye fún ibi tí o fẹ́ lo (Àwọn ilé-ìtajà ń lo Transparent)

6. Fi Adirẹsi Ibi-afẹde wọlé fún ibi tí o fẹ́ fi owó sí.
