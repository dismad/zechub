# Gbígba Zcash

## TL;DR

- Ra ZEC lori paṣipaarọ ti o ṣe atilẹyin ** awọn yiyọ kuro ni aabo ** (Gemini, BitcoinVN, Flyp.me) nigbati o ba ṣeeṣe
- Pupọ awọn paṣipaarọ aarin pataki (Coinbase, Kraken, Binance) ṣe atilẹyin ** awọn yiyọ kuro lasan ** nikan  daabobo ZEC rẹ lẹhin gbigba rẹ
- Awọn paṣipaarọ ti a ko ni idojukọ gba awọn paṣipọ ti ko ni idaduro ṣugbọn ni deede yọkuro si awọn adirẹsi ṣiṣan
- O kò nílò láti ra ZEC kíkún  àwọn ìyókù máa ń ṣiṣẹ́ dáadáa

---

In order to use Zcash in private (shielded) transactions, you need to have ZEC. To get ZEC, you'll need to buy it. You can buy ZEC from a number of different exchanges, which we will list below. It is recommended that you use an exchange that supports shielded withdrawals.

O tun le mina ZEC, sugbon ti o jẹ maa ko ṣee ṣe fun apapọ onibara.](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/mining-to-shielded-addresses-community-mining-pool/50281) àti [níbí](https://zec.suprnova.cc/StartMining)_.

## Awọn paṣipaarọ ti o ṣe atilẹyin awọn yiyọ kuro ni aabo

**[Àwọn Ìbejì](https://www.gemini.com)**

Gemini jẹ paṣipaarọ cryptocurrency ti a ṣe ilana, apamọwọ, ati olutọju ti o jẹ ki o rọrun ati ailewu lati ra ZEC, Ether, ati awọn owo-iworo miiran. O le rà ZEC pẹlu fiat tabi awọn owo crypto miiran.

**[BitcoinVN](https://bitcoinvn.io)**

BitcoinVN is a non-custodial instant exchange that supports shielded (z-address) deposits and withdrawals for ZEC — one of very few exchanges globally with full z-address support. Operating since 2014, you can swap BTC, ETH, USDT, and other cryptocurrencies for ZEC directly to your shielded wallet.

**[Flyp.me]](https://flyp.me)**

Flyp.me is a no sign-up crypto exchange. It was one of the safest and most private ways to exchange 30+ cryptocurrencies straight to your wallet. You can buy ZEC with other cryptocurrencies.

## Àwọn Àṣìṣe Tó Wà Lọ́pọ̀lọpọ̀ Tó Yẹ Kó O Yẹra fún

- **Gbigba lati adirẹsi ti o ni imọlẹ** nigbati paṣipaarọ ṣe atilẹyin awọn yiyọ kuro  nigbagbogbo lo adirese z rẹ ti o ba wa
- **Gbigba igbesẹ ideri** lẹhin ti o gba si adirẹsi ti o ni imọlẹ  lo ẹya ara ẹrọ "Awọn Owo Ideri" ti apamọwọ rẹ lẹsẹkẹsẹ
- **Ríra ohun tó pọ̀ ju bó ṣe yẹ lọ ní ìbẹ̀rẹ̀**  Bẹ̀rẹ̀ látorí kékeré kó o lè mọ bí nǹkan ṣe máa ń lọ kí o tó ra ohun tó tóbi
