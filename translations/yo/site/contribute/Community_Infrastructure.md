# Àlàfo Ìpèsè Ìpínlẹ̀ Ìlú ZecHub


### Àwọn Ohun Ìkọ́lé Ilẹ̀-Èdè 

All nodes, lightwalletd instances and other infrastructure is freely accessible and exists to increase developer access to the Zcash network. This allows the ZecHub bounty program to cater to developers in a more free & streamlined alternative to individual tasks on [Dework](https://dework.zechub.org). 


## Ohun elo 

- Fi àmì sí @ZecHubDAOMember nínú ìkànnì #zechub nínú Zcash Global Discord láti bèèrè ààyè. 

- A óo fi ọ kún ikanni kan níbi tí o ti lè rí ìtìlẹ́yìn gbà àti láti pín àtúnṣe. 

- https://discord.gg/zcash

## Àwọn Ìpèsè 

- A nifẹ si ohun ti o ṣe! ZecHub yoo funni ni awọn ẹbun laarin 5-15 ZEC fun iwe aṣẹ ti ohun elo tuntun rẹ. 

- Àwọn àbá díẹ̀ rèé: 

Oju opo wẹẹbu Ere fidio ti a pin pẹlu titẹsi URL ti o nilo isanwo ZEC.

Firanṣẹ Àwọn Àkọsílẹ̀ tí a fi Àkọọ̀rọ̀ pamọ́ láàrin Zcashd Node àti àkọọ́lẹ̀ Farcaster.

Shielded Exchange Service that logs ZEC deposited for assets with reply address, Hedged purchase of other assets made on Ethereum L2.
