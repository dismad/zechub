# Lílo Zcash nínú DeFi


## Àwọn Èrò Tó Sún Mọ́ ọn 

Zcash and NEAR Intents have been integrated, allowing users to swap Zcash (ZEC) with other top altcoins, including Bitcoin, Solana, NEAR, and XRP, without paying any fees. This integration is part of NEAR Protocol's efforts to create an infrastructure of autonomous and verifiable AI bots, which also brings benefits to Zcash by enabling AI-powered payment rails. Zcash users are now capable of accessing smart contracts and wider [DeFi applications](https://nym.com/blog/what-is-defi) nígbà tí wọ́n ń pa àṣírí ara wọn mọ́ nípasẹ̀ [Near Intents](https://app.near-intents.org).

## Ìmúdàgba nípa Zcash DeFi 

**Ohun ti o wa ni Layer 1**

Options are currently being explored to enable DeFi applications within the Zcash ecosystem using the current Layer 1. This could be possible by perofrming most of the contract operations off-chain with a sequencer & have the validation of those actions done on chain. A version of this was created in partnership with JP Morgan on their enterprise blockchain. As of NU5 a mechanism exists (TZE) to add this type of extension to Zcash. 

**zkEVM**

Eleyi yoo mu abinibi programmability to Zcash pẹlu kan EVM-ibamu foju ẹrọ ti o atilẹyin odo-imọ ẹri iṣiro. Eleyi yio gba Zcash lati ri idagba nipasẹ kan diẹ orisirisi Olùgbéejáde awujo ati ki o se igbelaruge ohun ilolupo ti asiri fifi awọn ohun elo ati tokeni. Eleyii yoo jẹ ki o ṣe afiwe si miiran tẹlẹ L2 asiri solusan. 

Iwadii ti n tẹsiwaju lori Imọ-ẹri-Ipa & Ilana Ibaraẹnisọrọ Interblockchain Cosmos ni ECC n ṣe. Awọn igbesẹ atẹle ni a n ṣe ayẹwo pẹlu aṣeyọri ti Isopọ Ethereum si PoS pẹlu eyikeyi awọn ọran ti o le dide. 

**Ìpínlẹ̀ ZSA/UDA's**

Zcash Shielded Assets/User Defined Asset ti wà ní ìdàgbàsókè pẹ̀lú ìrànlọ́wọ́ ẹgbẹ́ tí a yà sọ́tọ̀. Lẹ́yìn àtúnṣe ìlànà NU5 wọ́n ti súnmọ́ ìmúṣẹ wọn dáadáa. Àwọn ètò fún ìfọkàntán & ìdásílẹ̀ àgbélébùú ẹ̀ka àwọn ohun-ìní wọ̀nyí tí ó ń mú kí ìfọwọ́sowọ́pọ̀ wà ní ìgbésẹ̀ báyìí. Ìjápọ̀ kan sí ìfihàn Zcon3 lórí èyí ni ìsàlẹ̀ yìí.
