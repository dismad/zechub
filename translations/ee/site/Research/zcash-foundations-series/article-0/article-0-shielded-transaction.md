### Nusi wokpɔna le susu me do ŋgɔ na akɔntabubua: azɔlizɔzɔ si me womezãa mɔfiame aɖeke le o le ame ŋutɔ ƒe fexexe ŋu

> **Series:** *Zcash tso Gɔmeɖose Gbãtɔwo me* . **Nyati 0 lia . Seke la**
> **Nyaselawo:** ame yeye blibowo. Nya ɣaɣla aɖeke meli o, blockchain ƒe megbenya aɖeke meli o, eye akɔntabubu aɖeke mesusui o.
> **Nusi nàgblẽ ɖi:** susu ƒe kpɔɖeŋu nyuitɔ si ɖe alesi Zcash ɣlaa *amesi xe fe na amesi, kple ga home*, esime wògaɖea mɔ xexeame katã naɖo kpe edzi be womewɔ aʋatso ga aɖeke alo zã zi eve o.

Nyati ɖesiaɖe si ava emegbe le nyati siawo me la, lolona ɖe mɔ̃ si nèle go dom kplii la ƒe akpa ɖeka dzi. Eyata ne nya aɖe le afisia se le eɖokui me be asi-ʋuʋu la, *nyui*. Ema nye ŋugbedodo be míatrɔ ava akpɔe nyuie.

## 1. Nu ka tae wòle be nàtsɔ ɖe le eme?

Tsɔe be wotsɔ ati ƒo wò gadzraɖoƒegbalẽvi ɖe gli ŋu le dua ƒe ablɔdzi. Tegbee. Ame sia ame (wò aƒetɔ, wò dɔtɔ, amedzro aɖe, amesi ava xɔ wò ɖe dɔ me, dziɖuɖu aɖe) ate ŋu axlẽ aƒexɔfetu ɖesiaɖe, atikewɔfe ɖesiaɖe, nudzɔdzɔ ɖesiaɖe, kɔfi ɖesiaɖe, eye wòakpɔ amesiwo tututu nèɖo ga ɖe kple amesiwo ɖo ga ɖe wò.

Ema menye nususugblɔ si me susu mele o. **Alema tututue Bitcoin wɔa dɔe.**

Woyɔa Bitcoin zi geɖe be "ame si ƒe ŋkɔ womeyɔ o," gake menye nenemae o. Enye *ŋkɔ ɣaɣla*: wò ŋkɔ mele agbalẽ gã la dzi o, gake asitsatsa, ga home, kple kadodo ɖesiaɖe si le adrɛswo dome nye dutoƒo eye wònɔa anyi ɖaa. "Kɔsɔkɔsɔ me dzodzro" ƒe akpa bliboa li be woaɖe ŋkɔ bubu tsɛ ma ɖa eye wòabla adrɛswo ɖe ame ŋutɔŋutɔwo ŋu. Ne wonya do ƒome kple wò adrɛs ɖeka ko la, wò ganyawo ƒe ŋutinya ʋuna.

Wotu Zcash be wòaɖo nyabiase sesẽ aɖe si blea ame ŋu be:

> **Ðe míate ŋu akpɔ ga si nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ keŋkeŋ, si aɣla amesi ɖoe ɖa, amesi xɔe, kple ga home, evɔ míagaɖe mɔ na ame aɖe kokoko be wòaɖo kpe edzi be wowɔ ɖe seawo dzia?**

Taɖodzinu eve mawo wɔa avu kple wo nɔewo. Woate ŋu aɖo kpe dutoƒo agbalẽ gã aɖe dzi *elabena* amesiame ate ŋu akpɔe. Ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo tsɔtsɔ aɣla fia be ame aɖeke mate ŋu akpɔe o. Eyata aleke dukɔa awɔ aɖo kpe nane si ŋu womeɖe mɔ na be wòalé ŋku ɖo o dzi?

Nya ma si tsi tre ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu gbɔ kpɔkpɔe nye nusiwo kplɔ wo nɔewo ɖo sia ƒe ŋutinya bliboa. Mina míadze egɔme.

## 2. Xexe eve le Zcash me

Do ŋgɔ na nu bubu ɖesiaɖe la, kɔ nukpɔsusu totro aɖe si bɔ la me: **Zcash menye "ame ŋutɔ ƒe gaku o." Enye gaku si naa ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo abe tiatia ene.** Le nyateƒe me la, edze agbe gɔme abe Bitcoin ƒe fɔkpa ene, eye wòtsɔa ɖoɖo eve siwo sɔ kple wo nɔewo le blockchain ɖeka dzi.

| | **Xexeme si me kɔ** | **Xexeme si wotsɔ akpoxɔnu wɔe** |
|---|---|---|
| Ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo gbɔgblɔ | Dutoƒo, abe alesi Bitcoin | Ame ŋutɔ tɔ |
| Adrɛswo dzea egɔme kple | `t...` | `z...` or `u...` |
| Dɔla / amesi xɔe / ga home | **Edzena** na amesiame | **Ɣla** tso amesiame gbɔ |
| Nusiwo le ete tech | Bitcoin-style dutoƒo agbalẽ gã | Cryptographic ƒe ŋugbedodowo + zero-sidzedze kpeɖodziwo |

Ga ateŋu atso liƒo si le wo dome gɔ̃ hã: ga tsɔtsɔ yi* xexe si me wokpɔ akpoxɔnu le la woyɔna be *akpoxɔnu*, eye wo ʋuʋu ake do goe nye *akpoxɔnu ɖeɖeɖa*.

Xexe si me kɔ la nye "Bitcoin si gɔme nèse xoxo le mɔ si sɔ nu." Enye **xexe si wotsɔ akpoxɔnu wɔe** si me nya ɣaɣla dzeaniawo katã le, eye ema koe nye xexe si ŋu nya siwo kplɔ wo nɔewo ɖo sia tsɔ ɖe le.

## 3. Nusi wokpɔna le susu me: agbalẽkotoku siwo wotre nu na le dutoƒo board dzi

Susu me nɔnɔmetata ɖeka si woatsɔ ayi nyatia ƒe akpa mamlɛa mee nye esi. Míatrɔ ɖe eŋu ɣesiaɣi.

Kpɔ **dutoƒo nyatakakadzraɖoƒe** gã ɖeka si amesiame si le Anyigba dzi ate ŋu akpɔ ɣesiaɣi la ɖa.

* **Ga xɔxɔ** fia be ame aɖe atsɔ **agbalẽvi si wotu nu ɖo, si me mekɔ o** abla ɖe ʋuƒoa ŋu. *Ga agbɔsɔsɔme si le eme* kple *nya ɣaɣla aɖe si amesi xɔe koe ate ŋu axlẽ* le agbalẽkotokua me, elabena wotu agbalẽkotokua ɖe amesi xɔe la ŋutɔ ƒe safui ŋu. Xexeame katã kpɔe be *akplo aɖe do*. Ame aɖeke mate ŋu akpɔ nusi le eme o negbe aƒetɔa ko.

* **Ðeko board la tsina ɣesiaɣi.** Womegbãa agbalẽkotokuwo alo tutua wo gbeɖe o. Wotsɔa ga ƒoa yeyewo ɖe etame, tegbee.

* **Gazazã** fia be nàɖe afɔ ɖe xɔmetsovɔ aɖe megbe, aɖo kpe edzi be *"Nye tɔe nye agbalẽkotoku siwo womezã o siwo le ʋuƒo sia dzi la dometɔ ɖeka, eye woɖe mɔ nam be maʋui"*, emegbe nàtsɔ **void token** tɔxɛ aɖe aƒu gbe ɖe dutoƒo "gazazã" ƒe nugoe me eye nàbla **akplo yeye** na amesiame si nèle fe xem na.

Kɔnu sue ma (pin a void token, pin new envelopes, wo katã tso xɔmetsovɔ megbe) *nye* Zcash fexexe. Nu bubu ɖesiaɖe nye nyatakaka tsitotsito.

Azɔ mina míatsɔ ŋkɔ ŋutɔŋutɔ na fefenu mawo.

## 4. Ŋkɔnya atɔ̃awo

Nya atɔ̃ siawo nye Zcash si wotsɔ akpoxɔnu wɔe ƒe nyagbɔgblɔ bliboa. Srɔ̃ wo abe *ŋutinya* ene, ke menye abe nyagɔmeɖegbalẽ ene o, eye woalé ɖe wo ŋu.

| Le ŋutinyaa me la | Zcash nya ŋutɔŋutɔ | Nusi tututu wònye |
|---|---|---|
| Agbalẽkotokua me nyawo (ga home + aƒetɔ + nya ɣaɣla aɖe) | **De dzesii** | "Gaku" si nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ: asixɔxɔ ƒe akpa aɖe si nye ame aɖe tɔ |
| Agbalẽkotoku si wotre nu na, si me mekɔ o si le ʋuƒoa dzi | **De dzesi ɖokuitsɔtsɔna** | Nyatakakadzraɖoƒe ƒe nutrenu si ɖo kpe edzi be agbalẽkotoku aɖe li esime wòɣla nusi le |
| Nyatakakaxɔa ŋutɔ | **De dzesi ɖokuitsɔtsɔna ƒe ati** | *Nuŋlɔɖi ɖesiaɖe si wowɔ kpɔ* |
| Void token si le "zã" bin | **Nullifier** | Dzesi tɔxɛ aɖe si gɔmee nye "wozã nuŋlɔɖi sia fifia" |
| "Vɔvɔli la megbe" ƒe akunyawɔwɔ | **Zero-sidzedze ƒe kpeɖodzi** | Kpeɖodzi be gazazã bliboa sɔ, si meɖe wo dometɔ aɖeke fia o |

Ne mèɖo ŋku nu bubu aɖeke dzi le nyati sia me o la, ke ɖo ŋku kplɔ̃ sia dzi. Nusianu si kplɔe ɖo la nye *nusita* wòle be woatrɔ asi le akpa ɖesiaɖe ŋu abe alesi wòle ene ko.

## 5. Nusita wowɔ akpa ɖesiaɖe abe alesi wòle ene

Esiae nye akpa si numeɖela akpa gãtɔ ƒua du, eye eya tututue ma "Melé nya aɖewo ɖe tame" kple "Mese aɖaŋua gɔme." Akpa atɔ̃awo dometɔ ɖesiaɖe li be woakpɔ **kuxi ɖeka koŋ gbɔ.**

### Nuŋlɔɖia ƒe ɖokuitsɔtsɔna: ɣla emenyawo, gake na aʋatsokaka mate ŋu adzɔ o

Woate ŋu atsɔ dzo aʋu agbalẽkotoku dzro aɖe. cryptographic **note commitment** mate ŋui o. Bu eŋu be enye agbalẽkotoku si wotre * akunyawɔwɔ* nu, si me mekɔna o bliboe, si me ŋusẽ gã eve le:

- **Ɣladodo**: ŋkuléle ɖe agbalẽkotoku si wotre nu na ŋu megblɔa *naneke* na wò tso ga home alo amesi tɔe wònye si le eme ŋu o.
- **Binding**: ne wonya tree ko la, womate ŋu aɖɔli emenyawo o. Màte ŋu agblɔ emegbe be ga home bubu aɖe nɔ agbalẽkotokua me o.

Aleke nutrenu ate ŋu awɔ evea siaa zi ɖeka? Nyabiase ŋutɔŋutɔ si ŋu ŋuɖoɖo le ye nye ema. Enye **Nyati 3 lia (adzɔgbeɖeɖewo)** ƒe nyati. Fifia la, xɔ agbalẽkotokua be enye akunya eye nàyi edzi anɔ ʋuʋum.

### Nullifier: nusi nye aɖaŋudzela ŋutɔŋutɔ

Ne èzã nuŋlɔɖi aɖe la, ètaa eƒe **nullifier**, si nye "void token." Wobu dzesi sia tso *nuŋlɔɖia ŋutɔ* **kple** *wò safui ɣaɣla* me. Nuɖaɖa ma ƒlea nunɔamesi etɔ̃ le ɣeyiɣi ɖeka me, eye wo dometɔ ɖesiaɖe le vevie:

1. **Aƒetɔ koe ateŋu awɔe.** Èhiã safui ɣaɣla la be nàbu eŋu, eyata ame aɖeke mateŋu azã wò nuŋlɔɖiwo na wò o.
2. **Enye *dzesi * ɖeka* ɣesiaɣi na nuŋlɔɖi si wona.** Dze agbagba nàzã nuŋlɔɖi ɖeka zi eve eye àwɔ dzesi *ɖeka* si nye void zi evea siaa, eye dutoƒo "zã" bin la me le eme xoxo. Wogbe gazazã zi gbɔ zi eve. 
3. **Ame aɖeke mate ŋu akpɔe tso eƒe agbalẽkotoku me o.** Void token la dze abe ɖe medo ƒome kple agbalẽkotoku si me wòtso kura o ene.

Nu etɔ̃lia ma nye **Zcash ƒe adzamenyawo ƒe dzi**, eye wòdze na eya ŋutɔ ƒe akpa si le ete.

### Kpeɖodzi si nye sidzedze zero: xɔmetsovɔa ŋutɔ

Nusianu dzɔna le xɔmetsovɔ megbe, eye nusi nètsɔ dea asi na xexeame le ema megbe nye **sidzedze zero-kpeɖodzi**, ɖaseɖigbalẽ ƒomevi aɖe si womate ŋu aŋlɔ o. Eɖo kpe nusiawo katã dzi zi ɖeka le ɖoɖoezizi me be:

- *kplo si me zãm mele la, wotsɔa pin ɖe board la ŋu ŋutɔŋutɔ* (enye nuŋlɔɖi ŋutɔŋutɔ, si li xoxo),
- *Woɖe mɔ nam vavã be maʋui* (Melé ɖusime safui),
- *wobu nye void token nyuie* (no cheating the double-spend check),
- *nye agbalẽkotoku yeyeawo léa ga si sɔ pɛpɛpɛ abe xoxoa ene*: **ga aɖeke medzɔ tso naneke me o.**

Nukunu lae nye be kpeɖodzia ɖe nyateƒenya mawo dometɔ **ɖeke** fia o. Menye ga homea o, menye adrɛsawo o, menye agbalẽkotoku si o. Ðeko wòna nèka ɖe edzi be *nya sia nya si le etame la nye nyateƒe*. Alesi ema ate ŋu adzɔ gɔ̃ hãe nye **Se 5 (sidzedze zero ƒe kpeɖodziwo)**, si nye crescendo si le nusiwo kplɔ wo nɔewo ɖo me.

## 7. Fexexe aɖe, tso nuwuwu vaseɖe nuwuwu

Mina míakpɔ Alice ƒe fexexe na Bob, eye woaŋlɔ afɔɖeɖe ɖesiaɖe si woawɔ le dutoƒo kple ame ŋutɔ tɔ.

![alt nuŋɔŋlɔ](image-4.png)

De dzesi alesi masɔmasɔ si na be ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo tsɔtsɔ aɣla la wɔa dɔ:

- **Alice ƒe nuŋlɔɖi xoxoa** kuna to *nullifier* si le nugoe si wozã me dzi.
- **Bob ƒe nuŋlɔɖi yeyea** wodzi to *tsɔtsɔtsɔna* yeye aɖe si le board la dzi.
- Le amesiame si le ekpɔm gome la, **kadodo aɖeke mele nudzɔdzɔ eve siawo ŋu si woate ŋu akpɔ o.** Ga la ƒe mɔzɔzɔa fa miamiamia.

> **Aleke Bob wɔ nya gɔ̃ hã be woxe fe na ye?** Wotsɔ nya ɣaɣlawo de eƒe nuŋlɔɖia me *ɖe eƒe safui ŋu*. Eléa ŋku ɖe ʋuƒoa ŋu ɣesiaɣi eye *eƒe* agbalẽkotokuwo koe ʋuna nɛ, abe safui ɖeka si sɔ na gaƒoɖokui aɖewo koŋ ƒe amesinɔnɔ ene. Mɔ̃ si le megbe na esiae nye **safuiwo kpɔkpɔ**, si nye nyati si ava emegbe.

## 8. Nusi xexeame kpɔna vs. nusi nɔa ɣaɣla

| Nyateƒenya si ku ɖe fexexe ŋu | Amewo ƒe nukpɔkpɔa? |
|---|---|
| Be *a* shielded asitsatsa dzɔ |  Ẽ |
| Be ewɔ ɖe seawo katã dzi (aʋatsokaka aɖeke meli o, gazazã zi eve aɖeke meli o) |  Ẽ (to kpeɖodzia dzi) |
| **Amekae** ɖo ga la ɖa |  Woɣlae |
| **Amekae** xɔe |  Woɣlae |
| **Aleke gbegbee** woɖoe ɖa |  Woɣlae |
| **Nukae** do ŋgɔ ƒe nuŋlɔɖi si wozã |  Woɣlae |

Esia nye vovototodedeameme si tso Akpa 1. Dutoƒoe ɖoa kpe *seawo* dzi, ke menye *emenyawo* o. Kpeɖodzi kple ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ dzudzɔa avuwɔwɔ, elabena sidzedze zero ƒe kpeɖodzia na be nàte ŋu alé ŋku ɖe gbãtɔa ŋu evɔ màka asi evelia ŋu o.

## 9. Eƒe dzi: nusitae womate ŋu atsɔ agbalẽkotokua kple void token la asɔ kple wo nɔewo o

Ne èse susu ɖeka sia gɔme la, èse nusita Zcash nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ gɔme. Xlẽe vivivi.

- Wotsɔa **envelope (commitment)** blaa board la ne wodzi nuŋlɔɖi aɖe **dzi**.
- Wotsɔa **void token (nullifier)** ƒua gbe ɖe bin la me ne woŋlɔ nuŋlɔɖi ma ke **zã**, ɖewohĩ le ɣleti aɖewo megbe.
- Wowɔa wo to **nuɖaɖa ɣaɣla vovovowo** dzi, eye **dutoƒo akɔntabubu aɖeke meli** si trɔa ɖeka wòzua evelia o.

Eyata gotagome eteƒekpɔla kpɔa agbalẽkotokuwo ƒe tɔsisi aɖe wòdzena eye dzesi siwo me naneke mele o ƒe tɔsisi aɖe dona, gake **mate ŋu asɔ kple wo nɔewo o**. Womate ŋu agblɔ be "void token si woda egbe la sɔ kple envelope si wotsɔ pin ɖe March si va yi me o." Kadodoa li *ko* le nuŋlɔɖia tɔ ƒe sidzedze ɣaɣla me, eye kpeɖodzi si nye zero-sidzedze ɖo kpe edzi be kadodoa sɔ *eye meɖee fia o.*

Kadodo ma si gblẽ lae nye nusi kɔsɔkɔsɔ-kuxiwo me dzodzro dɔwɔƒewo ɖua kplɔ̃ le le Bitcoin me, kple nusi Zcash ɖoe koŋ lãna.

> **Do wò nukpɔsusu kpɔ:** Ne wobu nullifiers boŋ *ɖeko* tso nuŋlɔɖia me (safui ɣaɣla aɖeke mele eme o), nunɔamesi etɔ̃ siwo le Akpa 5 lia me dometɔ kae agbã, eye nukatae ema agblẽ adzamenyawo dome kpoo? *(Ðo eŋu le nuwuwu.)*

## 10. Agbegbegbeɖeɖe anukwaretɔe

Esia nye **susu ƒe kpɔɖeŋu**, menye spec la o. Be wòanɔ bɔbɔe na ame yeyewo la míewɔ nu ŋutɔŋutɔ geɖe bɔbɔe kpoo: Zcash ƒe atsyã geɖe siwo wotsɔ akpoxɔnu wɔe (Sprout, emegbe Sapling, fifia Orchard); asitsatsa ŋutɔŋutɔwo ate ŋu azã ga eye woawɔ *geɖe* nuŋlɔɖiwo zi ɖeka; "board" nye ati ƒomevi aɖe koŋ le mɔ̃ɖaŋununya nu, ke menye pinboard ŋutɔŋutɔ o; eye wowɔa asixɔxɔ ƒe dadasɔ ŋudɔ kple nya ɣaɣlawo ƒe agbalẽdzikpɔkpɔ bubu aɖewo. Nya mawo dometɔ aɖeke metrɔa ŋutinya si nèsrɔ̃ fifia o; woɖɔa eŋu ɖo. Míatsɔ nusi sɔ pɛpɛpɛ la akpe ɖe eŋu, nyati ɖeka ɖeka, eye míade aflaga nyuie ɣesiaɣi si míewɔe.

Nufiame nyuiwo nana wokana ɖe ame dzi to nya siwo wògblẽ ɖi gbɔgblɔ me. Akpa siae nye ŋugbedodo ma.

## 12. Kpuie ko la

- Bitcoin nye **nukpɔkpɔ si me kɔ**; Zcash na **shielded** xexe si me woɣla amedɔdɔ, amesi xɔe, kple ga home.
- Nusi dze abe ɖe wòtsi tre ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu ene (*ame ŋutɔ tɔ gake woate ŋu aɖo kpe edzi le dutoƒo*) ye nye nya bliboa, eye woate ŋu akpɔ egbɔ.
- Fexexe si wokpɔ ta na nye nu atɔ̃ siwo do ƒome kple wo nɔewo: **note** (gaku la), **note commitment** (agbalẽvi si wotre nu na), **note commitment tree** (dutoƒo board), **nullifier** (void token si xea mɔ na gazazã zi eve), kple **zero-knowledge proof** (vɔvɔli si ɖo kpe edzi be esɔ esime meɖe naneke ɖe go o).
- Adzamenyawo nɔa te ɖe **kadodo ɖeka si wotso** dzi mlɔeba: ame aɖeke si le egodo mate ŋu atsɔ nuŋlɔɖi aɖe ƒe dzidzi (ɖokuitsɔtsɔna) aƒo ƒu kple eƒe ku (nullifier) ​​o.
- Dukɔa ɖo kpe **seawo** dzi, ke menye **emenyawo** gbeɖe o.

Fifia èlé anyigbatata la ɖe asi. Nusiwo kplɔ wo nɔewo ɖo ƒe akpa mamlɛa yɔe fũ.

## Nyabiasewo ƒe Nyabiasewo

**Ðe Zcash nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ ɣesiaɣia?**
Ao, ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ ku ɖe xexe si *wokpɔ ta na* (`z...`/`u...` adrɛswo). Si me kɔ (`t...`) asitsatsa nye dutoƒo, abe Bitcoin ene.

**Ne woɣla nusianu la, nukae xea mɔ na ame be wòagata ga femaxee?**
Kpeɖodzi si me sidzedze zero mele o. Ezia asitsatsa ɖesiaɖe ƒe emetsonuwo dzi le akɔntabubu nu be woatsɔ nusiwo wotsɔ de eme ŋutɔŋutɔ, siwo womezã o, akpe ɖe wo ŋu, *eye* wòle ga homeawo ɣlam.

**Ðe woate ŋu azã gagbalẽ ɖeka ma ke zi evea?**
Ao, nuŋlɔɖi aɖe zazã ta eƒe nullifier; agbagbadzedze evelia ata nullifier si sɔ, si le "zã" bin me xoxo, eyata network la gbee.

**Ðe egodotɔwo ate ŋu atsɔ ame si ɖoe ɖa la aƒo ka kple amesi xɔea?**
Ao, ame aɖeke mate ŋu atsɔ ɖokuitsɔtsɔna (note ƒe dzidzi) kple nullifier (note ƒe ku) asɔ kple wo nɔewo ne etɔ la menya le adzame o.

### Ŋuɖoɖo na nukpɔkpɔ ƒe dodokpɔ (Akpa 9) .

Ne wobu nullifier la *ko* tso nuŋlɔɖia me, kple safui ɣaɣla aɖeke o la, ekema **ame sia ame** ate ŋu abu akɔnta le eŋu, si agbã nunɔamesi #1 (aƒetɔ koe ateŋu azã). Nusi gavloe wue nye be, woate ŋu akpɔ nullifier la tso dutoƒonyatakaka siwo ku ɖe nuŋlɔɖia ŋu me tẽ azɔ, si ate ŋu ana eteƒekpɔlawo **tsɔ nullifier la atrɔ ɖe eƒe ŋugbedodo ŋu**, si agbã nunɔamesi #3 eye wòaɖe ɖoɖo bliboa ƒe adzamenyawo me kpoo. Safui ɣaɣla lae nye nusi na be void token la *nye tɔwò ɖeɖeko* eye *womate ŋu aƒo ka na o.*
