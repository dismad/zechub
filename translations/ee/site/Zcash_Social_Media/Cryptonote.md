**Bytecoin** nye cryptocurrency gbãtɔ si wɔ ɖoɖowɔɖia ŋudɔ. Dɔ si wonya nyuie wu si zãnɛ egbea enye **Monero (XMR)**. Wozãe le TurtleCoin, Aeon, kple bubu geɖewo hã me.

## CryptoNote ƒe Nɔnɔme Veviwo

CryptoNote Protocol la naa nu vevi etɔ̃:

1. **Adzɔnuwo ƒe mɔɖeɖe kple kadodomanɔamesi**
2. **Dɔwɔwɔ ƒe Kpeɖodzi si le Tasɔsɔ** (ASIC te ŋu tsi tre ɖe eŋu) 
3. **Dynamic emission** .

## 1. Untraceability - Kplɔ̃ ƒe Asidede Asi

Wozãa **Ring Signatures** koŋ tsɔ kpɔa nusiwo womate ŋu akpɔ o gbɔ.

Ne èle asitsatsa ɖom ɖa la, wotsaka wò dutoƒo safui ŋutɔŋutɔ kple decoy safui geɖewo ("ring") - wo katã me gaku agbɔsɔsɔme ɖeka le. Esia na wòsesẽna ŋutɔ be woanya amesi tututu ɖo gakuawo ɖa.

**Ring size** kpɔa ŋusẽ ɖe ŋkɔmaɖemaɖe ƒe ɖoɖoa dzi vevie. Kplɔ̃ gãwo naa ame ŋutɔ ƒe adzamenyawo nɔa anyi nyuie wu.

![Ring Signatures ƒe numeɖeɖe](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FrXteGHXgAANE0F.png)

**Ne wotsɔe sɔ kple Zcash**: 
Zcash ƒe ŋkɔmaɖemaɖe ƒe ƒuƒoƒo nye asitsatsa *ɣeyiɣi* si wowɔ le ta si wotsɔ akpoxɔnu na me ƒe xexlẽme bliboa (si lolo wu CryptoNote ƒe asigɛ ƒe lolome siwo bɔ sã).

---

## Ring CT (Adzamenuwo ƒe Asitsatsa) .

**Ring CT** ƒe kpɔɖeŋua na adzamenyawo nyo ɖe edzi ŋutɔ le gaku siwo wotu ɖe CryptoNote dzi me.

Le esi teƒe be Ring CT naɣla amesi ɖoe ɖa ko la, ega **tɔtɔa asitsatsa ƒe ga homewo** le ame si ɖoe ɖa kple amesi xɔe dome.

![King CT nɔnɔmetata](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FrXuivgWYAAze7B.png)

Ezãa:
- Elliptic Curve ƒe Nuŋɔŋlɔ
- Pedersen ƒe Ŋugbedodowo
- Homomorphic Nya ɣaɣla

**Wozãa kpeɖodziwo** tsɔ ɖenɛ fiana be agbɔsɔsɔa lolo wu 0 eye wòle dometsotso siwo sɔ me **eye womeɖe asixɔxɔ ŋutɔŋutɔwo fiana o**.

**Adzame Adrɛs** hã tsɔa adrɛs siwo wozãna zi ɖeka kpena ɖe amesi xɔe ŋu.

![Adzame Adrɛswo + Kpeɖodziwo](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FrXut5aWAAMhuRb.jpg)

## 2. Dɔwɔwɔ ƒe Kpeɖodzi si Le Tasɔsɔ (ePoW) .

CryptoNote ƒe taɖodzinue nye be yeawɔ tomenukuɖoɖo si le dzɔdzɔe wu to tsitretsitsi ɖe ASICwo ŋu me.

Ezãa **CryptoNight** ƒe akɔntabubu (si nye ŋkuɖodzinu sesẽ ƒe dɔwɔwɔ). To vovo na Bitcoin’s SHA256 la, wowɔ CryptoNight be wòaxe vovototo si le CPU, GPU, kple ASIC tomenukulawo dome.

**CryptoNight ƒe afɔɖeɖewo:**
1. Dze ŋkuɖodzinu ƒe akpa gã aɖe (scratchpad) gɔme kple pseudorandom data
2. Wɔ nuxexlẽ/ŋɔŋlɔ ƒe dɔ gbogbo aɖewo le scratchpad la dzi
3. Hash scratchpad bliboa be nàwɔ asixɔxɔ mamlɛtɔ

![CryptoNight tomenukuƒe](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FrXvNs3XsAA37LG.jpg)

(De dzesii: Tso ɣemaɣi la, Monero ʋu tso CryptoNight gbɔ yi algorithm bubuwo gbɔ.)

## 3. Dynamic Emission

Le esi teƒe be wòaɖe nudzɔdzɔwo dzi akpɔtɔ zi ɖeka le afã me (abe Bitcoin ene) la, CryptoNote zãa **blok ƒe fetu si dzi ɖena kpɔtɔna bɔbɔe**.

Esia nana be ya si dona le yame ƒe ʋuʋu nɔa bɔbɔe wu le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe.

![Dynamic emission curve ƒe ʋuʋudedi](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FrXv8wpXoAEjUxW.png)

**Zcash Kadodo**: 
Zcash dɔwɔlawo ƒo nu tso ya si dona le yame ƒe mɔnu si le bɔbɔe wu ŋudɔwɔwɔ ŋu le etsɔme, si ateŋu ato "Zcash Posterity Fund" dzi.
