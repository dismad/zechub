# Nutoa me Gbeƒãɖeɖe to VDO.Ninja kple OBS Studio zazã me

Wowɔ nufiame kpui sia le [DWeb Camp 2023](https://dwebcamp.org/) to hatiwo kple lɔlɔ̃nu faa dɔwɔlawo ƒe ƒuƒoƒo aɖe dzi. Taɖodzinu si le dɔ sia ŋue nye be woawɔ viɖe tso smart phone dɔwɔnu siwo do ƒome kple offline MESH network zazã me hena video léle ɖe mɔ̃ dzi kple eɖeɖe ɖe go le nuwɔwɔ aduadu me.

Míezãa kɔmpiutadziɖoɖo eve siwo ŋu woate ŋu awɔ dɔ le [OBS Studio (Open Broadcaster software) .](https://obsproject.com/) kple [VDO.Ninja](https://vdo.ninja/). Woate ŋu awɔ kɔmpiutadziɖoɖo siawo ƒe kɔpi ahawɔ dɔ le wò kɔmpiuta dzi le mia gbɔ.

## OBS Studio (Ʋu Boardcaster ƒe kɔmpiutadziɖoɖo) .

OBS Studio nye Free and Open source software hena nuŋɔŋlɔ kple live streaming si li na dɔwɔɖoɖo geɖewo. Woɖe kɔmpiutadziɖoɖoa ɖe go zi gbãtɔ le ƒe 2012 me eye amewo kplɔnɛ ɖo le fefewɔƒewo kple video me nyatakakawo wɔlawo siwo le wo ɖokui si dome.

OBS Studio ƒe zãlawo ƒe ŋgɔdonyawo ate ŋu adze nu dziŋɔ ŋutɔ zi gbãtɔ si zãlawo. OBS studio la ma ɖe fesre eve me "Preview" kple "Broadcast". Do ŋgɔ ƒe fesrea ɖea video siwo li (fotoɖemɔ̃ vovovowo abe webcam, Iriun Webcam, OBS Virtual Camera, Video kple Browser source) si woyɔna be "Scenes" fiana eye "Broadcast" ɖea live stream fiana.

Be nàte ŋu atso didiƒe fotoɖemɔ̃ ƒe sisi tso VDO.ninja me ayi OBS Studio me la, èdzea egɔme kple "Browser Source" yeye tsɔtsɔ kpee kple "Sources > Add > Browser". Le fesre yeyea me la, àteŋu ana dzɔtsoƒe URL tso VDO.Ninja eye nàtia "Na dzɔtsoƒe nadze".

Fifia àte ŋu adze adzɔge tɔsisiawo kaka gɔme azɔ.

## VDO.Ninja

[VDO.Ninja ƒe ŋkɔ](https://vdo.ninja/) nye nyatakakadzraɖoƒe si woate ŋu azã Femaxee eye wòʋu ɖi si ana nàte ŋu atrɔ wò asitelefonwo woazu fotoɖemɔ̃ si me woɖea foto le. Woate ŋu awɔ kɔmpiutadziɖoɖoa ƒe kɔpi ahatsɔe ade kɔmpiuta si le mia gbɔ dzi alo àte ŋu azã [online version at https://vdo.ninja](https://vdo.ninja/).

VOD.Ninja ƒe ŋgɔdonya le bɔbɔe, ɖeko nàʋu VDO.Ninja le wò asitelefonwo ƒe nyatakakadzraɖoƒe eye nàtia "Tsɔ wò fotoɖemɔ̃ kpe ɖe OBS ŋu". Emegbe àtia wò fotoɖemɔ̃ kple odiomɔ̃ tso mɔ̃awo ƒe xexlẽdzesiwo me eye nàzi "Dze egɔme".  Emegbe àxɔ "kpɔ" kadodo si ateŋu atsɔ akpe ɖe OBS Studio ŋu.

## Nyabiasewo ƒe Nyabiasewo

- Video graphic card ƒomevi kawoe hiã na OBS Studio ?

Àte ŋu azã wò ŋutɔ wò kɔmpiuta si me graphic card nyui le eye ŋkuɖodzinu geɖe le alo le mɔ bubu nu la, àte ŋu azã hardware encoders [Teradek VidiU](https://www.bhphotovideo.com/c/product/1609186-REG/teradek_10_0235_vidiu_x_modem.html?gclid=EAIaIQobChMIl4aIo7zX_wIVDhqtBh0PgwhxEAAYAiAAEgInufD_BwE)
- Ðe OBS ɖe mɔ na wò be nàwɔ gbegɔmeɖeɖe le agbe me kple nyawo ƒe nuŋɔŋlɔa?

Plugin aɖewo li siwo nutoa me tɔwo kpe asi ɖe eŋu siwo dze abe ɖe wole nɔnɔme ma tɔgbe nam ene. [https://github.com/eddieoz/OBS-live-translation](https://github.com/eddieoz/OBS-live-translation)

- Àte ŋu awɔ wò ŋutɔ wò plugins na OBS Studio?

Ẽ, OBS le kpekpeɖeŋu lua, python ŋɔŋlɔdzesiwo. JavaScript hã na Overlays kple webviews.

- Ðe míezãa live fade yi black alo transitions?

Wò, nuwɔla la ŋutɔ gbɔe wòtso!

- Ðe latency aɖe li ne èle streaming wɔma?

Esia nɔ te ɖe teƒe si yim nèle yi afisi nèle streaming wɔm le dzi koŋ. Le kpɔɖeŋu me, YouTube ate ŋu ahe ɖe megbe aɖabaƒoƒo ɖeka alo esi wu nenema le video ŋuti dɔwɔwɔ si wowɔna le woƒe serverwo dzi hafi woɖea gbeƒãe ta.

- Odio dzi ɖena kpɔtɔna ne èle OBS zãm le slow machine dzi kple ne èle green-screening wɔm

Zã Hardware encoder alo zã stream yard
[https://support.streamyard.com/hc/en-us/articles/360056350852-How-to-Use-OBS-Virtual-Camera-with-StreamYard](https://support.streamyard.com/hc/en-us/articles/360056350852-How-to-Use-OBS-Virtual-Camera-with-StreamYard) alo [Tɔsisi ƒe Akpa.FM](http://riverside.fm/)
