# <img src="https://i.ibb.co/BN7SxwY/ZEC-USD.png" alt="Alt Text" width="50"/>   Non-Custodial Exchanges

[Zcash Nusiwo Menye Dedienɔnɔ ƒe Asitɔtrɔwo O](/site/Using_Zcash/DEX_List)

Le xexe si le tɔtrɔm ɣesiaɣi si nye cryptocurrency asitsatsa me la, dzidziɖedzi si menye ga si wotsɔ dzraa gae o si woyɔna hã be Decentralized Exchanges alo DEXs le alesi ezãlawo wɔa nu kple dijitaal nunɔamesiwoe la gbugbɔm. Mɔ̃ siawo naa asitsatsa ƒe mɔnu si trɔa asi le nu ŋu to domenɔlawo alo ame etɔ̃lia ƒe hiahiã ɖeɖeɖa kple dziɖuɖu gbugbɔgaɖoanyi na ezãlawo me.

Woŋlɔ ɖe etame be wonye asitɔtrɔ siwo menye gaxɔmenɔnɔ ƒe asitɔtrɔ siwo nana nète ŋu xɔa Zcash agbagbadzedzemanɔmee ahadzrae le ɖokuiwò si, eye mehiã be domenɔlawo nanɔ asitsatsa ƒe ɖoɖoa me o.

### **Nu Siwo Menye Vidzikpɔlawo ƒe Asitɔtrɔwo Gɔmesese**

Nudzraɖoƒe siwo menye gaxɔgbalẽviwo o, siwo woyɔna hã be Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs) nye mɔnu siwo naa cryptocurrency asitsatsa nɔa bɔbɔe evɔ mehiã be ezãlawo nade woƒe ga asitsaƒea ŋutɔ o. Ke boŋ ezãlawo kpɔa ŋusẽ ɖe woƒe safui siwo nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ dzi eye wodzraa wo tso woƒe gakotokuwo me tẽ evɔ mehiã be ame bubuwo nava o.

Mɔnu sia doa dedienɔnɔ kple adzamenyawo ɖe ŋgɔ, elabena ezãlawo meɖoa ŋu ɖe asitɔtrɔa ŋu be woalé woƒe nunɔamesiwo ɖe asi o si hã ɖea afɔku si le hacks alo wo dzi kpɔkpɔ gbegblẽ me dzi kpɔtɔna. Zi geɖe la, asitsatsa le asitsaƒe siwo menye gaxɔgbalẽviwo o me zãa nubabla siwo me nunya le tsɔ kpɔa egbɔ be asitsatsa si dzi womate ŋu aka ɖo o eye wòle gaglãgbe.

Viɖe vevi aɖe si le cryptocurrency ƒe asitɔtrɔ siwo menye gaxɔgbalẽviwo ɖɔliɖɔli o ŋue nye alesi wokpɔa ŋusẽ ɖe ezãlawo dzi ɖe woƒe nunɔamesiwo dzi geɖe wu. Esi wònye be gaɖɔliƒe siawo meléa nunɔamesiawo ɖe asi o ta la, ezãlawo kpɔa dzidzɔ ɖe woƒe dijitaal gawo ŋu bliboe eye wokpɔa ŋusẽ ɖe wo dzi.

### **Non Custodial Exchanges Vs Custodial ƒe asitɔtrɔ**

**#1 Dedienɔnɔ**: Nusiwo menye gaxɔmenɔnɔ ƒe asitɔtrɔwo o ɖea alesi wòhiã be ezãlawo naka ɖe dɔwɔƒe vevi aɖe dzi kple woƒe ga alo nunɔamesiwo ɖa. Esia wɔnɛ be ezãlawo te ŋu léa woƒe safui siwo nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ me ɖe asi eye wokpɔa ŋusẽ ɖe wo dzi, si ɖea afɔku si le hacks, amedzidzedze kple amedzidzedze kple platform ƒe afɔku siwo ate ŋu ato gaxɔmenɔnɔ ƒe asitɔtrɔwo me dzi kpɔtɔna.

**#2 Ameŋunyatakakawo**: Zi geɖe la, asitɔtrɔ siwo menye gaxɔmenɔlawo o naa ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo tsɔtsɔ aɣla geɖe wu to mɔɖeɖe na ezãlawo be woawɔ asitsadɔ tso woƒe gakotokuwo me tẽ evɔ mehiã be domenɔla aɖeke nava o. Woate ŋu awɔ asitsatsa kple ŋkɔmaɖemaɖe geɖe wu, elabena womedzraa nyatakaka veviwo ɖo o to vovo na Centralized Exchanges

**#3 Decentralization**: Nusiwo menye decentralization exchanges o sɔ kple decentralized ethos si le cryptocurrencies me wu. Zãlawo ƒe ɖokuisinɔnɔ kple ŋusẽ geɖe wu le woƒe asitsatsa ƒe dɔwɔnawo dzi, le ɖekawɔwɔ me kple gɔmeɖose siwo keke ta wu le blockchain mɔ̃ɖaŋununya me.

Ne míeƒo nu tso Custodial Exchanges ŋu la, Decentralization ƒe seƒe nɔa sue ŋutɔ zi geɖe le centralized exchanges akpa gãtɔ me si naa exchange team alo amegã siwo kpɔa zãlawo ƒe nyatakakawo alo nyatakaka siwo le exchange la dzi la dona.

**#4 Trɔtrɔ ɖe Sedede siwo le Tɔtrɔm Ŋu**: Zi geɖe la, asitɔtrɔ siwo menye gaxɔmenɔnɔ ƒe asitɔtrɔwo o te ŋu trɔna ɖe sedede ƒe nɔnɔme siwo le tɔtrɔm ŋu wu. Esi wònye be wometsɔa ga si wozãna le wo si o ta la, ɖewohĩ kuxi ʋɛ aɖewo koe anɔ wo ŋu le sedziwɔwɔ me ne wotsɔe sɔ kple ga si wotsɔ ɖɔa li gae.

**#5 Nu yeyewo dodo ɖe ŋgɔ kple dodokpɔ**: Nusiwo menye gaxɔmenɔnɔ ƒe asitɔtrɔwo ʋãa nu yeyewo wɔwɔ zi geɖe le crypto teƒea. Wodea mɔ̃ɖaŋununya siwo woɖe ɖe vovo ƒe ŋgɔyiyi ƒe dzi ƒo, abe asitsaha siwo wowɔna le wo ɖokui si (AMMs) kple ganyawo ƒe dɔwɔƒewo (DeFi) dɔwɔɖoɖowo ene.

**#6 Xexeame Katã ƒe Mɔnukpɔkpɔ**: Zi geɖe la, asitɔtrɔ siwo menye gaxɔgbalẽviwo ɖɔliɖɔli o naa mɔnukpɔkpɔ ezãlawo be woakpɔ cryptocurrencies le xexeame godoo, eye nuto siwo me sedede ƒe mɔxenuwo ate ŋu axe mɔ ɖe gaxɔgbalẽviwo ɖɔliɖɔli ƒe dɔwɔnawo ƒe anyinɔnɔ nu hã le.

**#7 KYC ƒe Nudidi aɖeke Meli o**: Asitɔtrɔ geɖe siwo menye vidzikpɔkpɔ o mebia tso ezãlawo si be woato nyanya wò asisi (KYC) ƒe ɖoɖo geɖe me o, si naa adzamenyawo kple amewo katã ƒe nyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ ƒe ɖoɖo aɖe si mele vidzikpɔkpɔ ƒe mɔnu aɖewo me o.

Azɔ, mina míadzro ʋee aɖewo siwo gbɔ woate ŋu akpɔ non-custodial exchanges siwo naa Zcash ƒe asitsatsa nɔa bɔbɔe la me. Mɔ̃ siawo zazã ana nàkpɔ mɔnu bɔbɔe aɖe si dzi nàto axɔ Zcash gaku geɖe.

### **Totoɖeme**

Non-custodial exchanges, alo DEXs, nye decentralized platforms si wɔnɛ be woate ŋu adzra cryptocurrency tẽ tso ezãlawo ƒe gakotokuwo me. Zãlawo léa woƒe safui ɣaɣlawo dzi kpɔkpɔ me ɖe asi, si wɔnɛ be dedienɔnɔ kple adzamenyawo nyona ɖe edzi.

Togbɔ be viɖe sẽŋuwo le asitɔtrɔ siwo menye gaxɔmenɔnɔ o ŋu hã la, ele vevie be míalɔ̃ ɖe edzi be woate ŋu ahe kuxiwo vɛ, abe gazazã ƒe nya siwo ate ŋu ado mo ɖa kple nusɔsrɔ̃ ƒe mɔnu si me kɔ wu na ezãla siwo si nuteƒekpɔkpɔ boo aɖeke mele o ene.

Abe alesi wòle le ganyawo ŋuti nyametsotso ɖesiaɖe gome ene la, ele be asitsalawo nalé ŋku ɖe nusiwo wotsɔ ɖo nɔƒe gbãtɔ, afɔku dzi ɖuɖu, kple mɔ̃ɖaŋununya si wonya nyuie ŋu nyuie hafi atiae be woaɖɔli nusiwo menye gaxɔmenɔnɔ o kple esiwo woatsɔ adzra gae.
