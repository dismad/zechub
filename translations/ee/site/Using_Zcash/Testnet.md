# Zcash Dodokpɔ ƒe Nyatakakadzraɖoƒe

## Nukae Nye Zcash Testnet?

**Zcash Testnet** nye blockchain si sɔ kple Zcash main network (Mainnet) ŋutɔŋutɔ si gbugbɔa ɖoɖowɔɖi, sewo, kple asitsatsa ƒe susuŋudɔwɔwɔ pɛpɛpɛ - gake vovototo vevi eve le esi:

1. **Ga ƒe asixɔxɔ ŋutɔŋutɔ aɖeke mele gakuwo ŋu o** - woyɔa wo be **TAZ**, menye ZEC o, eye dodokpɔ koe wozãna na wo. 
2. **Wodoa network ƒe ɖɔɖɔɖowo, dɔwɔnuwo, kple kɔmpiuta dɔwɔɖoɖowo kpɔna le afisia gbã** hafi woatsɔe ade Zcash blockchain ŋutɔŋutɔ dzi. 

Ne míagblɔe bubui la, Testnet le abe **sandbox alo dodokpɔ ƒe nɔnɔme** si me dɔwɔlawo, agbalẽdzikpɔlawo, kple xɔtulawo ateŋu ate susuwo kpɔ evɔ womatsɔ ga ŋutɔŋutɔ ade afɔku me o.


## Nukatae Testnet Li?

Testnet le vevie ŋutɔ na blockchain ƒe ŋgɔyiyi elabena **blockchain ŋutɔŋutɔwo abe Zcash ene metrɔna o** - ne wonya ɖo kpe asitsatsa dzi le network gã la dzi ko la, womate ŋu agbugbɔ wo awɔ o. Testnet naa **safe replica** be woate, ado wo kpɔ, eye woaɖɔ nɔnɔmewo ɖo hafi woaɖo wo ɖe Mainnet.

### Testnet zazã

#### 1. Kɔmpiutadziɖoɖowo ƒe Dɔwɔɖoɖo & Ðekawɔwɔ

Dɔwɔla siwo le gakotokuwo, asitɔtrɔwo, tomenukuƒewo, alo ameŋunyatakakawo ŋuti dɔwɔnuwo tum ate ŋu ado wo kpɔ dedie le Testnet. Ŋutetewo dometɔ aɖewoe nye:

- Adzɔnuwo ɖoɖo ɖa kple wo xɔxɔ 
- Tomenukuƒe yeye siwo me TAZ gaku siwo ƒe asixɔxɔ nye zero le 
- Zãla ƒe ŋgɔdonyawo kple APIwo tutu 
- Dodokpɔ le asitsatsa ƒe adzamenyawo ƒe nɔnɔmewo ŋu (si me kɔ vs esi wokpɔ ta na) . 

**Kpɔɖeŋu:** 
Dɔwɔnu siwo le abe [`zcash_tx_tool`](https://github.com/QED-it/zcash_tx_tool) zã Testnet tsɔ wɔ asitsatsa eye nàdo Zcash shielded asset functionalities kpɔ. 

**Xexeame ŋutɔŋutɔ ƒe nɔnɔme:** 
Gakotoku wɔla ateŋu atsɔ kɔmpiuta dɔwɔɖoɖowo aƒo ƒu ɖe Testnet RPC ƒe nuwuƒe eye wòawɔ agbenɔƒe bliboa ƒe kpɔɖeŋu - adrɛswo wɔwɔ, asitsatsa siwo wokpɔ ta na la ɖoɖo, kple ga si susɔ ƒe kpeɖodzi - hafi ayi agbe le Mainnet dzi.

#### 2. Dodokpɔ le Network ƒe Ŋgɔyiyiwo Ŋu

Zcash doa eƒe ɖoɖowɔɖi vevitɔ ɖe ŋgɔ ɣeaɖewoɣi (e.g., Nu5, Nu6). Testnet wɔa tɔtrɔ yeyewo **do ŋgɔ na Mainnet**, si na be dɔwɔlawo kple nutoa me tɔwo te ŋu dea dzesi vodadawo eye woɖɔa wo ɖo.

**Kpɔɖeŋu:** 
Wotutua se yeye si dzi woda asi ɖo alo asitsatsa ƒomevi aɖe ɖe Testnet dzi gbã. Ne wodoe kpɔ dzidzedze vɔ la, ewɔa dɔ le Mainnet dzi le block ƒe kɔkɔme si woɖo ɖi do ŋgɔ.

#### 3. Node ƒe Dɔwɔwɔwo Dodokpɔ

Zcash doa alɔ node kɔmpiuta dɔwɔɖoɖo geɖewo ƒe dɔwɔwɔ - . `zcashd` kple **Zebra** (Rust-based node si Zcash Foundation léa be na). Testnet na be woate ŋu ado nodes kpɔ le nɔnɔme ŋutɔŋutɔwo me ganyawo ƒe afɔku manɔmee. 

Node wɔlawo ate ŋu:

- Da asi ɖe block kaka dzi 
- Do RPC ƒe ŋgɔdonyawo kpɔ 
- Lé ŋku ɖe node ƒe nuwɔna ŋu le agba te 
- Do tomenukuƒe ƒe kɔmpiutadziɖoɖowo ƒe kadodowo kpɔ 

#### 4. Nusɔsrɔ̃ & Hehenana

Gɔmedzelawo ateŋu asrɔ̃ Zcash ƒe nɔnɔmewo abe tomenukuƒe, asitsatsa siwo ŋu wokpɔ ta na wɔwɔ, kple Adrɛs Ðeka zazã. 
Nutoa me nufiamewo kple nuŋlɔɖiwo naa mɔnukpɔkpɔ be woakpɔ **Testnet faucets, explorers, kple guides**.


## Testnet Zazã Nyateƒenyawo

### 1. Dɔwɔla Dodokpɔ (Gakotoku / App) .

- Do ka kple Zcash Testnet 
- Bia TAZ tso pɔmpi aɖe me 
- Ðo asitsatsa siwo ŋu wokpɔ ta na la ɖa 
- Kpɔ adzamenyawo kple UI ƒe liƒo ɖa 

ZEC ŋutɔŋutɔ aɖeke mebuna ne vodadawo dzɔ gɔ̃ hã o.

### 2. Trɔ asi le Ðekawɔwɔ Dodokpɔ

- Ƒu du Testnet node aɖe 
- Zã Zebrad JSON-RPC ƒe nuwuƒewo nàtsɔ awɔ dɔ tso asitsatsa ŋu 
- Do automated deposit/withdrawal logic kpɔ 

Ekpɔa egbɔ be nuwɔwɔ ƒe sedede le dedie eye wòxea mɔ na ga si bu.

### 3. Tomenukuƒewo ƒe Ðoɖowɔwɔ Dodokpɔwo

- Zã tomenukuƒewo ƒe nɔnɔmetatawo 
- Do block ƒe kpeɖodzinana kpɔ 
- Lé ŋku ɖe tomenukuƒewo ƒe teƒeɖoɖowo ŋu (TAZ ɖeɖeko) . 
- Tune tomenukuƒe ƒe dɔwɔwɔ 

Exea mɔ na dɔmawɔmawɔ alo gakpɔkpɔ si bu ne èle ʋuʋum yi Mainnet.

### 4. Sukudede / Protocol Numekuku

Numekulawo ateŋu ado nu yeyewo abe **stateless verification**, **zero-knowledge proof optimization**, alo protocol dodokpɔ bubuwo kpɔ to Testnet zazã me. 
Zãla deŋgɔwo ateŋu awɔ **Testnets tɔxɛwo alo regtest nutowo** hã hena dodokpɔ tɔxɛwo.


## Vovototo Vevi Siwo Le Mainnet Kple Testnet Dome

| Feature | Mainnet | Dodokpɔ ƒe nyatakakadzraɖoƒe |
|-----------------------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Asixɔxɔ si le gakuwo ŋu | ZEC vavãtɔ | TAZ (ga ƒe asixɔxɔ aɖeke mele eŋu o) |
| Afɔku | Ganyawo ƒe afɔku | Dedie na dodokpɔ |
| Protocol ƒe ŋgɔyiyiwo | Ewɔwɔ | Dɔwɔwɔ kaba |
| Tomenukuƒewo ƒe teƒeɖoɖowo | Nyateƒenya si woɖe ɖe go | Dodokpɔ ƒe fetu ɖeɖeko |
| Netwɔƒe ƒe dɔwɔnu | Adzɔnuwo wɔwɔ le agbe me| Dodokpɔ kple ŋgɔyiyi |

## Nukpɔsusu Totro Siwo Bɔ

- **Testnet gakuwo ƒe asixɔxɔ le nane** -> Aʋatso, TAZ ƒe asixɔxɔ nye zero. 
- **Testnet gakuwo ƒe bu le vevie** -> Aʋatso, asixɔxɔ ŋutɔŋutɔ aɖeke mebu o. 
- **Testnet kple Mainnet sɔ** -> Aʋatso, Testnet gbugbɔ ɖoa ​​anyi zi geɖe eye womele dedie le ganyawo gome abe Mainnet ene o.

## Nukae Nye TAZ?

**TAZ** nye Testnet ƒe Zcash gakuwo ƒe tɔtrɔ: 

- Menye ga ŋutɔŋutɔ o; womate ŋu aɖɔli ZEC alo fiat o 
- Wozãnɛ hena dodokpɔ, ŋgɔyiyi, kple nusɔsrɔ̃ 
- Ewɔna ɖe Zcash ƒe sewo katã dzi: woate ŋu aɖoe, aku, eye woazãe le adrɛs siwo wokpɔ ta na me 

**Kpɔɖeŋu:** 
Dɔwɔla ateŋu aɖo TAZ 100 tso Testnet adrɛs ɖeka dzi ayi bubu dzi atsɔ ado gakotoku ƒe nɔnɔme aɖe kpɔ evɔ matsɔ ZEC ŋutɔŋutɔ ade afɔku me o. 

Bu TAZ be enye **"fefe ga" na Zcash Testnet**.


## Nukae Nye Faucets?

**faucet** nye subɔsubɔdɔ si naa TAZ gaku femaxee hena dodokpɔ:

- Zi geɖe la, nyatakakadzraɖoƒewo alo APIwoe 
- Zãlawo naa Testnet adrɛs; pɔmpia ɖoa TAZ sue aɖe ɖa 
- Ƒo asa na alesi wòhiã be woaku TAZ kple asi 

**Kpɔɖeŋu:** 
1. Yi Testnet ƒe pɔmpi aɖe (e.g., [testnet.zecfaucet.com](https://testnet.zecfaucet.com) | [fauzec.com ƒe nyatakakadzraɖoƒea](https://fauzec.com/))  
2. Ŋlɔ wò Testnet adrɛs 
3. Bia TAZ 
4. Xɔ TAZ enumake be nàdze dodokpɔ gɔme 

**Nusita wòle vevie:** 
- Dodokpɔ dedie evɔ womatsɔ ZEC ade afɔku me o 
- Mɔnukpɔkpɔ na gɔmedzelawo kple dɔwɔlawo 
- Gakotokuwo, asitɔtrɔwo, kple dɔwɔɖoɖowo ƒe kpɔɖeŋuwɔwɔ kabakaba

## Le Testnet ƒe Dɔwɔwɔ vɔ megbe

- Adzɔnuwo wɔa nu abe Mainnet ene gake kple **zero-value TAZ** . 
- Woate ŋu ado asitsatsa siwo wokpɔ ta na, adrɛs geɖe, kple ameŋunyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ ƒe nɔnɔmewo kpɔ

## Kpuie Kpuie

- **Zcash Testnet** nye sandbox nɔnɔme si le dedie na xɔtutu, dodokpɔ, kple dodokpɔ 
- Zãzã ƒe nɔnɔmewo: developer dodokpɔ, node dodokpɔ, exchange integration, numekuku, kple hehenana 
- Wozãa **TAZ gaku** ɖe ZEC teƒe eye asixɔxɔ ŋutɔŋutɔ aɖeke mele wo ŋu o 
- Testnet le vevie hafi nàɖo nɔnɔmewo ɖe Mainnet dzi
