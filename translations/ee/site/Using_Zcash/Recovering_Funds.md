# Zcash Gakotoku ƒe Gaxɔmenɔnɔ

**Nukatae nàdzra wò safui ɣaɣla ɖo?** 

Ame ŋutɔ ƒe safuiwoe nye nya ɣaɣla na wò dijitaal nunɔamesiwo ƒe dedienɔnɔ. Wo léle ɖe te eye màma wo na ame bubuwo gbeɖe o le vevie ŋutɔ. 

> Le nya sia me la, woateŋu akpɔ **Nuku ƒe Nyagbe** be esɔ kple safui si nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ.

To wò safui ɣaɣlawo dzi kpɔkpɔ me la, woate ŋu agbugbɔ wo axɔ ɣesiaɣi. Zcash private keys ƒomevi 2 li (siwo me kɔ eye wokpɔa ta na), àteŋu atsɔ wo ade wò gakotoku me bɔbɔe, eɖanye to Sweep Funds dɔwɔwɔ zazã me alo wo tsɔtsɔ va abe akɔnta yeye ene o. Ne èlé wò safui siwo nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ dzi kpɔkpɔ me la, èléa wò nunɔamesiwo dzi kpɔkpɔ bliboe me ɖe asi, eye nèkpɔa egbɔ be yewo tɔ, dedienɔnɔ kple susu ƒe akɔdzeanyi le ye si.

# Dedienɔnɔ Kple Agbanɔamedzi

Ele vevie ŋutɔ be ezãlawo nase afɔku siwo le ame ŋutɔ ƒe safuiwo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ me gɔme eye woakpɔ safui siawo ta tso ame siwo ŋu womeɖe mɔ ɖo o ƒe gege ɖe eme me. Ga ƒe dedienɔnɔ nɔ te ɖe ezãla ƒe agbanɔamedzi be wòakpɔ woƒe safui ɣaɣlawo ta dzi.

## Ga Gbugbɔgadzɔ kple Ywallet

Wonya YWallet be enye tiatia nyuitɔwo dometɔ ɖeka hena ga siwo womate ŋu akpɔ o la xɔxɔ, tso *si me kɔ* kple ame ŋutɔ ƒe safui siwo wokpɔ ta na siaa gbɔ.

### 1) Ame ŋutɔ ƒe Safuiwo tsɔtsɔ tso duta 

1. Wɔ Ywallet ƒe kɔpi[.](https://ywallet.app)

2. Ne èʋui ko la, zi ‘More’ dzi le ete le ɖusime.

3. Tia 'Akɔntabubuwo'.

4. Le ɖusime le etame la, zi dzesi si nye plus dzi 

![Tsɔ kpe ɖe dzesi ƒe dzesi ŋu](https://i.postimg.cc/xJbVz7gB/plus.png)

5. Trɔ asi le 'Gbugbɔ gbugbɔ akɔnta aɖe ɖo'. 

6. Ŋlɔ Nuku ƒe nyagbe alo Private key

> **De dzesii**: Ne èlé ga ɖe gakotoku si medoa alɔ adrɛs siwo wokpɔ ta na o (Trust, Coinomi, Guarda kple bubuawo) la, ele be nàzã ‘Sweep Funds’ ƒe nɔnɔme.

### 2) Klɔ Gawo

1. Wɔ Ywallet ƒe kɔpi[.](https://ywallet.app)

2. Ne èʋui ko la, zi ‘More’ dzi le ete le ɖusime.

3. Yi anyime yi Dɔwɔnuwo ƒe akpaa dzi, zi ‘Sweep’ dzi.

4. Ŋlɔ wò nuku ƒe nyagbe (Gap limit scans hena adrɛs bubu siwo nukua wɔ)

![Sweep Gawo ƒe screen](https://i.postimg.cc/3055CBcN/sweep.png)

5. Ŋlɔ Asixɔxɔ ƒe Ta la ɖe teƒe si nèdi be yeazã (Exchanges use Transparent) .

6. Ŋlɔ afisi nèdi be yeade ga le ƒe Adrɛs si dzi nèyi.
