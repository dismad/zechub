# VPN & dVPN ƒe dɔwɔwɔ

Le dijitaal ƒe ɣeyiɣia me la, Internet dzi nyatakakawo kple dedienɔnɔ dzi kpɔkpɔ le vevie wu. Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) kple decentralized VPNs (dVPNs) wɔa akpa vevi aɖe le wò Internet dzi dɔwɔnawo kple nyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ me. Wiki ƒe axa sia ku vovototo vevi siwo le VPN xoxowo kple dVPN siwo le dodom dome, dedienɔnɔ ŋuti nukpɔsusuwo ne wole VPN tiam, kple VPN dɔwɔna siwo xɔa cryptocurrency si tsia dzi ɖe adzamenyawo ŋu, Zcash, ƒe xexlẽdzesiwo me.

# Nukae Nye Vovototoa?

__Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)__: VPN xoxowo wɔa mɔ si le dedie, si wotsɔ nya ɣaɣlawo ŋlɔe le wò mɔ̃a kple adzɔge dɔwɔƒe si dzi VPN dɔwɔƒea kpɔ ŋusẽ ɖo dome. Mɔ sia ɣlaa wò Internet dzi dɔwɔnawo ɖe ŋku siwo le ŋku lém ɖe ŋuwò, abe kɔmpiutadzidzelawo, ISP-wo, alo dziɖuɖudɔwɔƒewo ene. Wozãa VPN geɖe hena taɖodzinuwo abe amewo ƒe ŋkɔwo me toto, nyatakaka siwo ŋu woxe mɔ ɖo le anyigba ƒe nɔnɔme nu, kple ametakpɔkpɔ tso Internet dzi ŋɔdzidonamewo me ene.

![nɔnɔmetata223](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ZecHub/zechub/main/site/Privacy_Tools/assets/image-223.png)


__Decentralized VPNs (dVPNs)__: To vovo na ema la, dVPNs zãa blockchain kple peer-to-peer mɔ̃ɖaŋununya tsɔ wɔa decentralized network of nodes. Wotoa node siawo dzi toa ezãlawo ƒe ʋuwo ƒe zɔzɔ dzi, si wɔnɛ be esesẽna na dɔwɔƒe ɖeka aɖeke be wòalé ŋku ɖe network bliboa ŋu alo akpɔ edzi. Zi geɖe la, dVPN-wo te ŋu nɔa te ɖe mɔxexeɖenu nu wu, elabena dziɖuɖumegã aɖeke meli si kpɔa network la dzi o. Wosɔ nyuie na ezãla siwo le adzamenyawo kple dedienɔnɔ si nyo wu dim.

# Dedienɔnɔ Ŋuti Nukpɔsusuwo Ne èle VPN Tiam

Ne èle VPN dɔwɔnu tiam la, ele vevie be nàbu dedienɔnɔ ŋuti nu siwo gbɔna ŋu:

1. __Encryption__: Kpɔ egbɔ be VPN zã nya ɣaɣla ƒe ɖoɖo sesẽwo abe OpenVPN alo WireGuard tsɔ kpɔ wò nyatakakawo ta tso mɔxexeɖedɔléle nu me.

2. __No-Logs Policy__: Di dɔwɔƒe siwo si no-logs ɖoɖo sesẽ aɖe le, si fia be womeléa wò Internet dzi dɔwɔnawo ŋuti nuŋlɔɖiwo ɖe asi o.

3. __Server Locations__: Bu anyigbamama si le server teƒewo ŋu be nàkpɔ nyatakakawo tso nuto vovovowo me eye nàɖe ɣeyiɣi si woatsɔ awɔ dɔe dzi akpɔtɔ.

4. __Kill Switch__: Kill switch aɖe wò internet la ɖa ne VPN kadodoa ɖiɖi, si axe mɔ na wò nyatakakawo be woagadze o.

5. __Ameŋunyatakakawo Kple Ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒe__: Ku nu me tso VPN dɔwɔƒea ƒe ameŋunyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ ƒe nuwɔnawo kple eƒe ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒe ŋu, elabena dukɔ vovovowo ƒe nyatakakawo léle ɖe te ƒe se vovovowo le wo si.

6. __Leak Protection__: Kpɔ egbɔ be VPN xe mɔ na DNS kple WebRTC leaks be wò ŋkɔ madze o.

7. __Asi kple Nɔnɔmewo__: Da gazazã kple nɔnɔme siwo dɔwɔƒe vovovowo naa kpɔ be nàkpɔ ɖeka si sɔ kple wò nuhiahiãwo kple gazazã.

# VPN siwo Xɔa Zcash

Zcash (ZEC) nye cryptocurrency si wowɔ na ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo tsɔtsɔ nyo ɖe edzi, si wɔe be wònye tiatia si ame ɖekaɖeka siwo di be yewomaɖe yewoƒe ŋkɔ o le yewoƒe ganyawo me la lɔ̃na wu. Togbɔ be menye VPN dɔwɔƒewo katãe xɔa Zcash tẽ o hã la, wo dometɔ aɖewo ate ŋu axɔ cryptocurrency fexexe to domenɔlawo ƒe dɔwɔƒewo dzi. Ke hã, ele vevie be nàbia VPN dɔwɔƒea tẽe be yeakpɔ fexexe ƒe mɔnu yeyetɔ kekeake. VPN dɔwɔƒe ʋɛ aɖewo siwo wonya be woxɔa cryptocurrency fexexee nye esi:

1. [__Mullvad ƒe VPN__ .](https://mullvad.net/en)
   
   Mullvad VPN nye VPN dɔwɔƒe si ŋu wodea bubui ŋutɔ si wonya ɖe eƒe ɖokuitsɔtsɔna vevie ɖe zãlawo ƒe adzamenyawo kple dedienɔnɔ ŋu ta. Enye VPN dɔwɔƒe ʋɛ siwo xɔa cryptocurrency, siwo dometɔ aɖewoe nye... 
   Zcash, abe fexexemɔnu ene. Mullvad ƒe nu veviwo dometɔ aɖewoe nye:

   __No-Logs Policy__: Mullvad zɔna ɖe no-logs ɖoɖo sesẽ aɖe dzi, si fia be medzraa nyatakaka aɖeke si ku ɖe wò Internet dzi dɔwɔnawo ŋu ɖo o.

   __Strong Encryption__: Subɔsubɔdɔa zãa nya ɣaɣla ƒe ɖoɖo sesẽwo, siwo dome WireGuard hã le, tsɔ kpɔa wò nyatakakawo ta.

   __Multi-Hop VPN__: Mullvad na tiatia na multi-hop kadodowo, siwo kplɔa wò ʋuwo to server geɖewo dzi be woatsɔ ado adzamenyawo kple dedienɔnɔ ɖe ŋgɔ.

   __Tɔdzisasrã ƒe Nɔnɔme__: Tɔdzisasrã ƒe nɔnɔme le esi si ate ŋu akpe ɖe ame ŋu wòato mɔxexeɖenu ƒe afɔɖeɖe aɖewo ŋu.

   __Akɔntabubu si Womeɖe Ŋkɔ O Wɔwɔ__: Wodea akɔnta xexlẽdzesi si wowɔ le vome na zãlawo, si wɔnɛ be megahiã be woaxɔ ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo le asidede agbalẽ te me o.

   __Zcash Fexexe__: Mullvad xɔa Zcash abe fexexe ƒe tiatia ene, si na be ezãlawo te ŋu xea fe ɖe woƒe nudɔdɔ ta kple adzamenyawo si wodo ɖe ŋgɔ.Mullvad VPN.

3. [__Nym VPN (dVPN)__ Ƒe 1999 ƒe ɣleti gbãtɔ me.](https://nymtech.net/)
   
   Nym VPN nye VPN si woɖe ɖe vovo (dVPN) si léa fɔ ɖe zãla ƒe adzamenyawo kple dedienɔnɔ dodo ɖe ŋgɔ ŋu to ameŋunyatakakawo ƒe kadodo si wotu ɖe ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo dzi dzi. Nym VPN wɔa dɔ to vovo na VPN xoxowo to 
   mixnet zazã, afisi wotsɔa zãlawo ƒe ʋuwo toa nodes ƒe network dzi. Nym VPN ƒe nɔnɔme veviwo dometɔ aɖewoe nye:

   __Decentralization__: Nym VPN nye decentralized kple open-source, si ɖea afɔku si le central control kple censorship me dzi kpɔtɔna.

   __Adzamenyawo Mixnet__: Subɔsubɔdɔa kplɔa zãlawo ƒe ʋuɖoɖo to mixnet dzi, si wɔnɛ be esesẽna na dɔwɔƒe ɖeka ɖesiaɖe be wòalé ŋku ɖe network ƒe ʋuɖoɖo ŋu alo adzro eme.

   __Node Operation__: Zãlawo ateŋu awɔ nodewo ŋudɔ atsɔ akpe asi ɖe network la ŋu eye woakpɔ teƒeɖoɖowo, si ana xɔtuɖoɖowo nade teƒe ɖeka geɖe wu.

   __Adzamenyawo Sesẽ__: Wowɔ Nym VPN kple adzamenyawo le susu me, eye mebia tso ezãlawo si be woana ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo le akɔntabubu wɔwɔ me o.
