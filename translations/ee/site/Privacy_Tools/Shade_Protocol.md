# Vɔvɔli ƒe Ðoɖowɔɖi

![Vɔvɔli ƒe Ðoɖowɔɖi ƒe Dzesi](https://framerusercontent.com/images/Af8EzlNiwGr4FTFX7oqdBuE.png)

## Nukae nye Vɔvɔli ƒe Ðoɖowɔɖi?

Shade Protocol nye DeFi (Decentralized Finance) ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖo si kpɔa ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo ta si ƒe taɖodzinue nye be yeana ganyawo ŋuti nu vovovowo esime wòle ekpɔm be ezãlawo ƒe nya ɣaɣlawo kple dedienɔnɔ le wo si. Wotue ɖe Secret Network dzi, Shade Protocol léa fɔ ɖe ganyawo ƒe nɔnɔme si wotu ɖe ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo dzi wɔwɔ ŋu, si naa dɔwɔnuwo abe stablecoins, nunɔamesi siwo wowɔ, gadodo ƒe mɔnuwo, kple bubuwo ezãlawo. Shade Protocol ƒe adzɔnu vevitɔe nye Silk Stablecoin, si ƒoa ameŋunyatakakawo ƒe nɔnɔmewo kple asixɔxɔ ƒe liƒo nu ƒu.

## Mɔ̃ɖaŋununya Si Wozã 

**Adzame Nyatakakadzraɖoƒe**

Wotu Shade Protocol ɖe Secret Network, si nye layer 1 blockchain si naa ameŋunyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ smart contracts dzi. Secret Network zãa nyatakaka siwo wotsɔ nya ɣaɣlawo ŋlɔ, nusiwo dona, kple nɔnɔme tsɔ kpɔa egbɔ be asitsatsa ƒe nya ɣaɣlawo le ɣaɣla, si kpɔa ezãla ƒe nyatakakawo ta be woagaɖe wo ɖe go le dutoƒo agbalẽ me o.

![Network ƒe Nɔnɔmetata ɣaɣla](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:828/format:webp/1*yyZ5hFOw6z8zXX_rgJn5iw.png)

Secret Network na be dɔwɔlawo te ŋu tua dɔwɔɖoɖo siwo woɖe ɖe vovo kple nyatakaka siwo wotsɔ nya ɣaɣlawo ŋlɔ, le Secret kple blockchain bubuwo dzi le dzɔdzɔme nu to cross chain communication dzi, si ʋua zazã yeye sẽŋuwo na Web3.

**Cosmos SDK kple Tendermint Nu vevi**

Wowɔ Cosmos be woatsɔ aƒo blockchains siwo to vovo la nu ƒu to mɔ nyuitɔ kple egbegbe tɔ siwo dzi woato atu blockchain siawo gɔme to dɔwɔnu yeyewo abe Tendermint kple Cosmos SDK zazã me. 
Ðoɖowɔɖia zãa Cosmos SDK, si nye modular ɖoɖo si wotsɔ tu blockchain dɔwɔɖoɖo siwo woate ŋu akeke ɖe enu eye woate ŋu awɔ dɔ aduadu. Esia na Shade Protocol kpɔa viɖe tso Cosmos ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ŋusẽ kple dɔwɔwɔ ɖekae me.

![Cosmos SDK kple Tendermint ƒe Nɔnɔmetata vevi](https://s3.ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/gimg.gateimg.com/learn/83dfbb311bfd6ce22d1c9468160d8e33b4c5ce8e.jpg)

Le nukpɔsusu ɖeka kple network dedienɔnɔ gome la, Shade Protocol zãa Tendermint Core, si naa Byzantine Fault Tolerant (BFT) ƒe nukpɔsusu ɖeka, si naa dedienɔnɔ gã kple nuwuwu kabakaba.

**Adzamenyawo Takpɔkpɔ Ganyawo Gbɔkpɔha**

DeFi zãa nya ɣaɣlawo takpɔkpɔ ƒe mɔnu deŋgɔwo abe asitsatsa siwo wotsɔ nya ɣaɣlawo ŋlɔe, sidzedze zero ƒe kpeɖodziwo, kple ame ŋutɔ ƒe smart contracts tsɔ léa zãla ƒe adzamenyawo kple nyatakakawo ƒe nya ɣaɣlawo ɖe te le blockchain networkwo dzi. To mɔ̃ɖaŋunuwo abe Secret Network ƒe adzame nubablawo kple Cosmos SDK zazã hena dɔwɔwɔ aduadu me la, egbɔkpɔnu siawo wɔnɛ be woate ŋu awɔ asitsatsa ɣaɣlawo, ame ŋutɔ ƒe nunɔamesiwo dzi kpɔkpɔ, kple nyatakakawo ɖeɖe ɖe go tiatia aɖe, si kpɔa egbɔ be ganyawo ƒe dɔwɔnawo gakpɔtɔ le dedie eye wowɔ ɖe ameŋunyatakakawo ŋuti sewo dzi. Mɔnu sia kpɔa ezãlawo ta tso amewo yometiti kple ametafatafa me, si naa ganyawo ƒe ablɔɖe si woɖe ɖe vovo esime wole ameŋunyatakakawo ŋuti dzidzenuwo dzi wɔm.

## Dɔbiagbalẽviwo

**Vɔvɔli kple Seda ƒe Dzesiwo**

Shade Protocol zãa nukpɔkpɔ ƒe safuiwo na eƒe dzesi vevi eveawo: Vɔvɔli ($SHD) kple Seda ($SILK). Vɔvɔli ($SHD) wɔa dɔ geɖe, ewɔa dɔ abe gaxɔ, dziɖuɖu, kple gakpɔkpɔ mama ƒe dzesi ene le lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖoa me. Wozãnɛ le gadede asi, dziɖuɖu ŋuti aɖaŋuɖoɖowo, gazazã ƒe ɖoɖowɔwɔ, asitsatsa, kple gagbalẽwo me. Silk ($SILK), le go bubu me la, nye protocol’s privacy focused stablecoin, pegged to a vovovo kusi si me xexeame katã ƒe ga kple adzɔnuwo abe sika, Bitcoin, USD, Euro, kple Yen.

### Adzame

Susu vevi siwo tae zãlawo ate ŋu alɔ̃ Shade Protocol dometɔ ɖekae nye adzamenyawo si wònana. Wokpɔa asitsatsa siwo le Shade dzi ta be amewo nagakpɔ wo o, si wɔnɛ be ganyawo ŋuti nyatakakawo gakpɔtɔ nye nya ɣaɣlawo. To vovo na dutoƒo blockchain xoxo siwo me amesiame kpɔa asitsatsa ŋuti nyatakakawo le la, Shade Protocol ƒe dzesiwo zãa nyatakaka siwo wotsɔ ɣla, nusiwo dona, kple nɔnɔmewo, si fia be nyatakaka veviwo nɔa ɣaɣla ɖe ŋku siwo le ŋku lém ɖe eŋu. Esia nana be ezãlawo ƒe nyatakakawo kple dedienɔnɔ nyona ɖe edzi, si wɔnɛ be ame ɖekaɖekawo te ŋu wɔa ganyawo ŋuti dɔwɔnawo evɔ womeɖea woƒe nyatakakawo alo asitsatsa ŋutinya ɖe go o, si wɔnɛ be wokpɔa wo ta tso ŋɔdzinu siwo ate ŋu ado mo ɖa me eye wokpɔa ganyawo ŋuti nya ɣaɣlawo ta.

### Asixɔxɔ si Li Ke

Silk Stablecoin naa asixɔxɔ ƒe liƒo, si le vevie ŋutɔ na ezãla siwo di be yewoaƒo asa na tɔtrɔ si do ƒome kple cryptocurrencies zi geɖe. Wotsɔa seda blaa nunɔamesiwo ƒe kusi aɖe, si kpena ɖe eŋu be eƒe asixɔxɔ nɔa anyi le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe. Stablecoin ɖesiaɖe kpɔa eƒe asixɔxɔ tso teƒe aɖe koŋ, zi geɖe la, wotsɔa wo sɔna kple United States dɔlar. Gake mɔnu sia hea afɔku veviwo vɛ, eye zi geɖe la, womedea dzesii o. Seda ɖea afɔku siawo dzi kpɔtɔna to eƒe liƒo me léle ɖe asi le xexeame ƒe tɔtrɔ kpata dome evɔ meɖoa ŋu ɖe asixɔxɔ ƒe dzesi ɖeka abe United States dɔlar ene ŋu o. Ke boŋ wotu Silk ƒe asixɔxɔ ɖe dzesi si woda kpekpeme na si woyɔna be Silk Currency Basket dzi. Kusi sia me xexeame ƒe ga tiatia aɖewo (USD, CAD, EUR, JPY) kple adzɔnuwo abe Bitcoin kple sika ene le. To ga kple nunɔamesi geɖewo dede eme me la, Silk kpɔa peg ƒe liƒo geɖe wu ne wotsɔe sɔ kple ga ɖekaɖekawo. Numekuku kple dodokpɔ geɖe ɖee fia be sika kple Bitcoin, abe asixɔxɔ ƒe nudzraɖoƒe siwo ŋu kakaɖedzi le ene, wɔa dɔ wu ga xoxowo, si tae wotsɔ wo de kusia me.

### Decentralisation ƒe ɖoɖowɔwɔ

Abe DeFi ƒe mɔ̃ ene la, Shade Protocol wɔa dɔ titina dziɖuɖu aɖeke manɔmee, si naa ezãlawo kpɔa ɖokuisinɔnɔ le ganyawo gome. Shade Protocol ƒe dziɖuɖu si woɖe ɖe vovo la ɖea mɔ na eƒe nuto si me dzesiwo le be woakpɔ gome le nyametsotsowɔwɔ me tẽ. Token xɔlawo ateŋu ado tɔtrɔwo ƒe susu ɖa, ada akɔ ɖe afɔɖeɖewo ŋu, eye woawɔ akpa aɖe le ɖoɖowɔɖia ƒe ŋgɔyiyi kple mɔfiame me. Esia nana be woama dziɖuɖua ɖe amesiwo kpɔ gome le eme ƒe ƒuƒoƒo vovovowo me tsɔ wu be dziɖuɖumegã aɖe nalée ɖe asi. To blockchain mɔ̃ɖaŋununya zazã me la, dziɖuɖu ƒe ɖoɖoa le gaglãgbe, ele dedie, eye metrɔna o, si doa lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖo si xɔa amewo katã ɖe eme wu eye wòte ŋu nɔa te ɖe nɔnɔme sesẽwo nu wu.

### Dɔwɔwɔ ɖekae

Cosmos ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe akpa aɖe nyenye fia be Shade Protocol ateŋu awɔ nu kple blockchain bubuwo bɔbɔe, si ana gazazã nanyo ɖe edzi eye wòana mɔnukpɔkpɔ geɖe zãlawo be woawɔ woƒe nunɔamesiwo ŋudɔ le network vovovowo dzi. To ameŋunyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ ƒe mɔ̃ɖaŋununya siwo tso Secret Network zazã me la, Shade Protocol kpɔa egbɔ be cross chain dɔwɔna siawo lé nya ɣaɣlawo kple fɔmaɖimaɖi me ɖe asi. Menye ɖeko dɔwɔwɔ aduadu sia doa ŋusẽ dɔwɔɖoɖo siwo woɖe ɖe vovo ƒe asitɔtrɔ kple dɔwɔwɔ ko o, ke edoa blockchain ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖo si do ƒome kple wo nɔewo eye wòsẽ wu hã ɖe ŋgɔ, si ɖea ŋuɖoɖo ɖe kadodo siwo sa ɖe aga ŋu dzi kpɔtɔna eye wòdoa ganyawo gbɔ kpɔnu siwo woɖe ɖe vovo ƒe zazã ɖe ŋgɔ le mɔ si keke ta wu nu.

## Nyanuwuwuw

Shade Protocol nye mɔɖela ƒe dɔ le DeFi ƒe teƒea, si naa ganyawo gbɔ kpɔnu siwo wotu ɖe mɔ̃ɖaŋununya sesẽ dzi si wotu ɖe ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ dzi. To stablecoin si me adzamenyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ ƒe nɔnɔmewo le nana kple ganyawo ŋuti dɔwɔnu gbogbo aɖewo toto vɛ me la, Shade Protocol ɖoe be yeakpɔ ezãla siwo dea asixɔxɔ dedienɔnɔ, liƒo, kple dziɖuɖu ƒe ɖoɖowɔwɔ ŋu la gbɔ.

Shade Protocol ƒe gɔmeɖose siwo nye ɖekawɔwɔ kple asixɔxɔ léle naa gɔmeɖoanyi sesẽ na liƒo kple ezãla ƒe nuteƒekpɔkpɔ si me kuxi aɖeke mele o, si na wòɖoe be wòanye etsɔme kplɔla le DeFi ɖoɖowɔɖiwo me. Esi dziɖuɖu si trɔna ɖe nɔnɔmewo ŋu, si woate ŋu atrɔ asi le eŋu, kple mɔfiame si me kɔ nyuie si dzi woato ato Vɔvɔli ƒe Ðoɖowɔɖi ƒe gɔmedzenu yeyewo vɛ ta la, ɖikeke mele eme o be dzidziɖedzi kabakaba ƒe ŋutete lolo ŋutɔ.
