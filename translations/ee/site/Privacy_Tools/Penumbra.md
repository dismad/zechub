# Penumbra ƒe agbalẽ 

Penumbra li be wòanye layer-1 network si le ŋgɔ yim, si ŋu wokpɔ ta na bliboe, si le ho ʋlim le Cosmos ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ me. Enaa ezãlawo te ŋu wɔa asitsadɔ dedie, tsɔa dzesiwo dea afɔku me eye woɖɔlia wo nɔewo, eye wowɔa asitsatsa evɔ womeɖea woƒe metadata ɖe go le kɔsɔkɔsɔa dzi o. Abe blockchain si wokpɔ ta na bliboe ene la, enaa asitsatsa siwo wokpɔ ta na hã le web dzi evɔ megblẽa nu le ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo alo decentralization ŋu o — Penumbra ƒe adzamenyawo le gɔmedzedzea me, eye asitsatsa siwo me kɔ alo asixɔxɔ ƒe ƒuƒoƒo si me kɔ mele o.

## Mɔ̃ɖaŋununya si wozãna le Penumbra IBC me 

IBC ƒe Ðekawɔwɔ: Va le dzodzom le xexlẽdzesi ɖeka dzi enye Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC) protocol, si nye mɔ̃ɖaŋununya yeye kple nu yeye si naa Penumbra ƒe dɔwɔwɔwo nɔa bɔbɔe. Ele vevie ŋutɔ na dɔwɔwɔ ɖekae dodo ɖe ŋgɔ le network la me. IBC hã wɔa dɔ to blockchain network vovovowo ƒe kadodo kple nyatakakawo ɖɔliɖɔli me. Gawu la, IBC wɔa akpa vevi aɖe le nunɔamesiwo ƒe asitɔtrɔ, nyatakakawo mama, kple kadodo si le Penumbra dome si me kuxi aɖeke mele o la wɔwɔ me. Dɔwɔwɔ aduadu kple mɔ̃ɖaŋununya sia kpena ɖe ezãlawo ŋu be woatsɔ dzesiwo kple nunɔamesiwo ayi blockchain vovovowo dzi fuɖename aɖeke manɔmee. Le go bubu me la, IBC hã kpena ɖe scalability ŋu, enaa asitsatsa kabakaba nyona ɖe edzi, enaa dɔwɔwɔ nyona ɖe edzi, eye wòɖea megbetsitsi le Penumbra ƒe network me dzi kpɔtɔna.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs): Penumbra zãa zero-knowledge proof cryptography mɔ̃ɖaŋununya tsɔ kpɔa egbɔ be woɖo kpe asitsatsaawo katã dzi eye woda asi ɖe wo dzi evɔ meɖea nyatakaka aɖeke abe amesi ɖoe ɖa, amesi xɔe, alo ga home si wotsɔ yi na ame bubuwo ene o.

Decentralized Exchange with Privacy: Panumbra ƒe kadodoa ƒe taɖodzinu vevitɔe nye ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ. Netwɔƒea ate ŋu awɔ asitsatsa ƒe mɔnu si woɖe ɖe vovo si doa asitsatsa kple crypto asitsatsa ƒe adzamenyawo ɖe ŋgɔ esime wòle ganyawo ŋuti nyatakakawo hã ta kpɔm eye wònaa gome ezãlawo be woakpɔ woƒe asitsatsa ƒe mɔnuwo ɖeɖefia dzi.

## Zã Nyawo

Asitsatsa si le dedie: Penumbra ɖea mɔ na ezãlawo be woate asitsatsa siwo ŋu wokpɔ ta na le crypto nunɔamesi ɖesiaɖe me kpɔ. Esi wònye be Penumbra nye blockchain si ŋu wokpɔ ta na bliboe ta la, enaa ezãlawo te ŋu wɔa asitsatsa le wo ɖokui si, si kpɔa ame si ɖoe ɖa, amesi xɔe, ga home, kple nunɔamesi ƒomeviwo ta ne wole zɔzɔm to kɔsɔkɔsɔwo me; ekpɔa egbɔ be asitsatsaawo nye ame ŋutɔ tɔ eye womate ŋu akpɔ wo alo aɖe wo gɔme ne zãla aɖeke meɖe mɔ na wo o. Edze be míagblɔ be Penumbra ƒe adrɛs ɖesiaɖe doa alɔ nukpɔkpɔ ƒe ŋutete - xɔtutu sia ɖea mɔ na ezãlawo be woatiae be yewoaɖe yewoƒe dɔwɔna afia ame etɔ̃lia siwo ŋu woɖe mɔ ɖo le sedziwɔwɔ alo susu bubuwo ta. Enyo be míade dzesii be Penumbra ƒe asitsatsa wɔa dɔ le kekeli ƒe duƒuƒu nu eye asitsatsa ƒe fetu sue aɖe koe woxena. Akpe na kpeɖodzi-ɖe-nusɔsrɔ̃ si dzi woda asi ɖo. 

Staking: Le xeviwo ƒe ŋkumekpɔkpɔ me la, Penumbra di be yeanɔ nuɖuɖu ƒe kɔsɔkɔsɔa tame. Esi wònye be wonye xexeame ƒe kpeɖodzinana ƒe kadodo gbãtɔ si ŋu wokpɔ ta na ta la, woƒe taɖodzinu ɖeka koe nye be woakpɔ egbɔ be amedɔdɔwo ƒe nya ɣaɣlawo le wo si eye woabu akɔnta na amesiwo da asi ɖe edzi. Nu dodzidzɔname bubu si Penumbra naa enye PEN staking token, si kpɔa network la ta esime wònaa gome token xɔlawo be woakpɔ gome le dɔa dzikpɔkpɔ me to kɔsɔkɔsɔ ƒe akɔdada ƒe ɖoɖowo dzi. Wotsɔa native token si woyɔna be dPEN naa amedɔdɔ ɖesiaɖe si le network la dzi. Be nànya ko la, woŋlɔa dPENwo ɖe tadeaguƒe siwo wokpɔ ta na la me eye woate ŋu adzra wo abe nunɔamesi bubu ɖesiaɖe ene. Le nuwo ƒe kɔsɔkɔsɔdziɖuɖu ƒe akpa dzi la, kadodoa ƒe etsɔme le amesiwo kpɔ gome le eme la si me esi wole akɔ dam ɖe aɖaŋuɖoɖowo dzi eye wole gome kpɔm le nyametsotsowo wɔwɔ me esime wole ekpɔm be dɔwɔƒe si le titina la mekpɔ ŋusẽ ɖe dɔa dzi o. 

Token Swap: Token Swap nye zazã bubu na Penumbra. Netwɔƒea doa alɔ shielded swaps le crypto nunɔamesi ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe dome — ne woɖoe ɖa la, wotre swaps katã nu! To ZSwap zazã me la, ezãlawo ate ŋu aɖɔli crypto nunɔamesi eve ɖesiaɖe evɔ womaɖe nyatakaka veviwo abe asitsatsa ŋutinya alo ɣeyiɣi ƒe dzesi siwo ate ŋu akpe ɖe ame ŋu be woawɔ avu kple ganyawo ƒe ametafatafa o. Togbɔ be Maximal Extractable Value (MEV) sɔ kple kɔsɔkɔsɔ siwo me kɔ hã la, kɔsɔkɔsɔ siwo ŋu wokpɔ ta na bliboe abe Penumbra ene la mekpɔa MEV o elabena network ƒe asitsatsa meɖea nyatakaka siwo ku ɖe zãla ƒe dɔwɔna ŋu la dona o.

Asitsatsa: Kɔsɔkɔsɔ si wokpɔ ta na bliboe — Penumbra, naa asitsalawo kpɔ ŋusẽ be woaɖe nyatakaka siwo ku ɖe woƒe asitsatsa ƒe mɔnuwo kple nɔnɔmewo ŋu afia tsɔ wu be woaɖe woƒe dɔwɔnawo katã afia xexeame.
