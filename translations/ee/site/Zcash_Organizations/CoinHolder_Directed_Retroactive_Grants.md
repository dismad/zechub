# Gakpekpeɖeŋunana Siwo Wotsɔna Ðoa Nu Megbee Si Gakuxɔlawo Ðo Mɔ Ðo

**Coinholder-Directed Retroactive Grants Program** naa ga dɔ siwo wowu enu siwo na asixɔxɔ si ŋu woate ŋu aɖo kpee na Zcash ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ.

Exɔa ga tso **Coinholder-Controlled Fund** (12% le block rewards + Deferred Dev Fund Lockbox nuku), si ZIP 1016 ɖo eye wowɔ dɔ le NU6.1 (November 2025) me. 

Ðoɖowɔɖia zɔna ƒe etɔ̃ ɖesiaɖe vaseɖe afã 3 lia (~2028). Gawo le multisig me le Key-Holder Organizations (e.g., Shielded Labs, FPF) si.

---

Gakpekpeɖeŋunana nye **retroactive only** - teƒeɖoɖo na nudzɔdzɔ siwo va yi (e.g., ŋgɔyiyi, ɖekawɔwɔ, nutoa me tutu). Ele be dɔ si woawɔ le etsɔme nawɔ dɔ ɖe Zcash Community Grants (ZCG, 8% of rewards) dzi.

---

## Dɔbiagbalẽvi Siwo Dze

**Nu kpɔkpɔ**:

Dɔ si wowu enu, si ŋu woate ŋu aka ɖo le dutoƒo si ɖea vi na Zcash (adzamenyawo, vixɔxɔnyi, dɔwɔnuwo, dedienɔnɔ).

**Dzidzenu** (gakuxɔlawo daa asɔ):

  - Eɖe ŋusẽkpɔɖeamedzi fia eye asixɔxɔ si wòtsɔ na.
  - Ðekawɔwɔ kple Zcash ƒe asixɔxɔwo (adzamenyawo, decentralization).
  - Kpeɖodzi siwo me kɔ (kɔda, nyatakakawo, metriks).


**Mɔxexeɖenu aɖeke meli o**: 

Ame sia ame (ame ɖekaɖekawo, ƒuƒoƒowo, habɔbɔwo) ate ŋu abia alo woaɖoe.

**Aɖaŋuɖoɖo siwo le ho ʋlim**: 

Woda akɔ ɖe vovo; ZEC ƒe kpekpeɖeŋu kɔkɔtɔ kekeake ɖua dzi.

**Veto ate ŋu adzɔ**: 

To Key-Holders gbɔ le senyawo alo nuveviwɔame na ezãlawo/nutoa me (ƒã, kple susu).

## Ale Si Nàwɔ Atsɔ Dɔbiagbalẽvi Ana

**Dzra Aɖaŋuɖoɖoa ɖo**:

- Ðe dɔ si wowu enu, ŋusẽkpɔɖeamedzi, kpeɖodzi (kadodowo, nusiwo woatsɔ awɔ dɔe) me.
- Gblɔ ga home si wobia (si sɔ kple ZEC/USD) kple nusi tae.
- De fexexe ƒe adrɛs kple amesi nàte ŋu aƒo ka na hã eme.


**Tsᴐe yi**:


- Miŋlɔe ɖe Zcash Nutome Nyamedzroƒe (forum.zcashcommunity.com) le Gakpekpeɖeŋunana ƒe akpaa dzi.
- Tanya: "[Gbugbɔgadzɔ ƒe Kpekpeɖeŋunana] - [Dɔa ƒe Ŋkɔ]".
- Zã dzesidenu ƒe ɖoɖo si me kɔ.


**Nuwuwu game**:


- Ɣleti etɔ̃ ɖesiaɖe; woɖe gbeƒãe le nyamedzroƒe/X (e.g., Q4 2025: October 12, 2025, 11:59 PM UTC).
- Nusiwo woɖo ɖa megbe: Gbugbɔ ɖoe ɖa le ɣeyiɣi si gbɔna me alo nàbia ZCG.


**De dɔ asi na**:

Ðo nutoa me ƒe nyabiasewo ŋu le ŋkeke 30 ƒe numetoto me.

#### de dzesii: FPF ye kpɔa edzi (sedziwɔwɔ, kadodo); aɖaŋuɖoɖo siwo woƒo ƒu ɖe GitHub dzi be woate ŋu akpɔe.

## Akɔdada ƒe Ðoɖo kple Cadence

**Ƒe etɔ̃ ɖesiaɖe ƒe ɣeyiɣi ƒe didime** (e.g., Q4 2025, Q1 2026):

**Ɣeyiɣi mamlɛtɔ si woatsɔ aɖoe ɖa**: 

Mid-quarter (woɖe gbeƒãe).

**Ŋkeke 30 ƒe Totoɖeme**: 

Edzea egɔme le ɣeyiɣi mamlɛa megbe; dzro/nyaŋuɖoɖo le nyamedzroƒe ƒe kawo me (wowɔa kaƒoƒo kpuie zi geɖe).

**Akɔdada Ɣeyiɣi**: 

Ekplɔa ŋkuléle ɖe nu ŋu ɖo (e.g., ~kwasiɖa 1-2 le November me na Q4).

**Emetsonuwo & Gazazã**: 

Ema megbe kpuie; gakpekpeɖeŋu siwo dzi woda asi ɖo siwo woxena ɖe ɖoɖo ɖesiaɖe nu (KYC na >$50k).

**Akɔdada**:

  - Gakuxɔlawo daa akɔ kple ZEC ƒe gaxɔgbalẽviwo (womaxe mɔ ɖe gaku nu o; Orchard pool dze).
  - Mɔnu: Akɔdada si woɖe ɖe vovo (e.g., to akɔdada ƒe dziɖuɖumegãwo dzi).
  - **Dɔdada**: ZEC ƒe ameha 420,000 ya teti + ame akpa gãtɔ bɔbɔe ẽ.
  - Ame siwo le wo ɖokui si ƒe akɔdada; gaku siwo woate ŋu azã ake le aɖaŋuɖoɖowo katã me.
  - Nusiwo do tso eme le dutoƒo, siwo me kɔ.


#### de dzesii: **Fifia ƒe Kpekpe (tso January 5, 2026 dzi)**: Wowu Q4 2025 nu (November ƒe akɔdada); Q1 2026 ƒe nyatakakawo ɖoɖo ɖa kpuie - lé ŋku ɖe nyamedzroƒe ŋu hena gbeƒãɖeɖewo.
