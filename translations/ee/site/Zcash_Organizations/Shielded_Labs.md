# <img src="https://i.ibb.co/fGYHTfZ5/Shielded-labs-shield-white-2.png" alt="Alt Text" width="50"/>    Shielded Labs


[Shielded Labs](https://shieldedlabs.net) woɖoe abe Switzerland Habɔbɔ ene le December 2022 me, eye medo ƒome kple Zcash Community Grants (ZCG) o eye mewɔ ɖoɖo aɖeke be yeabia ZCG ƒe gakpekpeɖeŋu o.

Zcash le kpekpem ɖe Zcash ŋu fifia tso United States habɔbɔ 2, Electric Coin Company (ECC) kple Zcash Foundation, siwo nye amesiwo koe xɔa ŋgɔyiyi ƒe ga la gbɔ. ZCG menye habɔbɔ o, ke boŋ enye kpekpeɖeŋunana kɔmiti le Gɔmeɖoanyia te, si kpɔa eƒe ga dzi hekpɔa edzi. Habɔbɔ siwo le wo ɖokui si geɖe wu siwo kpea asi ɖe Zcash ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖoa ŋu hiã be woate ŋu akaka ŋusẽ eye woazu esiwo woatsɔ awɔ dɔ le teƒe vovovowo wu.

Zcash le gaŋutiɖoɖo vevi siwo kpɔa ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo ta tum ɖo, eye afɔku li be dɔ si wɔm wole la ate ŋu axe mɔ ɖe sededewo nu. Vevietɔ la, Zcash ƒe dodo ɖe U.S. nye nu ɖeka si ate ŋu ado kpo nu, eye ele be míatu lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖo si te ŋu nɔa te ɖe wo léle nu kpata. 

Shielded Labs ƒe nɔƒe le Switzerland elabena enye dukɔ si me crypto-xɔlɔ̃wɔwɔ le eye wòkpɔ ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ ƒe ŋutinya didi aɖe.

## Nukpɔkpɔ

Shielded Labs ƒe taɖodzinue nye be yeadzi zãlawo ƒe xɔxlɔ̃ ɖe edzi, awɔ zazã ƒe nɔnɔme yeyewo na Zcash, eye yeakpe asi ɖe ɖoɖowɔɖiwo wɔwɔ ŋu. 

**Afisiwo woalé ŋku ɖo le aɖaŋunuwɔwɔ me ƒe wɔwɔfia:** 

### Zãla ƒe Vixɔxɔ: 

Shielded Labs awɔ dɔ be yeadzi zãlawo ƒe xɔxlɔ̃ ɖe edzi to Zcash ƒe ŋkɔ ŋɔŋlɔ ɖe asitɔtrɔ geɖe me, dɔwɔwɔ kple dɔwɔƒewo le fexexe ƒe ƒoƒo ɖekae ŋu, kple Zcash ƒe anyinɔnɔ ɖe edzi le DEXwo kple Defi dɔwɔɖoɖowo me. Atsɔ dɔ siwo doa Zcash zazã ɖe ŋgɔ, abe Zcash Shielded Assets ene, aɖo nɔƒe gbãtɔ, eye wòado alɔ tɔdzisasrãwo, AMMwo, kple fiat on/off-ramps ƒe ŋgɔyiyi.

### Amegbetɔ ƒe Gomenɔamesiwo Kple Ablɔɖe: 

Shielded Labs awɔ ɖeka kple amegbetɔ ƒe gomenɔamesiwo kple dukɔmeviwo ƒe ablɔɖe habɔbɔwo be woatsɔ Zcash ade amesiwo hiãe wu la si me.

### Ðoɖowɔɖiwo ƒe Totoɖeme: 

Vaseɖe fifia la, ECC ye wɔ network upgrades katã. Resilience bia be woawɔ decentralization, eye Shielded Labs akpe ɖe Zcash ŋu be decentralization to asikpekpe ɖe core protocol development ŋu me.

## Ɖoe anyi

Shielded Labs awɔ fork na Zcash GitHub nudzraɖoƒea, awɔ dzɔtsoƒe ƒe kɔpi yeyea ŋudɔ, eye wòawɔ hehe biabia na ƒoƒo ɖekae. Ne míetsɔe be ga kple dɔwɔlawo ƒe nunɔamesiwo sɔ gbɔ le esi la, Shielded Labs akpɔ kpekpeɖeŋu si ayi edzi abe forked codebase nudzraɖoƒea dzikpɔla ene.

Anye zi gbãtɔ si network ɖɔɖɔɖo aɖe dze egɔme tso dɔwɔla si le eɖokui si si ɖee fia be Zcash nye network si ŋu mɔɖeɖe mele o vavã.

Ɣeyiɣi aɖe ƒe kpekpeɖeŋu si wotu ɖe dɔa dzi le gɔmedzedzea me ahiã be wòakpe ɖe eŋu wòaɖo gɔmeɖoanyi na habɔbɔa be wòatsi.

## Gakpekpeɖeŋunana

Le gɔmedzedzea me la, Shielded Labs nye ga bliboe to nudzɔdzɔwo me & taɖodzinue nye be yeakpɔ ga atsɔ adze dɔwɔwɔ gɔme le ƒe gbãtɔ me. 

Le gadodo ɣeyiɣi didi gome la, Shielded Labs gblɔ aɖaŋuɖoɖo aɖe si nye "Network Sustainability Fund" be wòanye Zcash ŋgɔyiyi gaxɔla to block ƒe fetu dzi le afã si kplɔe ɖo le Q4 2024 me megbe. 

Esia abia be woawɔ ɖoɖo ɖe gadodo ƒe ɖoɖowɔɖi si ŋu wotrɔ asi le kple mɔnu si woatsɔ aɖo ga ɖe Sustainability Fund & ɖoɖowo hena dɔwɔwɔ ɖe zcashd/zebrad dzi.
