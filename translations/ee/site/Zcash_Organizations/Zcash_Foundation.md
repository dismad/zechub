# <img src="https://i.ibb.co/Nr9xqgX/image-2024-02-03-165803028.png" alt="Alt Text" width="50"/>  Zcash Foundation

## Dɔdasi Ŋuti Nyatakaka

Míaƒe taɖodzinu le ZF enye be míawɔ dɔwɔnu siwo akpe ɖe network siwo le ʋuʋu ɖi ŋu, si ana amesiame kple amesiame nate ŋu akpɔ eya ŋutɔ ƒe nyatakakawo ta le eya ŋutɔ ƒe ɖoɖowo nu. Nu vevitɔ si le ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo ŋue nye be woate ŋu atia nusi woama na ame bubuwo alo womagblɔe o. Ame ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo gbɔ kpɔkpɔ lɔ ɖokuisinɔnɔ kple lɔlɔ̃ ɖe edzi siaa ɖe eme; ele vevie na amegbetɔ ƒe bubu kple dukɔmeviwo ƒe habɔbɔwo ƒe yiyi le lãmesẽ me.

## Amewo Kple Ƒuƒoƒowo

Zcash Foundation ƒe dɔwɔlawo le ~12 siwo woma le xexeame godoo. Woƒe susu nɔa ɖoɖowɔɖiwo wɔwɔ, ɖoɖowɔɖiwo ƒe dedienɔnɔ, ɖoɖowɔɖiwo dzikpɔkpɔ, xɔtuɖoɖowo wɔwɔ kple Zcash nutoa me wɔnawo dzikpɔkpɔ ŋu. Menye Zcash Foundation koe nye dɔwɔwɔ le Zcash ŋu ko o eye eyata eƒoa eɖokui ɖe ameŋunyatakakawo takpɔkpɔ ƒe mɔ̃ɖaŋununyawo hã toto vɛ me na [amegbetɔ ƒe kpekpeɖeŋunana](https://zfnd.org/sai/) kple na amewo katã ƒe nyonyo.

Zcash Foundation Grants naa mɔnu aɖe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖoa be woatsɔ akpɔ ga na dɔ siwo kpena ɖe ameŋunyatakakawo ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ƒe ɖoɖoa ŋu abe [Zebra](https://zebra.zfnd.org/) (node ​​ƒe dɔwɔwɔ si le eɖokui si na Zcash) & [FROST](https://frost.zfnd.org/) kpakple [vovovowo](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2021/06/08/tor-project-receives-670k-from-zcash-open-major-grants-for-coding-language-upgrade/) dɔ bubuwo to [Zcash Nutome Gakpekpeɖeŋunakɔmiti dzi](https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/). 

Gɔmeɖoanyiha la hã léa [Zcon](https://zfnd.org/zcon/), ƒe sia ƒe ƒe takpekpe si wowɔna le ame ŋutɔ ŋkume si ku ɖe Zcash ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ ŋu eye wòdoa alɔ gbe me, nutoa me tɔwo ƒe ɖoɖowo abe [Zcon Vozes](https://zfnd.org/zcon-voices/), nutome tɔxɛ [takpekpewo](https://zfnd.org/zcash-dev-summits/), si nye Zcash Foundation A/V [Klub](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/the-zero-knowledge-audiovisual-club/43733), fifia Sidzedze Zero Odio kple Nukpɔkpɔ [Klub](https://www.zkav.club/) kple nuto vovovo bubu siwo le ʋuʋu ɖi [yɔyɔ](https://zfnd.org/arborist-calls/), nutoa me [nudzɔdzɔwo](https://zfnd.org/zf-events/#:~:text=Zcomm) kple [AMA ƒe](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/community-call-with-the-zcg-candidates-december-2024/49707).

Zcash Foundation wɔa [Zcash Asitsadzesiwo ƒe ɖoɖoa dzi](https://zfnd.org/new-zcash-trademark-policy/) per the updated conditions of it's donation by the Electric Coin Co. which helps "Takpɔkpɔ Zcash, eƒe dɔwɔƒe siwo do ƒome kplii, kple Zcash nutoa me tɔwo ƒe ŋkɔ ta" to "de dzesi(ing) nyatakakatsoƒe siwo dziɖuɖua da asi ɖo, si ɖea nyatakaka siwo nye ametafatafa alo esiwo mesɔ o ƒe mɔnukpɔkpɔ dzi kpɔtɔna" kple "De dzesi(ing) dziɖuɖua ƒe dɔwɔɖoɖowo kple dɔwɔnawo, si ɖea ametafatafa ƒe dɔwɔnawo ƒe mɔnukpɔkpɔ dzi kpɔtɔna." 

## Habɔbɔ ƒe Dɔwɔɖoɖo

The Zcash Foundation is a [501(c)(3)](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/820707962) tax-exempted, non-profit [dɔmenyonunana](https://zfnd.org/#:~:text=Committed%20to%20Transparency). Tsɔ kpe ɖe woƒe [Team](https://zfnd.org/#:~:text=Our%20Team), woƒe Habɔbɔ si nye [Dɔdzikpɔlawo](https://zfnd.org/#:~:text=Board%20of%20Directors) eye wòana nutoa me dziɖuɖu ƒe tiatiawɔwɔ nanɔ bɔbɔe to akɔdada le Zcash Nutome Aɖaŋuɖoha [Panel](https://zfnd.org/zcash-community-advisory-panel/).

## Gakpekpeɖeŋunana

Zcash Foundation megaxɔa ga tso Zcash ƒe ŋgɔyiyi [ga](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1014) as of November, 2024. Fifia wozãa ga si woatsɔ awɔ The Foundation to [nudzɔdzɔ siwo wowɔna tsã, siwo dzi woate ŋu aɖe adzɔ le](https://www.jotform.com/form/251874383638065). Woate ŋu akpɔ alesi wozãa gae le woƒe ɣleti etɔ̃ ɖesiaɖe ƒe [Transparency Reports](https://zfnd.org/category/transparency-reports/).

Ðoɖo sia naa ga ƒuƒoƒo siwo le wo ɖokui si be woawɔ ŋgɔyiyi gã siwo yia edzi (alo dɔ bubu) si aɖe vi na Zcash ƒe lãwo ƒe agbenɔnɔ. Ame atɔ̃ ƒe kɔmiti si Zcash Nutome Aɖaŋuɖoha tiae tiaa kpekpeɖeŋunana. Míele dzi dem ƒo na ƒuƒoƒo kple habɔbɔ siwo di be yewoado Zcash zazã, dedienɔnɔ, adzamenyawo kple exɔlɔ̃ ɖe ŋgɔ vevie be woabia Zcash Nutome Kpekpeɖeŋunana!
