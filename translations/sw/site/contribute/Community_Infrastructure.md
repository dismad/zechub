# ZecHub Jamii Miundombinu Mfumo


### Miundombinu ya Jumuiya 

All nodes, lightwalletd instances and other infrastructure is freely accessible and exists to increase developer access to the Zcash network. Hii inaruhusu ZecHub bounty mpango ili kukidhi watengenezaji katika huru zaidi & streamlined mbadala kwa kazi za mtu binafsi juu ya [Dework](https://dework.zechub.org). 


## Matumizi 

- Tag @ZecHubDAOMember katika kituo cha #zechub katika Zcash Global Discord kuomba ufikiaji. 

- Tutakuongeza kwenye kituo ambapo unaweza kupokea msaada na kushiriki taarifa mpya. 

- https://discord.gg/zcash

## Miradi 

- We are interested in what you make! ZecHub will issue bounties between 5-15 ZEC for documentation of your new app. 

- Mawazo fulani: 

Shared Video Player tovuti na URL kuingia ambayo inahitaji ZEC malipo.

Relay Encrypted Memos kati Zcashd Node na akaunti Farcaster.

Shielded Exchange Service kwamba kumbukumbu ZEC zilizowekwa kwa ajili ya mali na anwani ya jibu, Hedged ununuzi wa mali nyingine kufanywa juu ya Ethereum L2.
