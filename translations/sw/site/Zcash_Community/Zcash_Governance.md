# Zcash Funding & Utawala Maelezo ya jumla

Zcash ya on-chain fedha mfano, block-zawadi utaratibu, na majukumu ya mashirika makubwa

## 1. Jinsi Zcash Block Rewards Kazi

Zcash ni uthibitisho-ya-kazi cryptocurrency. Kila block kuchimbwa inashiriki yake ** block ruzuku ** (ZEC wapya kuundwa) pamoja na ada ya manunuzi kwa mujibu wa kanuni fasta itifaki kuweka na mitandao upgrades.

- **Mfano wa sasa (baada ya NU6 / Novemba 2024 na kuendelea) ** 
  Kama ya Aprili 2026 usambazaji ni:

Mpokeaji. Asilimia. Nini fedha / hali.
|--------------------------------|------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
Wachimbaji - 80% - Malipo ya moja kwa moja kwa wachimbaji.
Zcash Community Grants (ZCG) 8% Msaada wa jamii (inaendelea hadi ~2028)
Lockbox (protocol-controlled) 12% Fedha zinakusanyika; hakuna utaratibu wa matumizi bado; kura ya jamii ya baadaye inahitajika

- ** Kabla ya NU6 mfuko wa kihistoria wa dev (2020-Nov 2024) ** 
  20% ya kila ruzuku ya jumla ilienda moja kwa moja kwa mashirika ya maendeleo:

  - 7% -> Electric Coin Company (ECC) / Bootstrap Mradi 
  - 5% -> Zcash Foundation (ZF) 
  - 8% -> Zcash Misaada ya Jumuiya (ZCG)

Hii 20% "mali ya maendeleo" ilibadilishwa na 8% ZCG + 12% lockbox mfano kupitia [ZIP 1015](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1015).

### Mapendekezo ya Maendeleo: ZIP 1016 - Jumuiya na Coinholder Funding Model
ZIP 1016 (ilipendekezwa Februari 2025, hali: Ilipendekezwa) inaanzisha mfano wa ufadhili uliodhibitiwa zaidi.
- Kuendelea 8% ya mgawo kwa ZCG.
- Kubadilisha 12% lockbox katika "Coinholder kudhibitiwa Mfuko" (mbegu na fedha zilizopo lockbox + unaoendelea 12% misaada ya kundi).
- Kuamsha mtindo huu mpaka nusu ya tatu (takriban miaka 3).
- Kuwawezesha wamiliki wa sarafu za ZEC kupiga kura kila robo mwaka juu ya misaada kupitia mchakato uliofafanuliwa na jamii (wingi rahisi, kiwango cha chini cha quorum ya 420,000 ZEC).
- Require Key-Holder Organizations (currently including ZF and Shielded Labs, with Bootstrap/ECC referenced in grant contexts) to administer disbursements via multisig, bound by legal agreements and coin-holder decisions.
- Maintain all ZIP 1015 requirements on lockbox use (funding ecosystem grants).

This proposal aims to shift from organization-controlled to direct coin-holder governance for the 12% allocation. It does not alter the ZIP process or trademark rules.

## 2. Mashirika ya Msingi na Vyanzo vya Fedha Zao

**Electric Coin Company (ECC) / Bootstrap Project**  
- Waumbaji wa awali wa Zcash (2016). 
- Kihistoria kupokea ~ 7% ya mfuko dev hadi Novemba 2024. 
- In January 2026, the core engineering and product team resigned from Bootstrap/ECC due to governance disputes and formed the Zcash Open Development Lab (ZODL).  
- ECC/Bootstrap haipati tena ufadhili wa moja kwa moja wa itifaki na haitumii tena timu ya msingi ya maendeleo. Inategemea michango, udhamini, na hazina yake mwenyewe. 
- Ana umuhimu wa kihistoria lakini si tena shirika la maendeleo ya itifaki ya kazi. 
-> Angalia wasifu kamili: [Electric Coin Company](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/electric-coin-company)

** Zcash Open Maabara ya Maendeleo (ZODL) ** 
- Formed in January 2026 by the original Zcash protocol developers (the core ECC engineering and product team) after they left Bootstrap/ECC.  
- Kupanda zaidi ya $25 milioni katika ufadhili mbegu kutoka wawekezaji kubwa ikiwa ni pamoja na a16z Crypto na Coinbase Ventures. 
- The team, consisting of the original inventors and developers of the Zcash protocol, continues core protocol development, ZIP contributions, and privacy-focused tools including the Zodl mobile wallet (rebranded from Zashi).  
- Hakuna moja kwa moja kwenye mzunguko wa mkataba wa fedha; inafanya kazi kama VC-kuungwa mkono huru maabara ililenga kuendeleza Zcash faragha miundombinu. 
-> Angalia wasifu kamili: [ZODL](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/ZODL)  
-> tovuti rasmi: [zodl.com](https://zodl.com/)
  
**Zcash Foundation (ZF) ** 
- Independent 501 ((c) ((3) isiyo ya faida ililenga miundombinu, programu ya node, utafiti, na afya ya mazingira. 
- Kihistoria kupokea 5% ya mfuko dev. 
- No longer receives direct protocol funding post-NU6. Relies on donations and grants.  
- Inashikilia alama ya biashara ya Zcash (iliyotolewa na ECC mnamo 2019) na ina jukumu muhimu katika utawala. 
- Anaendesha Jopo la Ushauri wa Jumuiya ya Zcash (ZCAP) na husaidia kuwezesha uchaguzi wa jamii. 
- Hufanya kazi kama Shirika la Mmiliki wa Ufunguo chini ya ZIP iliyopendekezwa 1016. 
-> Angalia wasifu kamili: [Zcash Foundation](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/zcash-foundation)  
-> tovuti rasmi: [zfnd.org](https://zfnd.org/)

**Zcash Misaada ya Jumuiya (ZCG) ** 
- The Zcash Community Grants program funds independent teams and projects to perform major ongoing development and other work for the public good of the Zcash ecosystem.  
- Utoaji wa misaada huamuliwa na kamati iliyochaguliwa na jamii. 
- Inaendelea kupokea kamili 8% ya block tuzo (baada ya NU6), kusimamiwa kupitia Financial Privacy Foundation. 
- Misaada hutolewa kupitia maombi ya uwazi na mchakato wa kupiga kura wazi kwa jamii. 
-> Angalia wasifu kamili: [Zcash Community Grants](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/zcash-community-grants)  
-> tovuti rasmi: [zcashcommunitygrants.org/](https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/)

**Fundation ya Faragha ya Fedha (FPF) ** 
- Shirika lisilo la faida lililoingizwa katika Visiwa vya Cayman. 
- Receives the 8% block subsidy allocation directly from the protocol (per ZIP 1015) and handles all legal, financial, and operational administration for the Zcash Community Grants program.  
- Inatoa muundo wa mwavuli na msaada wa kiutawala kwa shughuli za ZCG, pamoja na malipo, mikataba, na kufuata. 
- ZCG inafanya kazi kama chombo cha kujitegemea kilichochaguliwa na jamii chini ya mwavuli wa FPF. 
-> Angalia wasifu kamili: [Financial Privacy Foundation](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/financial-privacy-foundation)  
-> Tovuti rasmi: [Financialprivacyfoundation.org/](https://www.financialprivacyfoundation.org/)

** Maabara ya Kulindwa ** 
- Independent, mchango unaofadhiliwa Zcash msaada shirika makao yake makuu katika Uswisi. 
- The first organization in the Zcash ecosystem that has never received direct or indirect funding from the Development Fund or block rewards.  
- Inazingatia mipango ambayo inawafaidi wamiliki wa ZEC na inatoa kipaumbele kwa sauti ya mmiliki katika kuunda mwelekeo wa Zcash. 
- Hufanya kazi kama Shirika la Wamiliki wa Ufunguo chini ya ZIP iliyopendekezwa 1016 kwa usimamizi wa Mfuko unaodhibitiwa na Mmiliki wa Sarafu. 
- Inachangia maendeleo ya itifaki, mchakato wa ZIP, na utawala (wakilishi wa mhariri wa Zip). 
-> Angalia wasifu kamili: [Labs Shielded](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/shielded-labs)  
-> tovuti rasmi: [shieldedlabs.net](https://shieldedlabs.net/)

## 3. Utawala - Jinsi Maamuzi Yanavyofanywa

Utawala wa Zcash ni mchanganyiko wa "sheria za itifaki kwenye mnyororo" na "makubaliano ya kijamii nje ya mnyororo":

1. ** Mchakato wa ZIP (Mapendekezo ya Uboreshaji wa Zcash) ** 
   - Mtu yeyote anaweza kuwasilisha ZIP. 
   - Mjadala wa umma kwenye vikao, Discord, GitHub. 
   - Wahariri wa ZIP (kwa sasa Jack Grigg, Daira-Emma Hopwood, Kris Nuttycombe katika uwezo wa mtu binafsi, Arya kutoka ZF, na wawakilishi kutoka Shielded Labs) kukagua na kuamua kukubalika. 
   - ZIPs kukubalika ni pamoja na katika kuboresha mtandao ijayo.

2. **Mkataba wa Alama ya Biashara (2019-2024) ** 
   - ECC ilitoa alama ya biashara ya Zcash kwa ZF mnamo 2019. 
   - Mkataba awali required makubaliano ya pande zote mbili kutoka ECC na ZF kwa ajili ya yoyote ya kuboresha mtandao kujenga mpya makubaliana itifaki. 
   - Mnamo Aprili 2024 ECC ilitangaza nia ya kumaliza; ilani rasmi ya kumalizika ilitolewa Agosti 2024. 
   - As of 2025, ZF is the sole steward of the Zcash trademark and has adopted a new permissive trademark policy reflecting ecosystem decentralization. The trademark no longer functions as a governance veto mechanism.

3. ** Zcash Jopo la Ushauri wa Jumuiya (ZCAP) ** 
   - Kikundi cha kujitolea cha wataalamu wa mazingira. 
   - Inatumika kwa ajili ya uchaguzi wa jumuiya isiyo ya lazima juu ya maamuzi makubwa.

4. ** On-chain Ratification ** 
   - Mara baada ya kuboresha mtandao ni deployed, wengi wa mtandao hash kiwango lazima kupitisha (hakuna ngumu-fork hatari kama makubaliano imefikiwa).

5. ** Uelekeo wa Baadaye - The Lockbox & ZIP 1016** 
   - Fedha 12% lockbox ni kujilimbikiza katika itifaki. 
   - ZIP 1016 proposes converting this into a Coinholder-Controlled Fund with quarterly coin-holder voting and multisig administration by Key-Holder Organizations (ZF and Shielded Labs currently noted).
