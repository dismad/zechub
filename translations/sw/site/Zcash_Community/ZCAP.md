# Jopo la Ushauri wa Jumuiya ya Zcash 

The Zcash Community Advisory Panel, or ZCAP, is a panel conformed by members of the Zcash Community, established as a means to involve the community and soliciting their advisory input whenever the Zcash Foundation needs to make a decision involving or afecting said community. Since it's creation in 2018, the panel has helped define the way to go for the Zcash foundation in many important decisions, some of which include:

[Zcash alama ya biashara](https://github.com/ZcashFoundation/Elections/blob/master/2018-Q2/General-Measures/monetary-policy.md): Kura hii ilikuwa kuhusu kama Zcash Foundation lazima kufuata makubaliano ya kisheria na Electric Coin Company (ECC) kushiriki umiliki na udhibiti wa Zcash alama ya biashara

[Sera ya Fedha](https://github.com/ZcashFoundation/Elections/blob/master/2018-Q2/General-Measures/monetary-policy.md): Kura hii ilikuwa kuhusu kama Zcash Foundation inapaswa kutetea mabadiliko yoyote ya sera ya fedha Zcash, kama vile kiwango cha utoaji, ratiba ya nusu, au kiwango cha mfumuko wa bei

[ASIC Upinzani](https://github.com/ZcashFoundation/Elections/blob/master/2018-Q2/General-Measures/asicresistance_ballot.md): Kura hii ilikuwa kuhusu kama Zcash Foundation lazima kipaumbele ASIC upinzani kama thamani na lengo kwa ajili ya mtandao Zcash

[Zcash Community misaada Kamati ya Uchaguzi](https://zfnd.org/zcg-election-june-2023/): Kura hii ilikuwa kuhusu kuchagua wanachama wawili wa Zcash Community Grants (ZCG) kamati, ambayo ni wajibu wa kupitia na kufadhili mapendekezo ya ruzuku kwa ajili ya miradi Zcash kuhusiana

[Programu ya Zcash Madogo Grants](https://zfnd.org/launching-the-zcash-minor-grants-program/): Kura hii ilikuwa kuhusu kupitisha au kukataa maombi ya ruzuku kwa ajili ya miradi midogo ya Zcash ambayo iliomba ufadhili kutoka Zcash Foundation

[Kupitishwa na maendeleo ya Zcash Shielded Mali](https://vote.heliosvoting.org/helios/elections/5dd57b92-01ed-11ec-a0a8-ae3066fac55d/view), which enables the use of DeFi generic assets within the private by default Zcash ecosystem.

[Uchaguzi wa Zcash Foundation Board of Directors wanachama](https://vote.heliosvoting.org/helios/elections/960b8832-60f9-11ed-b96e-ee6991044d9e/view)


The idea behind ZCAP is that it is formed by a diverse group of community members with different nationalities, roles and opinions, to guarantee it remains comitted to its goal of representing the voice and interest of Zcash users and stake holders, while maintining an open and diverse network.

Kuanzia Oktoba 2023, ZCAP inaundwa na [wanachama 188](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Y-GbVFA1csIgPbvHOR-1ruOlh7YdA-CHvMtd6R8iSdY/edit#gid=0)Pia, Zcash Foundation "imepanua vigezo vya kustahiki kwa uanachama wa ZCAP, pamoja na vigezeo vilivyopendekezwa na wanachama wa jamii ya Zcash".
