# Zcash Wallet Fund Ufufuzi

** Kwa nini kuweka ufunguo wako binafsi? ** 

Funguo binafsi ni siri ya usalama wa mali yako digital. Kuweka yao salama na kamwe kushiriki nao na watu wa tatu ni muhimu. 

> Katika muktadha huu ** Mbegu Maneno** inaweza kuonekana kama sawa na ufunguo binafsi.

By maintaining control over your private keys, the recovery process is always possible. There are 2 types of Zcash private keys (transparent and shielded), you can easily import them into your wallet, whether by using the Sweep Funds function or importing them as a new account. By keeping control over your private keys, you maintain total control over your assets, ensuring ownership, security and peace of mind.

# Usalama na Wajibu

Ni muhimu kwa watumiaji kuelewa hatari zinazohusika katika kushughulika na funguo binafsi na kuweka funguo hizi kulindwa kutokana na upatikanaji wa zisizoidhinishwa. usalama wa fedha inategemea wajibu wa mtumiaji kulinda funguo zao binafsi.

## Ufufuzi wa Fedha na Ywallet

YWallet ni kutambuliwa kama moja ya chaguzi bora kwa ajili ya kurejesha fedha inaccessible, wote kutoka * uwazi tu * na kulindwa funguo binafsi.

### 1) Private muhimu Import 

1. Pakua Ywallet[](https://ywallet.app)

2. Mara moja kufunguliwa, chini kulia bonyeza 'Zaidi'

3. Chagua 'Akaunti'

4. Katika kona ya juu kulia bonyeza pamoja ishara 

[Pia ishara kifungo](https://i.postimg.cc/xJbVz7gB/plus.png)

5. Badilisha 'Rudisha akaunti' 

6. Ingiza neno la msingi au ufunguo wa kibinafsi

> **Kumbuka**: Kama wewe uliofanyika fedha katika mkoba ambayo haina msaada wa anwani ulinzi (Trust, Coinomi, Guarda nk) utakuwa na kutumia 'Sweep Funds' kipengele.

### 2) Funds Sweepe

1. Pakua Ywallet[](https://ywallet.app)

2. Mara moja kufunguliwa, chini kulia bonyeza 'Zaidi'

3. Scroll chini ya Tools sehemu, bonyeza 'Sweep'

4. Ingiza neno lako mbegu (Gap kikomo scans kwa anwani ya ziada yanayotokana na mbegu)

[Sweep Fedha screen](https://i.postimg.cc/3055CBcN/sweep.png)

5. Ingiza Thamani Pool kwa ajili ya marudio unataka kutumia (Exchanges kutumia Transparent)

6. Ingiza anwani ya marudio kwa ajili ya ambapo unataka amana fedha.
