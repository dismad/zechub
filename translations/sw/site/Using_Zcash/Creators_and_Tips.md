## Zcash.me

[Zcash.me Logo](https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_images/1983177127806533632/LbLuoQjB_400x400.jpg)

### Maelezo ya jumla

**Zcash.me** is a profile directory and discovery platform that makes it easy for anyone to find and send ZEC to Zcash users. It serves as the "Linktree for Zcash" - a simple way to share your shielded address and social presence with a single link.

Sehemu. Maelezo
|-------------------|---------|
** Tovuti** [https://zcash.me/](https://zcash.me/) |
♬ Twitter ♬ @zcashme](https://x.com/zcashme) |
 * Profile Format *  ya juu `zcash.me/[username]` |
** Hali**. Kuishi (na vipengele katika maendeleo)

### Sifa Muhimu

#### Inapatikana kwa Sasa
- ** Custom Profile kurasa ** - ukurasa binafsi na jina la mtumiaji, bio, avatar, na eneo
- **Shielded Address Display** - One-click nakala kifungo kwa ajili ya anwani yako umoja
- ** Kijamii Link Ushirikiano ** - X, LinkedIn, Telegram, na tovuti desturi
- ** Mfumo wa Uthibitishaji ** - Alama iliyothibitishwa kwa uaminifu wa jamii
- **Referral Leaderboard** - Kufuatilia juu waelekezaji na viongozi wa jamii
- ** Hali ya Shughuli ** - Inaonyesha muda wa mwisho wa kazi

### Jinsi ya Kuanza
1. Tembelea [https://zcash.me/](https://zcash.me/) na bonyeza ** Kujiunga **
2. Chagua jina la kipekee, kuongeza bio na picha profile
3. Weka anwani yako ya umoja wa Zcash (`u1...`)
4. Unganisha akaunti zako za kijamii
5. Shiriki kiungo chako: `zcash.me/[yourusername]`
