# <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/198608b2-9059-4cb7-aeb8-9354971376fd" alt="ZODL Logo" width="50"/> ZODL (Zcash Open Development Lab)

[Tovuti](https://zodl.com/) - [GitHub](https://github.com/AgoraCyber) - [X/Twitter](https://x.com/zodl_app) - [Kutoelewana](https://discord.gg/zodl)

## Taarifa ya Ujumbe

ZODL inaona ulimwengu bila ufuatiliaji mkubwa wa kifedha, ambapo watu wanaofuata sheria wanaweza kufanya shughuli kwa uhuru na kwa faragha. Shirika hilo linaamini kuwa hakuna uhuru bila faragha, na kwamba faragha ya kifedha inapaswa kuwa chaguo-msingi na ufikiaji wa soko haki ya binadamu.

## Historia na Mazingira

ZODL (Zcash Open Development Lab) was founded in early 2026 by Josh Swihart, the former CEO of the Electric Coin Company (ECC). In January 2026, the entire ECC engineering and product team resigned following a governance dispute with Bootstrap, the 501(c)(3) nonprofit board that oversees ECC. The core team that had originally designed, implemented, and maintained Zcash's most critical systems transitioned to ZODL to continue their work independently.

Zashi mkoba, awali maendeleo na kudumishwa chini ya ECC, ilikuwa rebranded kwa Zodl chini ya shirika jipya. Watumiaji hawakuwa na kuchukua hatua yoyote, kama programu rebranded moja kwa moja na update ijayo.

## Uongozi

- **Josh Swihart** - CEO & Founder (former CEO of the Electric Coin Company)

The broader team is composed of the original Zcash protocol creators and developers who previously built and maintained Zcash at ECC. This includes deep expertise in cryptography, zero-knowledge proofs, and protocol engineering.

## Bidhaa

### Zodl Wallet (zamani Zashi)

Zodl ni mkononi binafsi custodial mkoba iliyoundwa kwa ajili ya shughuli binafsi Zcash. Inachanganya kina itifaki-kiwango cha maarifa na user-kirafiki design, kuficha utata wakati kudumisha usalama.

** Majukwaa:**
- [iOS (App Store)](https://apps.apple.com/app/zodl/id6444974742)
- [Android (Play Duka)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.electriccoin.zcash)
- [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/co.electriccoin.zcash.foss/)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/AgoraCyber)

**Vipengele muhimu:**

- ** Kulindwa kwa chaguo-msingi:** shughuli zote kutumia mifuko Zcash ya kulindwa, kuficha mtumaji, mpokeaji, na kiasi cha shughuli kwa kutumia sifuri-ujuzi cryptography
- ** One-Tap Shielding:** Kubadilisha ZEC uwazi kwa fedha binafsi na bomba moja
- **CrossPay:** Tuma ZEC iliyohifadhiwa wakati wapokeaji wanapokea mali zao zinazopendekezwa (BTC, sarafu thabiti, nk)
- **Swaps binafsi:** Kujengwa katika swaps powered by NEAR nia, kuepuka kubadilishana centralized
- **Keystone Ushirikiano:** Hardware mfuko wa fedha msaada kwa ajili ya kuhifadhi baridi ya ulinzi ZEC kupitia hewa-gaped makubaliano kusaini
- ** Malipo ya Flexa: ** Tumia ZEC katika wauzaji wakubwa ikiwa ni pamoja na GameStop, Chipotle, Sheetz, na Barnes & Noble
- ** Ushirikiano wa Coinbase: ** Kununua ZEC moja kwa moja ndani ya mkoba bila usajili tofauti wa kubadilishana
- ** Ujumbe Encrypted:** Zcash-kiwango cha faragha kwa ajili ya ulinzi memos kando au huru ya shughuli
- ** Unified anwani:** Rahisi usimamizi wa anwani katika uwazi, Sapling, na Orchard mabwawa
- **Tor Support:** Kuboreshwa mtandao ngazi ya faragha kwa ajili ya shughuli za utangazaji

** Dhamira ya faragha:**
- Hakuna kufuatilia shughuli mkoba, historia ya shughuli, au tabia ya mtumiaji
- Tu anonymized ripoti ajali ni zilizokusanywa, na hakuna uwezo wa kuunganisha data kwa watumiaji maalum

### Utendaji na Athari

Tangu uzinduzi wake wa awali kama Zashi mnamo 2024, mkoba huo umesababisha ukuaji mkubwa katika mfumo wa ikolojia wa Zcash:

- Kukua bwawa la Orchard lililolindwa kutoka takriban ZEC 1M hadi 4M ZEC wakati wa 2025 (ongezeko la zaidi ya 400%)
- Imesindika zaidi ya dola milioni 600 katika swaps za ZEC tangu Oktoba 2025

## Jukumu katika Zcash Ecosystem

ZODL hutumikia jukumu la mara mbili katika mazingira ya Zcash:

1. ** Maendeleo ya Itifaki:** Timu inaendelea kuendeleza msingi wa programu ya itifaki ya Zcash na zana, kufanya kazi pamoja na timu zingine za mazingira kama vile Zcash Foundation na Zingo Labs
2. **User-Facing Products:** Building and maintaining the primary consumer wallet for Zcash, focused on making private digital payments accessible to mainstream users
3. ** Ukuaji wa Hifadhi ya Kuhifadhiwa:** Ubunifu wa mkoba wa ZODL umeelezewa kama "kichocheo kikubwa cha pekee kinachohusika na kuweka Zcash tena kwenye ramani"
4. ** Open Source:** Kazi zote ni wazi chanzo, kuruhusu mtu yeyote kukagua kanuni code, ukaguzi wa mkoba, na kuthibitisha utekelezaji

## Falsafa: Mlinzi wa Mwenye Enzi Kuu

ZODL's guiding philosophy is centered on the concept of "Guardian of the Sovereign" -- the belief that individuals should maintain full control over their financial privacy and sovereignty. The organization deliberately prioritizes user sovereignty over platform control, building tools that empower individuals rather than intermediaries.
