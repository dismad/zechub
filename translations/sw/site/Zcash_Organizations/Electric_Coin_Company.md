## Taarifa ya Ujumbe

The mission at ECC is to empower economic freedom. Financial privacy is core to this endeavor, and essential to protect human dignity, security, self-sovereignty, and consent. Obtaining financial privacy should be as easy as closing the curtains in your home. But with the emergence of Web2, power is deeply centralized, and those with control are already in your living room. You cannot close the curtains. We must build something new.

## Zashi: [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/zashi-zcash-wallet/id1672392439) - [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.electriccoin.zcash) - [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/co.electriccoin.zcash.foss/)

Zashi ni kujilinda ulinzi ZEC mkoba kwa ajili ya shughuli encrypted digital.

Zashi ni chombo kamili kwa ajili ya onboarding marafiki na familia katika Zcash na malipo binafsi digital. kubuni yake intuitive inafanya kuwa rahisi kwa mtu yeyote, hata wale bila uzoefu crypto kabla ya kuanzisha mkoba, kupata ZEC, na kuanza shughuli katika dakika.

Ushirikiano:
- [Coinbase:](https://electriccoin.co/blog/coinbase-zashi-1-2-release/) crypto newbies wanaweza kununua ZEC moja kwa moja ndani ya mkoba kuruka hassles ya usajili akaunti, KYC, na kuondoa sarafu kutoka kubadilishana.

- [Flexa:](https://electriccoin.co/blog/zashi-flexa-integration-is-here-spend-zec-at-thousands-of-retailers/) inafanya kuwa rahisi kwa kweli kutumia ZEC, kuruhusu watumiaji wapya kutumia katika maelfu ya maeneo ya rejareja kama Barnes & Noble, GameStop, na Chipotle.

- [Keystone:](https://electriccoin.co/blog/drumroll-please-introducing-zashi-keystone-hardware-wallet-integration-for-shielded-zec/) mafanikio makubwa kwa ajili ya jamii Zcash kwamba imekuwa miaka katika kufanya. ushirikiano huu alama mageuzi muhimu katika Zcash usability kwa kuwezesha kuhifadhi baridi ya walinzi ZEC.

## Utafiti na Maendeleo

Timu wameongoza mafanikio kutambuliwa katika matukio kadhaa, kwanza kuwa [uzinduzi wa Zcash](https://electriccoin.co/blog/zcash-begins/) kama matumizi ya kwanza ya kibiashara duniani ya Zero-Ujuzi uthibitisho.

Zaidi kadhaa zifuatazo kama vile [Sapling mtandao kuboresha](https://electriccoin.co/blog/sapling-activation-complete/) allowing for lighter, shielded transactions with a time reduction of 90% for constructing transactions, and a memory reduction of over 97%, enabling for shielded transactions on a mobile device for the first time.

mafanikio makubwa kuwa hivi karibuni. [Uundaji na kupelekwa](https://electriccoin.co/blog/nu5-activates-on-mainnet-eliminating-trusted-setup-and-launching-a-new-era-for-zcash/) Tangu mwaka wa 2022, hii imekuwa hai kwenye Zcash Mainnet na inaruhusu watumiaji kutumia ujenzi salama zaidi na rahisi kuboreshwa kwa itifaki yao ya malipo. 

## Ushirikiano na Ushirikishanaji 

The ECC team supports and contributes to the Zcash ecosystem in many ways, collaborating with both internal & external teams regularly to help maintain and improve the Zcash protocol. 

Partnerships include working with Gemini to enable shielded transactions on their platform, Flexa to enable easy Zcash mobile payments at retal locations, hardware wallet companies like Keystone to enable shielded cold storage of ZEC, Brave for Zcash support in the browser, & other teams in the Zcash ecosystem to bring imrovements to Zcash. 

ECC pia ni msaidizi wa fahari wa [PGP kwa Crypto](https://x.com/pgpforcrypto) a meeting series that brings together professionals, enthusiasts, and experts in the cryptocurrency space to discuss developments, share insights, and foster community engagement. PGPforCrypto is led by Board Member and VP of Strategic Alliances at Electric Coin Co, Paul Brigner.

## Sera na Udhibiti

Maarifa na uongozi wa ECC kuhusu faragha kwenye mnyororo na athari zake kwa usalama wa kibinafsi na kitaifa ni muhimu sana kwa watunga sera. juhudi thabiti za kuongoza sera ipasavyo kila inapowezekana zimefanywa tangu ECC ilipoanzishwa. 

Examples of outreach include dialogue with Financial Action Task Force (FATF) taking part in meetings which resulted in the organizations recommendations for the cryptocurrency sector. As a result of these efforts Zcash became approved by the New York State Department of Financial Services (NYDFS), the only privacy-protecting cryptocurrency to do so.
