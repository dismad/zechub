# Zaino Indexer

Zaino ni Indexer, maendeleo katika kutu na timu Zingo, ambayo inalenga kuchukua nafasi ya lightwalletd na kushinikiza mbele zcashd deprecation mradi.

Zaino offers essential features for both light clients, such as wallets and applications that do not require the full blockchain history, and full clients or wallets. It also supports block explorers, granting access to both the finalized blockchain and the non-finalized best chain and mempool managed by a Zebra or Zcashd full validator.

## Kwa nini kuwe na Index mpya?

Sababu kuu ni kupata tayari kwa ajili ya baadaye. Zcashd na lightwalletd zilijengwa katika 2016 forked kutoka code bitcoind, kwa kutumia C plus. Jukwaa na kanuni kutumika kujenga huduma zote mbili ni kuanza kupata zamani, vigumu kuongeza, kudumisha na kujenga makala ya kisasa juu ya.

Rust is a modern, robust and secure language that allows Zcash to be prepared for future development, inviting new developers to build plenty of new functionality on and around the Zcash ecosystem.

Hata hivyo, Zaino inakusudia kuwa nyuma sambamba ambapo inawezekana, Kutoa APIs na interfaces kwamba kusaidia kupunguza msuguano katika kupitishwa na kuhakikisha kwamba pana Zcash mazingira inaweza kufaidika na Zaino's enhancements bila muhimu rewrites au kujifunza curves.

Pia, Zaino itaruhusu kutenganisha mwanga mteja utendaji kutoka full node, kupitia RPC upatikanaji na maktaba mteja kamili, kuruhusu watengenezaji kuunganisha Zaino na kupata data mlolongo moja kwa moja kutoka zao mwanga matumizi ya mteja, kuweka data nyeti kutoka Zebra node kutengwa na salama.
