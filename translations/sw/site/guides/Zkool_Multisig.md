This guide provides a step by step walkthrough on how to perform multisig transactions using Zkool. It includes creating accounts, sending or receiving funds, and setting up distributed key generation (DKG) for multisig. Screenshots are included for each major step.

## 4. Kufanya Multisig Transactions: Kuanzisha Usambazaji Key Generation (Multisig)

Multisig katika Zkool inatumia **Distributed Key Generation (DKG) ** kuhakikisha washiriki wengi kudhibiti akaunti ya pamoja.



### Hatua 1: Kuanzisha DKG
Kuchagua ** Jina ** kwa mkoba pamoja (kwa mfano Anabelle).

Kuweka ** Idadi ya Washiriki **.
  
Chagua yako ** mshiriki kitambulisho **.
  
Kufafanua ** Idadi ya Signers Inahitajika (kizingiti) **.
    
Chagua ** Akaunti ya Fedha **.
  

! [img7](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8a90ca85-5439-4937-b16d-a570e69d55f0)



### Hatua ya 2: Ongeza Anwani ya Washiriki
- Enter each participants **Unified Address** (recommended).


**Kumbuka:** Kama wewe kutumia Orchard tu au Sapling tu anwani, multisig itakuwa mdogo kwa pool kwamba tu (Orchard au sapling). 
Hii ina maana mkoba pamoja hawezi kupokea fedha kutoka mabwawa mengine. 
Kwa upatanifu wa juu na kubadilika, daima tumia **Anwani za Umoja**. 


### Hatua ya 3: Run DKG raundi
Kusubiri kwa washiriki wote kubadilishana ** raundi ya 1 ** na ** mzunguko 2 ** vifurushi.

## Matokeo

Using Zkool, you can: create accounts, send and receive funds, and set up a **multisig wallet** using Distributed Key Generation. This ensures **enhanced security** and **collaborative and private fund management**.
