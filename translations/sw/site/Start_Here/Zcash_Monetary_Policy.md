# Zcash Msingi

## Ni nini uchumi wa Zcash?

Zcash's monetary base is the same as Bitcoin's fixed supply of 21 million ZEC currency units. Every 75 seconds, a new [block](https://zcash.readthedocs.io/en/latest/rtd_pages/glossary.html#:~:text=Block,mempool%20in%20an%20unconfirmed%20state.) ni kuchimbwa kwa blockchain Zcash na block tuzo ya 3.125 ZEC inakuja katika mzunguko. block hii tuzo ni kusambazwa kwa wachimbaji na [Zcash mfuko wa maendeleo](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1014).

The amount of the block reward cuts in half about every four years until all 21 million ZEC are in circulation. Zcash inflation almost precisely mimics that of Bitcoin. It is important to note that as new coins are created inflation goes down, and at each halvening the rate drops significantly.
