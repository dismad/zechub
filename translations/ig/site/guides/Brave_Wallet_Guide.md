## Ụdị Adreesị

Nke a bụ nkọwa maka ụdị adreesị Zcash ọ bụla:

**t-addr (t1...)** - Transparent addresses work like Bitcoin addresses, meaning transactions are public on the blockchain. They are easier to use with non-Zcash wallets but do not provide privacy.

** z-addr (zs...) ** - Adreesị echedoro na-eji teknụzụ nzuzo Zcash (Sprout / Sapling) iji zoo onye zitere, onye nnata, na ego. Naanị igodo nlele ma ọ bụ onye nwe ya nwere ike ikpughe nkọwa.

**UA (u... ma ọ bụ QR) ** - Unified Addresses na-ejikọta ọtụtụ ụdị adreesị (transparent, shielded, Orchard) n'ime otu. Nke a na-enye ohere maka ndakọrịta kachasị ma na-ahọrọ nzuzo ebe o kwere mee.
