# Zcash Akụrụngwa Echebe

Zcash Shielded Assets (ZSA) bụ nkwalite a na-atụ aro maka usoro Zcash nke ga-eme ka e nwee ike ịmepụta, nyefee, ma gbaa akụ omenala na Zcash chain.

Ọ bụrụ na ị maara usoro ERC-20](https://ethereum.org/en/developers/docs/standards/tokens/erc-20/) ọkọlọtọ akara na Ethereum blockchain, ZSAs bụ Zcash dị ka ERC-20 tokens bụ Ethereum.

Zcash Shielded Assets ga-eme ka e nwee ike ịmepụta akara ngosi omenala na Zcash blockchain, si otú ahụ na-ekwe ka akara ngosi ndị ọzọ karịa [ZEC](https://wiki.zechub.xyz/using-zec-privately) iji rite uru site na amaghị aha na nzuzo nke azụmahịa echekwara na Zcash blockchain.

A major potential use of ZSAs would be for the issuing of stablecoins on the Zcash protocol. Stablecoins are cryptocurrencies that peg their value to a fiat currency, such as the US Dollar or Euro. Currently, some of the most widely circulated stablecoins are ERC-20 tokens such as [USDC](https://www.circle.com/en/usdc) na [Dai](https://docs.makerdao.com/).

Ihe ọzọ enwere ike iji ZSA mee bụ maka inye akara njikwa. Dịka ọmụmaatụ, Zechub (onye na-ebipụta wiki a) bụ Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) ma nwee ike ịmepụta ma nye ndị otu ya ZSA maka ebumnuche ịtụ vootu na atụmatụ na mkpebi ọchịchị.

A na-emepe ZSA site na [QEDIT](https://qed-it.com/), n'okpuru a isi onyinye si [Zcash Foundation](https://wiki.zechub.xyz/zcash-foundation) na mmekorita ya na [Electric Coin Company](https://wiki.zechub.xyz/electric-coin-company). As this project is still being actively developed, updates are posted on [this thread](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/grant-update-zcash-shielded-assets-monthly-updates/41153) nke Zcash forum. The [ZSA onyinye ngwa](https://zcashgrants.org/gallery/25215916-53ea-4041-a3b2-6d00c487917d/33106640/) site na QEDIT dị site na Zcash Foundation grants website.

## ZSA Nkwado Onyinye

The ZSA aro maka echebe Àgwà (ZSA / UDA) e gosiri site [QEDIT](https://qed-it.com/) ndị otu iji wuo akụ na-echebe ihe na Zcash blockchain. A na-akpọkarị ndị a dị ka akụ ndị akọwapụtara onye ọrụ (UDA) ma ọ bụ dị ka Zcash Shielded Assets (ZSA).

Site na atụmatụ a, ndị otu na [QEDIT](https://qed-it.com/) plans to bring DeFi to the Zcash ecosystem, and at the same time enable the use of the best privacy technology within the existing DeFi ecosystem with regards to a pool survey, where the team asked, and the community answered: [generic shielded assets (ZSA/UDA) are the most requested feature at the moment](https://twitter.com/BenarrochDaniel/status/1428327864034791429)

Nkwupụta ndị a na-agbaso usoro [Zcash Improvement Proposal (ZIP) ]](https://zips.z.cash/zip-0000) nkọwapụta ma kọwaa ya na ZIP 226 & ZIP227.

1. [ZIP 226](https://qed-it.github.io/zips/zip-0226): Nyefee na Ọkụ nke Akụrụngwa Zcash Shielded
2. [ZIP 227](https://qed-it.github.io/zips/zip-0227): Mwepụta nke Zcash Shielded Assets
