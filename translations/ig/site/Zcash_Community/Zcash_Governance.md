# Zcash Funding & Governance Nchịkọta

Zcash's on-chain funding model, block-reward mechanics, na ọrụ nke ndị isi òtù

## 1. Otu Zcash Block Rewards si arụ ọrụ

Zcash bụ Proof-of-Work cryptocurrency. Ngwongwo ọ bụla a na-egwupụta na-ekesa ** block subsidy ** (ZEC e mepụtara ọhụrụ) gbakwunyere ụgwọ azụmahịa dị ka iwu protocol siri ike setịpụrụ site na nkwalite netwọk.

- ** Ihe nlere ugbu a (post-NU6 / November 2024 gaa n'ihu) ** 
  Site na Eprel 2026 nkesa bụ:

◯ Onye ọ na-akwụ ụgwọ ◯ Pasent ◯ Ego ole o nyere / ọnọdụ ya ◯
|--------------------------------|------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
Ndị na-egwupụta akụ. 80% Direct block reward to miners.
Zcash Community Grants (ZCG) 8% Onyinye obodo (na-aga n'ihu ruo ~2028)
 Lockbox (nke a na-achịkwa site na usoro iwu)  12%  Ego na-agbakọta; enweghi usoro mmefu ọ bụla; a chọrọ ntuli aka obodo n'ọdịnihu 

- **Pre-NU6 akụkọ ihe mere eme dev ego (2020-Nov 2024) ** 
  20% nke ego enyemaka ọ bụla na-aga ozugbo na òtù mmepe:

  - 7% -> Electric Coin Company (ECC) / Bootstrap Project  
  - 5% -> Zcash Foundation (ZF) 
  - 8% -> Zcash Community Grants (ZCG)

Nke a 20% "dev ego" e dochie anya 8% ZCG + 12% lockbox nlereanya site [ZIP 1015](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1015).

### Mgbanwe a na-atụ aro: ZIP 1016 - Community na Coinholder Funding Model
ZIP 1016 (atụmatụ February 2025, ọnọdụ: Atụmatụ) na-ewebata ụdị ego na-enweghị isi. Ọ ga-:
- Gaa n'ihu na-ekenye 8% na ZCG.
- Tụgharịa 12% lockbox n'ime a "Coinholder-achịkwa Fund" (mkpụrụ site ẹdude lockbox ego + na-aga n'ihu 12% ogbe subsidy).
- Tinye ụdị a ruo mgbe ọkara nke atọ (ihe dị ka afọ 3).
- Nye ndị nwere mkpụrụ ego ZEC ikike ịtụ vootu kwa ọnwa na onyinye site na usoro obodo akọwapụtara (ọnụ ọgụgụ dị mfe, opekempe quorum nke 420,000 ZEC).
- Require Key-Holder Organizations (currently including ZF and Shielded Labs, with Bootstrap/ECC referenced in grant contexts) to administer disbursements via multisig, bound by legal agreements and coin-holder decisions.
- Debe ihe niile ZIP 1015 chọrọ na iji igbe mkpọchi (nkwado ego nke usoro okike).

Ebumnuche a bụ ịkwaga site na njikwa nke nzukọ gaa na nchịkwa onye nwe mkpụrụ ego maka ntinye 12%. Ọ naghị agbanwe usoro ZIP ma ọ bụ iwu ụghalaahịa.

## 2. Òtù Ndị Bụ́ Isi na Ebe Ha Si Enweta Ego

**Electric Coin Company (ECC) / Bootstrap Project** 
- Ndị okike mbụ nke Zcash (2016). 
- N'akụkọ ihe mere eme natara ~ 7% nke ego dev ruo Nọvemba 2024. 
- Na Jenụwarị 2026, ndị ọrụ injinịa na ndị ọrụ ngwaahịa gbara arụkwaghịm na Bootstrap / ECC n'ihi esemokwu ọchịchị wee guzobe Zcash Open Development Lab (ZODL). 
- ECC/Bootstrap anaghịzi enweta ego protocol na-akwụghị ụgwọ ma ọ naghịzi eji ndị ọrụ mmepe isi. Ọ dabere na onyinye, nkwado, na akụ nke aka ya. 
- Na-ejide akụkọ ihe mere eme dị mkpa mana ọ bụghịzi nzukọ mmepe usoro iwu na-arụ ọrụ. 
-> Lee profaịlụ zuru ezu: [Electric Coin Company](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/electric-coin-company)

**Zcash Open Development Lab (ZODL) ** 
- E guzobere ya na Jenụwarị 2026 site n'aka ndị mmepe mbụ nke usoro Zcash (isi ụlọ ọrụ ECC na ndị otu ngwaahịa) mgbe ha hapụsịrị Bootstrap / ECC. 
- Enweela ihe karịrị $25 nde na ego mbido site n'aka ndị na-etinye ego buru ibu gụnyere a16z Crypto na Coinbase Ventures. 
- The team, consisting of the original inventors and developers of the Zcash protocol, continues core protocol development, ZIP contributions, and privacy-focused tools including the Zodl mobile wallet (rebranded from Zashi).  
- No direct on-chain protocol funding; operates as a VC-backed independent lab focused on advancing Zcash privacy infrastructure.  
-> Lee profaịlụ zuru ezu: [ZODL](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/ZODL)  
-> Ebe nrụọrụ weebụ gọọmentị: [zodl.com](https://zodl.com/)
  
**Zcash Foundation (ZF) ** 
- Independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit focused on infrastructure, node software, research, and ecosystem health.  
- N'akụkọ ihe mere eme natara 5% nke ego dev. 
- No longer receives direct protocol funding post-NU6. Relies on donations and grants.  
- O nwere akara azụmaahịa Zcash (nke ECC nyere na 2019) ma na-arụ ọrụ dị mkpa na ọchịchị. 
- Na-agba ndị Zcash Community Advisory Panel (ZCAP) ma na-enyere aka n'ịkwado ntuli aka obodo. 
- Na-arụ ọrụ dị ka Key-Holder Organization n'okpuru atụmatụ ZIP 1016. 
-> Lee profaịlụ zuru ezu: [Zcash Foundation](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/zcash-foundation)  
-> Ebe nrụọrụ weebụ: [zfnd.org](https://zfnd.org/)

**Zcash Community Grants (ZCG) ** 
- The Zcash Community Grants program funds independent teams and projects to perform major ongoing development and other work for the public good of the Zcash ecosystem.  
- Kọmitii ndị obodo họpụtara na-ekpebi ego a ga-enye. 
- Continues to receive the full 8% of block rewards (post-NU6), administered through the Financial Privacy Foundation.  
- Grants are awarded through a transparent application and voting process open to the community.  
-> Lee profaịlụ zuru ezu: [Zcash Community Grants](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/zcash-community-grants)  
-> Ebe nrụọrụ weebụ: [zcashcommunitygrants.org/](https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/)

**Financial Privacy Foundation (FPF) ** 
- Otu nzukọ na-enweghị uru e debara aha ya na Cayman Islands. 
- Receives the 8% block subsidy allocation directly from the protocol (per ZIP 1015) and handles all legal, financial, and operational administration for the Zcash Community Grants program.  
- Ọ na-enye ihe owuwu nche anwụ na nkwado nchịkwa maka ọrụ ZCG, gụnyere ịkwụ ụgwọ, nkwekọrịta, na nnabata. 
- ZCG na-arụ ọrụ dị ka ụlọ ọrụ kwụụrụ onwe ya nke ndị obodo họpụtara n'okpuru nche anwụ FPF. 
-> Lee profaịlụ zuru ezu: [Financial Privacy Foundation](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/financial-privacy-foundation)  
-> Ebe nrụọrụ weebụ: [financialprivacyfoundation.org/](https://www.financialprivacyfoundation.org/)

**Ụlọ nyocha a na-eche nche** 
- Nzukọ nkwado Zcash na-enweghị onwe ya, nke a na-enye onyinye na Switzerland. 
- The first organization in the Zcash ecosystem that has never received direct or indirect funding from the Development Fund or block rewards.  
- Focuses on initiatives that benefit ZEC holders and prioritizes holder voice in shaping Zcash's direction.  
- Na-arụ ọrụ dị ka Key-Holder Organization n'okpuru ZIP 1016 maka nchịkwa nke Coinholder-Controlled Fund. 
- Na-enye aka na mmepe protocol, usoro ZIP, na ọchịchị (onye nchịkọta akụkọ ZIP). 
-> Lee profaịlụ zuru ezu: [Shielded Labs](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-organizations/shielded-labs)  
-> Ebe nrụọrụ weebụ: [shieldedlabs.net](https://shieldedlabs.net/)

## 3. Ọchịchị - Otú E Si Eme Mkpebi

Ọchịchị Zcash bụ ngwakọta nke "iwu usoro iwu n'elu" na "mmekọrịta mmekọrịta mmadụ na ibe ya":

1. ** Usoro ZIP (Atụmatụ Mmezi Zcash) ** 
   - Onye ọ bụla nwere ike itinye akwụkwọ ZIP. 
   - Mkparịta ụka ọha na eze na forums, Discord, GitHub. 
   - ZIP Editors (ugbu a Jack Grigg, Daira-Emma Hopwood, Kris Nuttycombe na ikike onye ọ bụla, Arya si ZF, na ndị nnọchi anya Shielded Labs) nyochaa ma kpebie nnabata. 
   - A nabatara ZIPs na nkwalite netwọk ọzọ.

2. **Nkwekọrịta ahia ahia (2019-2024) ** 
   - ECC nyere ZF akara azụmaahịa Zcash na 2019. 
   - Nkwekọrịta ahụ chọrọ nkwekọrịta nke ECC na ZF maka nkwalite netwọk ọ bụla na-emepụta usoro nkwekorita ọhụrụ. 
   - N'ọnwa Eprel afọ 2024, ECC kwupụtara ebumnuche ịkwụsị ọrụ ya; e nyere ọkwa nkwụsị ọrụ na Ọgọst 2024. 
   - Ka ọ na-erule afọ 2025, ZF bụ naanị onye nlekọta nke akara ngosi Zcash ma nakweere iwu ọhụụ ọhụụ ọhụụ na-egosipụta nchịkwa nke gburugburu ebe obibi.

3. **Zcash Community Advisory Panel (ZCAP) ** 
   - Otu ndị ọrụ afọ ofufo nke ndị ọkachamara n'ihe banyere gburugburu ebe obibi. 
   - A na-eji ya eme ntuliaka obodo na-ejighi iwu maka mkpebi ndị dị mkpa.

4. **On-chain Nkwado** 
   - Ozugbo emelitere netwọkụ, ọtụtụ n'ime ọnụọgụ hash nke netwọkụ ga-anabata ya (enweghị ihe ize ndụ nke ike ma ọ bụrụ na enwetara nkwekọrịta).

5. **Ọdịnihu Direction - The Lockbox & ZIP 1016** 
   - Akụ 12% nke igbe nchekwa na-agbakọta n'usoro iwu. 
   - ZIP 1016 proposes converting this into a Coinholder-Controlled Fund with quarterly coin-holder voting and multisig administration by Key-Holder Organizations (ZF and Shielded Labs currently noted).
