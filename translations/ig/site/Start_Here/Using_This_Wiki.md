# Iji Wiki a

Some of this might be overwhelming. We get it! There's a lot of information here. Here is a simple step-by-step guide that highlights how you should navigate the wiki.

## Ọhụrụ na Zcash

Ọ bụrụ na ị bụ onye ọhụrụ na Zcash, nke a bụ ebe dị mma iji nweta ọsọ ọsọ. Ihe mbụ ị ga - eme bụ lelee ibe ndị a:

[Zcash New User Guide](https://zechub.wiki/start-here/new-user-guide#content)

[Zcash Resources](https://zechub.wiki/start-here/zcash-resources#content)

[Zcash Basics](https://zechub.wiki/start-here/what-is-zec-and-zcash#content)

## Njikere iji Zcash

Mgbe ị gụsịrị akwụkwọ mmeghe, na ihe ndị metụtara ha, lelee [Iji ZEC](https://zechub.wiki/guides/using-zec-privately#content) na [Nkuzi](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PL6_epn0lASLFF28ePj7P5TvtMG_WE7p3O&si=4vleWUbCGbNJu1-M) vidiyo iji nweta mmeghe iji ZEC.

## Abụ m onye otu obodo Zcash

Ọ bụrụ na ị bụ onye otu obodo, ihe dị ịtụnanya! Ihe kachasị mma ị nwere ike ime iji nọrọ na ụbọchị bụ ịgbaso akwụkwọ akụkọ kwa izu [Zcash Ecosystem Digest](https://zechub.substack.com/s/zcash-ecosystem-digest) ma soro akaụntụ niile na [Ndepụta nke ibe obodo Zcash](https://zechub.wiki/zcash-community/community-links#content).

## Achọrọ m inye aka na ZecHub

Ọ dị mma! Biko gụọ [Gịnị bụ ZecHub](https://zechub.wiki/start-here/what-is-zechub#content) wee hụ [Nyere Mee ZecHub](https://zechub.wiki/contribute/help-build-zechub) ịmụta otú e si enye onyinye.
