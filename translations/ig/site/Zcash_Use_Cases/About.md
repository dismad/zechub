# Jiri Zcash na Real World

Zcash abụghị naanị ihe gbasara nzuzo n'echiche  ọ bụ maka ** nnwere onwe ego kwa ụbọchị **.

This section shows you **exactly how to use Zcash in real-life scenarios**, with step-by-step guides and best practices.

## Ihe Ị Ga-amụta

- Otú ị ga-esi chebe ego gị
- Otu esi zere mmejọ ndị a na-ahụkarị nke na-ekpughe data gị
- Etu esi etinye Zcash n'ọnọdụ ụwa n'ezie

## Otú E Si Eji Ihe Ndị A Eme Ihe

Akwụkwọ ọ bụla bụ:
- Nke dị mkpirikpi (nkeji 5-7)
- Nzọụkwụ-site-nzọụkwụ
- Ilekwasị anya na ụwa n'ezie 


Ọ bụrụ na ị bụ onye ọhụrụ, bido ebe a: [Gịnị bụ Zcash](/start-here/what-is-zec-and-zcash)
