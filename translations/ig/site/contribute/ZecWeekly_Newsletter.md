# Akwụkwọ akụkọ ZecWeekly

ZecWeekly is a newsletter that goes out every Sunday morning. It includes all the news that happened during the week in the Zcash ecosystem. The news is curated weekly by community members and all relevant links are added to the newsletter. Please subscribe to the newsletter [here](https://zechub.substack.com/).

## Na-enye aka

Newsletter contributions work best when one contributor prepares the edition for the correct week, follows the current bounty or coordination thread, and submits the pull request after the weekly links are ready. Please do not submit a future edition before ZecHub has posted or confirmed the date for that edition. Early pull requests often miss late-week updates, conflict with an assigned curator, or use the wrong deadline.

### 2. Gbanwee ebe nchekwa

Ọ bụrụ na ị bụ onye ọhụrụ na GitHub, jiri usoro ọrụ a:

1. Mepee [ZecHub nchekwa](https://github.com/ZecHub/zechub).
2. Pịa **Fork** ma mepụta fork n'okpuru akaụntụ GitHub gị.
3. Na forks gị, mepụta ngalaba ọhụrụ maka mbipụta ahụ. Aha ngalaba doro anya bara uru, dịka `digest-may-30-2026`.
4. Jide n'aka na arịrịọ ịdọpụ gị ga-elekwasị anya `ZecHub/zechub` dị ka isi nchekwa na `main` dị ka alaka nke isi.

Ọ bụrụ na ị jiri akara iwu, otu usoro ọrụ ahụ dị ka nke a:

```bash
git clone https://github.com/YOUR-USERNAME/zechub.git
cd zechub
git checkout -b digest-month-day-year
```

### 3. Mepụta faịlụ akwụkwọ akụkọ

Jiri [template akwụkwọ akụkọ](https://github.com/ZecHub/zechub/blob/main/newsletter/newslettertemplate.md) dị ka gị amalite. akwụkwọ akụkọ mbipụta na-n'ime [`newsletter`](https://github.com/ZecHub/zechub/tree/main/newsletter) folda.

Mgbe ị na- eke faịlụ:

- Tụnyere usoro aha faịlụ nke mbipụta ahụ rịọrọ ma ọ bụ jiri mbipụta ndị a nabatara na nso nso a.
- Debe otu usoro nkebi ahụ dị ka template ọ gwụla ma ọrụ ahụ rịọrọ maka usoro dị iche.
- Tinye njikọ site na izu dị mkpa naanị.
- Dee nkọwa dị nkenke ma doo anya maka njikọ ọ bụla ka ndị na-agụ ya wee ghọta ihe mere o ji dị mkpa.
- Na-asụgharị ma ọ bụ na-achịkọta isi mmalite ndị na-abụghị Bekee n'asụsụ Bekee mgbe ọ dị mkpa.
- Lelee njikọ ọ bụla tupu imeghe arịrịọ ịdọrọ.

### 4. Na-anakọta njikọ n'oge kwesịrị ekwesị

ZecWeekly normally covers the Zcash ecosystem activity for the current week and is published near the end of the week. The safest timing is:

- Bido ịnakọta njikọ mgbe e bipụtara akwụkwọ akụkọ ma ọ bụ ọrụ dị ugbu a.
- Na-edepụta ihe ndị ị ga-eme n'izu ahụ.
- Nyefee arịrịọ ịdọrọ ahụ nso na ụbọchị ntinye arịọrọ, mgbe ị nyochachara maka mmelite ngwụcha izu.
- Enyela akwụkwọ akụkọ maka izu na-abịa n'ihu tupu ọrụ maka ụbọchị ahụ adị ma ọ bụ tupu ZecHub ekwenye na ị kwesịrị ịkwadebe ya.

If an issue says to submit by a specific date, follow that date. If there is a conflict between this page and a current issue, follow the current issue.

### 5. Mepee arịrịọ ịdọrọ

Mgbe faịlụ akwụkwọ akụkọ gị dị njikere:

1. Tinye mgbanwe gị na ndụdụ gị.
2. Mepee arịrịọ ịdọrọ na `ZecHub/zechub` na `main` alaka.
3. Jiri aha dabara n'akwụkwọ ị na-agụ, dị ka `Zcash Ecosystem Digest | May 30th`.
4. Jikọọ nsogbu ahụ na arịrịọ ịdọrọ ka ndị nyocha wee nwee ike ijikọ ọrụ ahụ na ọrụ ahụ.

Ihe atụ nke arịrịọ ịdọrọ:

```md
Closes #ISSUE_NUMBER

Summary:
- Adds the Zcash Ecosystem Digest for Month Day.
- Uses the newsletter template and the current issue deadline.
- Checks links and descriptions for the requested week.
```

Mgbe arịrịọ ọpụpụ ahụ mepere, lelee maka nyocha nyocha. Ọ bụrụ na ZecHub rịọrọ maka ndezi, melite otu alaka ahụ kama ịmepe ịrịọ nke abụọ maka otu mbipụta ahụ.

### Ihe ndị mmadụ na-emekarị nke ị ga-ezere

- Ịmepe arịrịọ ịdọrọ tupu ụbọchị mbipụta ma ọ bụ ọrụ ekwenye.
- Na-arụ ọrụ na nsogbu nke nwere arịrịọ ịdọrọ jikọtara.
- Na-eziga arịrịọ ịdọrọ na ndụdụ gị kama `ZecHub/zechub`.
- Iji aha faịlụ na ezighi ezi ma ọ bụ itinye faịlụ ahụ n'èzí `newsletter` folda.
- Iṅomi mbipụta ochie n'emeghị ka ụbọchị, njikọ, na nkọwa ya nile dị ọhụrụ.
- Ịgbakwunye njikọ sitere n'izu na-ezighi ezi.
- Ịhapụ njikọ ndị mebiri emebi, njikọ abụọ, ma ọ bụ ederede ebe nchekwa site na ndebiri.
- Ịmepe arịrịọ ịdọrọ ọhụrụ mgbe nyochachara nkọwa kama imelite alaka mbụ.
