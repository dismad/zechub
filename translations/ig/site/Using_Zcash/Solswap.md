##  **Nzọụkwụ 4: Lelee Gas & Ụgwọ** 
- Maka **same-chain swaps**, jide n'aka na ị nwere oke nke akara gas (*ETH maka Ethereum, SOL maka Solana*). 
- ** Cross-chain swaps ** chọrọ gas na ma isi iyi na ebe yinye. 
- Nyochaa nkesa ụgwọ: 
  - Ụgha Ụgwọ: **0.85%** 
  - Gas nke Network 
  - Ụgwọ ndị na-eweta ọrụ nkwụsịtụ (~**0.3%**)

##  **Nzọụkwụ 5: Gbanwee Ntọala (Nhọrọ) ** 
Kpatụ ** Swap Settings** ka: 
- Gbanwee **slippage** (default **0.3%**, enwere ike idozi ya ruo 30%). 
- Mee ka **ụgwọ ndị dị mkpa** na netwọkụ jupụtara.
