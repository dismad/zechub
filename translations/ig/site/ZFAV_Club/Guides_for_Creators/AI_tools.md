# Ngwaọrụ AI: Ederede, Ihe oyiyi, Video, Audio na-emepụta (online)

Nke a bụ ndepụta iji mee ka ndụ dịkwuo mfe. Otú e si eji ha eme ihe bụ ihe ọzọ.

Ide ihe kachasị mma nwere usoro mmụta. Ọ ga-ekwe omume ịmụta isi ihe ngwa ngwa.

## Onye na-emepụta ederede / Ndị enyemaka

[Prompt analyzer](https://novelai.net/tokenizer) (tokenizer) iji mụta otú ihe ngosi si arụ ọrụ na ọtụtụ akara ngosi ha na-eji.

Ị nwere ike ịrịọ ndị enyemaka Chatbot mgbe niile ka ha nyere gị aka ịmepụta ezigbo mkpali maka gị.

### Ngwá ọrụ n'efu:

- [Anthropic si Claude](https://claude.ai/) - nwere ike ịchọ VPN ịdebanye aha (US naanị)
- [ChatGPT 3.5](https://chat.openai.com/) - na-arịọ maka nọmba ekwentị
- [Bing Chat (GPT4) ]](https://www.bing.com/search?q=Bing+AI&showconv=1&FORM=hpcodx) - ole na ole a na- agwa mmadụ ka o mee kwa ụbọchị
- [Llama 2 - 70B](https://www.llama2.ai/) - n'efu na webụsaịtị

### Ngwá ọrụ akwụ ụgwọ:

- [Free2Z Nkata2Z](https://free2z.com/ai) - Jiri ZEC / 2Zs kwụọ ụgwọ maka GPT-3.5 na GPT-4 cents/prompt
- [Kpọtụrụ GPT-4](https://chat.openai.com/auth/login) - $20 kwa ọnwa.
