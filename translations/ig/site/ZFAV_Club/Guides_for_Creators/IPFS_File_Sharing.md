# Ịkekọrịta faịlụ P2P na IPFS na Brave Browser

Anyị nwere ike iji IPFS kesaa nnukwu faịlụ mgbasa ozi n'etiti anyị n'ụzọ Peer-to-Peer.

Ya mere, gịnị bụ nsogbu: IPFS gateways anaghị arụ ọrụ maka faịlụ buru ibu, ha ga-egbu oge.

** Ihe achọrọ **: [IPFS Desktop](https://docs.ipfs.tech/install/ipfs-desktop/) + [Brave Browser](https://brave.com/)

Tldr, Vidio ahụ dị na njedebe.

Anyị nwere ike iji nke a n'etiti anyị na ZF AV Club maka ịnyefe faịlụ mgbasa ozi buru ibu ma dekọọ ya maka gị niile n'ihi na ọ na-atọ ụtọ ma dị oke mma !!

* PLUS *, anyị ga-arụkọ ọrụ ọnụ na ndị otu IPFS na Protocol Labs iji mepụta ikpe eji eme ihe a.
Uru ọzọ nke sitere na njikọ ndị e mere na dWeb Camp!

Without IPFS installed on the receiving machine AND using a browser that is not Brave: You will need to wait for a the gateway to load the file. *This will will return a 504 error on large files.*

Osote: Otu esi etinye faịlụ ndị ọzọ na igwe gị ka anyị wee ghara inwe nsogbu nsogbu nke ọdịda mgbe ọnụ mbụ ahụ na-apụ.
