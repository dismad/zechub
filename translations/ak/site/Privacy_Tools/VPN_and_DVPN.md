# VPN & dVPN na ɛyɛ adwuma

Wɔ dijitaal bere yi mu no, intanɛt so kokoam nsɛm ne ahobammɔ a wɔbɛkɔ so akura mu no ho hia sen biara. Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) ne decentralized VPNs (dVPNs) di dwuma titiriw wɔ wo intanɛt dwumadi ne data a wobɛbɔ ho ban mu. Saa wiki krataafa yi hwehwɛ nsonsonoeɛ titire a ɛda atetesɛm VPN ne dVPN a ɛreba ntam, ahobanbɔ ho nsusuiɛ berɛ a worepaw VPN, ne VPN dwumadie ahodoɔ a ɛgye cryptocurrency a ɛfa kokoamsɛm ho, Zcash.

# Dɛn Ne Nsonsonoe no?

__Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)__: Atetesɛm VPNs yɛ ɔkwan a ahobammɔ wom, a wɔabɔ no kokoam wɔ wo mfiri ne akyirikyiri server a VPN dwumadie no di so ntam. Saa kwan yi de wo intanɛt so dwumadi ahorow sie na aniwa a ɛhwehwɛ wo te sɛ hackers, ISPs, anaa aban adwumayɛbea ahorow. Wɔde VPNs di dwuma kɛse wɔ atirimpɔw ahorow te sɛ browsing a wɔmmɔ obiara din, nsɛm a wɔabara wɔ geo-restricted a wobenya, ne ahobammɔ a wɔbɔ fi cyber ahunahuna ho.

![mfonini223](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ZecHub/zechub/main/site/Privacy_Tools/assets/image-223.png)


__Decentralized VPNs (dVPNs)__: Nea ɛne eyi bɔ abira no, dVPNs de blockchain ne peer-to-peer mfiridwuma di dwuma de yɛ decentralized network of nodes. Wɔde wɔn a wɔde di dwuma no akwantu fa saa node ahorow yi so, na ɛma ɛyɛ den ma adwumakuw biako biara sɛ ɛbɛhwɛ anaa ahwɛ ntam nkitahodi no nyinaa so. dVPNs taa yɛ nea ɛko tia censorship kɛse, efisɛ tumidi biara nni hɔ a ɛwɔ mfinimfini a ɛhwɛ network no so. Wɔfata yiye ma wɔn a wɔde di dwuma a wɔrehwehwɛ kokoam nsɛm ne ahobammɔ a ɛkɔ anim.

# Ahobammɔ ho Nsusuwii Bere a Worepaw VPN

Sɛ worepaw VPN dwumadie a, ɛho hia sɛ wosusu ahobanbɔ ho nneɛma a ɛdidi soɔ yi ho:

1. __Encryption__: Hwɛ sɛ VPN no de encryption protocols a ɛyɛ den te sɛ OpenVPN anaa WireGuard di dwuma de bɔ wo data ho ban fi interception ho.

2. __No-Logs Policy__: Hwehwɛ providers a wɔwɔ no-logs policy a ɛyɛ katee, a ɛkyerɛ sɛ wɔmfa wo intanɛt dwumadi ahorow ho kyerɛwtohɔ nsie.

3. __Server Beaeɛ__: Susuw asasesin mu nkyekyɛmu a ɛwɔ server mmeaeɛ no ho na ama woanya nsɛm a ɛfiri mpɔtam ahodoɔ mu na woatew latency so.

4. __Kill Switch__: Kill switch bɛtwa wo intanɛt no mu sɛ VPN nkitahodi no kɔ fam a, na ɛremma wo data no ntumi nda adi.

5. __Kokoamsɛm ne Tumi__: Hwehwɛ VPN dwumadie no kokoam nsɛm ho nneyɛeɛ ne ne tumidi mu, ɛfiri sɛ aman ahodoɔ wɔ mmara ahodoɔ a ɛfa data a wɔde sie ho.

6. __Leak Protection__: Hwɛ sɛ VPN no siw DNS ne WebRTC leaks ano na ama woanya wo din.

7. __Boɔ ne Nneɛma a ɛwɔ mu__: Hwɛ ɛka ne nneɛma a wɔn a wɔde ma ahodoɔ de ma no mu na woanya bi a ɛne w’ahiadeɛ ne wo sikasɛm hyia.

# VPN ahorow a Egye Zcash Tom

Zcash (ZEC) yɛ cryptocurrency a wɔayɛ ama kokoam nsɛm a ɛkɔ anim, na ɛma ɛyɛ nea ankorankoro a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔbɔ wɔn din wɔ wɔn sikasɛm mu nkitahodi mu no ani gye ho. Bere a ɛnyɛ VPN dwumadie nyinaa na ɛgye Zcash tẽẽ no, ebinom betumi agye cryptocurrency sikatua denam ntamgyinafoɔ dwumadie so. Nanso, ɛho hia sɛ wo ne VPN dwumadie no hwehwɛ tẽẽ hwehwɛ sikatua akwan a ɛyɛ foforɔ paa. VPN dwumadie kakraa bi a wonim sɛ ɛgye cryptocurrency sikatua ni:

1. [__Mullvad VPN__ na ɛyɛ adwuma.](https://mullvad.net/en)
   
   Mullvad VPN yɛ VPN dwumadie a wɔbu no kɛseɛ a wonim no sɛ ɛde ne ho a emu yɛ den ma ɔdefoɔ kokoamsɛm ne ahobanbɔ. Ɛyɛ VPN a wɔde ma kakraa bi a wogye cryptocurrency no mu biako, a nea ɛka ho ne 
   Zcash, sɛ ɔkwan a wɔfa so tua ka. Mullvad no mu nneɛma atitiriw bi ne:

   __No-Logs Policy__: Mullvad di no-logs nhyehyeɛ a ɛyɛ katee akyi, a ɛkyerɛ sɛ ɛnkora data biara a ɛfa wo intanɛt dwumadiɛ ho.

   __Strong Encryption__: Ɔsom no de encryption protocols a ɛyɛ den di dwuma, a WireGuard ka ho, de bɔ wo data ho ban.

   __Multi-Hop VPN__: Mullvad de option ma multi-hop connections ma, a ɛde wo traffic fa server ahodoɔ pii so de ma kokoamsɛm ne ahobanbɔ tu mpɔn.

   __Bridge Mode__: Ɛwɔ bridge mode a ebetumi aboa ma wɔatwa censorship nhyehyɛe ahorow bi ho.

   __Anonymous Account Creation__: Wɔde akontaabu nɔma a wɔayɛ no kwa ma wɔn a wɔde di dwuma no, na ɛmma enhia sɛ wonya ankorankoro ho nsɛm bere a wɔrekyerɛw wɔn din no.

   __Zcash Payment__: Mullvad gye Zcash tom sɛ sikatua kwan, ɛma wɔn a wɔde di dwuma no tumi tua wɔn nkrataahyɛ ho ka denam kokoamsɛm a ɛkɔ anim so.Mullvad VPN.

3. [__Nym VPN (dVPN)__ Ɔde ne nsa kyerɛɛ ne so.](https://nymtech.net/)
   
   Nym VPN yɛ decentralized VPN (dVPN) a ɛtwe adwene si so sɛ ɛbɛma ɔdefoɔ kokoamsɛm ne ahobanbɔ akɔ anim denam kokoam nsɛm a ɛfa ntwamutam so. Nym VPN yɛ adwuma soronko wɔ atetesɛm VPN ahorow ho denam 
   utilizing a mixnet, baabi a wɔde user traffic fa nodes ntam. Nym VPN no mu nneɛma atitiriw bi ne:

   __Decentralization__: Nym VPN yɛ decentralized ne open-source, ɛtew asiane a ɛwɔ central control ne censorship mu.

   __Privacy Mixnet__: Ɔsom no de dwumadiefoɔ akwantuo fa mixnet so, na ɛma ɛyɛ den ma adwumakuo baako biara sɛ ɛbɛhwɛ anaa ahwehwɛ ntwamutam akwantuo mu.

   __Node Operation__: Wɔn a wɔde di dwuma no betumi ayɛ nodes adwuma de aboa wɔ ntwamutam no mu na wɔanya akatua, a ɛbɛma nnwuma no akɔ so ayɛ adwuma.

   __Strong Privacy__: Wɔayɛ Nym VPN a kokoamsɛm wɔ adwene mu, na ɛnhwehwɛ sɛ wɔn a wɔde di dwuma no de wɔn ankasa nsɛm bɛma bere a wɔreyɛ akontaabu no.
