# Zcash Podcast a ɛwɔ Digital Cash Network so no
![Nsɛm a wɔde kyerɛe_2023-01-22_21-50-14](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81990132/213973803-b0629c92-08d4-459b-99f1-594854a7db3a.png)

Zcash Podcast no gye nsɛm te sɛ updates, asɛmti pɔtee bi a emu dɔ, ne nea ɛkeka ho ma Zcash mpɔtam hɔfo a wɔwɔ hɔ dedaw no di na wɔkyɛ, na ɛsan nso ma hokwan ma Digital Cash Network channel akrafo a wɔwɔ hɔ dedaw no sɛ wobehu Zcash yiye. 

Ne titiriw no, saa kwan yi twe adwene si:

* adwene a ɛne sɛ wobɛtra ase a wonni banked off of cryptocurrency na wode decentralizing w’asetra nyinaa.
* curating a community a ɛda adi sɛ wɔn ani begye Zcash nnyinasosɛm ne mfiridwuma ho kɛse sɛ wɔde toto crypto atiefo a wɔkyekyem pɛpɛɛpɛ ho a.

Bio nso, live format no ma wonya abusuabɔ bi a wɔne atiefo no wɔ, a wotumi de wɔn ho hyɛ mu na wobisa nsɛm live fi ahɔhoyɛfo ne ahɔho no ho, a eye ma akyidifo foforo a wobedu wɔn nkyɛn, bere a wɔda so ara ma kwan ma wɔdi nsɛm a ɛwɔ form tenten no akyiri yi no.
