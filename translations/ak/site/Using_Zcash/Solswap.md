# **Sɛnea wobɛsesa ZEC wɔ Phantom Wallet mu**



![img1](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/SJOlnt-ceg.png)

---

##  **Anamɔn 1: Bue Swap Interface no** 
Fi ase **Phantom app** no na pia **Swap** bɔtn no so, . 
anaa kɔ **[solswap.org](https://solswap.org/)** fi wo Phantom browser no so. 


![img2](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/S1Cp-KWqxe.jpg)

---

##  **Anamɔn 2: Paw Networks ne Tokens a wode bɛto hɔ** 
- Paw wo **source network** (e.g., *Ethereum* anaa *Solana*) afei fa to hɔ ma sesa. 


![img3](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/S1SaGYZ9xx.jpg)

- Paw base token te sɛ **SOL, USDT, anaa USDC**. 
- Paw **ZEC** sɛ wo **destination token**. 
- Hwɛ sɛ Zcash nam swap interface no so wɔ hɔ. 



![img4 na ɛwɔ hɔ](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/ry4QQF-5gx.jpg)

---

##  **Anamɔn 3: Hyehyɛ Sika dodow & Nhwehwɛmu Quote** 
- Hyehyɛ sika dodow a wopɛ sɛ wosesa. 
- Phantom bɛda **sika a wɔabu akontaa sɛ wobenya** adi wɔ sika a wɔatua akyi. 


![img5](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/B1U1NYW5xe.jpg)

---

##  **Anamɔn 4: Hwɛ Gas & Fees** 
- Sɛ wopɛ **same-chain swaps** a, hwɛ hu sɛ wowɔ native gas token no a ɛdɔɔso (*ETH ma Ethereum, SOL ma Solana*). 
- **Cross-chain swaps** hwehwɛ gas wɔ source ne destination chains nyinaa so. 
- Hwɛ sɛnea wɔakyekyɛ sika a wɔbɔ no mu: 
  - Phantom Ka: **0.85%** na ɛyɛ adwuma. 
  - Network Gas a Wɔde Di Dwuma 
  - Bridging Ɔdemafoɔ Ka (~**0.3%**) . 
  
  
---

##  **Anamɔn 5: Sesa Nsiesiei (Wɔpɛ)** 
Kɔ **Sesa Nsiesiei** so kɔ: 
- Siesie **slippage** (default **0.3%**, wotumi sesa kosi 30%). 
- Ma **priority fees** nkɔ soro wɔ congested networks so. 

---

##  **Anamɔn 6: Si Swap so dua** 
- Hwɛ swap ho nsɛm nyinaa mu. 
- Klik **Swap Now** na fi ase yɛ adwuma no. 


![img6](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/HkU1UKZ5gx.jpg)

---

## **Anamɔn 7: Monitor Gyinabea** 
- Di wo swap no akyi wɔ **Nnansa yi Dwumadi** tab no mu. 
- Sɛ wopɛ cross-chain swaps a, fa wo **transaction ID** ne **Li.Fi Scanner** di dwuma ma bere ankasa mu nsɛm foforo. 


![img7](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/S1NBwKbcxe.jpg)
