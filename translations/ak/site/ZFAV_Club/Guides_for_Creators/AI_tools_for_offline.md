### Mfonini a wɔde yɛ adwuma

Ɛhe na wobɛtwe mfonini ahorow a wɔde bɛyɛ mfonini awo ntoatoaso? 
Wɔ [**Anim a Wɔreyɛ Atuu** so.](https://huggingface.co/models?pipeline_tag=text-to-image&sort=trending) ne [**Civitai, na ɛwɔ hɔ](https://civitai.com/)** yɛ pii a ɛsono model ahorow.

## Windows - download amanne kwan so mfonini ahorow

Mpɛn pii no, GPU a ɛba fam koraa a ɛwɔ 4GB anaa video memory na ɛho hia ma AI adwinnade biara.

Nvida GPU ahorow no yɛ adwuma yiye.

- [Frɛ AI](https://invoke-ai.github.io/InvokeAI/) (ɛhwehwɛ 4GB GPU memory)
- [NMKD Ntrɛwmu a Ɛgyina GUI GUI](https://nmkd.itch.io/t2i-gui)
- [Ɛyɛ mmerɛw sɛ wɔbɛtrɛw mu 3.0](https://github.com/easydiffusion/easydiffusion)

### Mac OS - download amanne kwan so mfonini ahorow

- [Twe Nneɛma: AI Awo Ntoatoaso](https://apps.apple.com/ee/app/draw-things-ai-generation/id6444050820) efi AppStore - a wontua hwee
(Ebetumi de StableDiffusion mfonini ahorow pii adi dwuma)
- [NtrɛwmuBee](https://diffusionbee.com/) - de ho
(Ebetumi de StableDiffusion mfonini ahorow pii adi dwuma)
- [Frɛ AI](https://invoke-ai.github.io/InvokeAI/) (ɛhwehwɛ 4GB GPU memory)

### Linux - download amanne kwan so mfonini ahorow

- [Frɛ AI](https://invoke-ai.github.io/InvokeAI/) (ɛhwehwɛ 4GB GPU memory)
