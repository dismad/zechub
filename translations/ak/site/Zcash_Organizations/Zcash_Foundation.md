# <img src="https://i.ibb.co/Nr9xqgX/image-2024-02-03-165803028.png" alt="Alt Text" width="50"/>  Zcash Foundation

## Asɛmpatrɛw Asɛm

Yɛn botaeɛ wɔ ZF ne sɛ yɛbɛbɔ nnwinnadeɛ a ɛboa ma nkitahodiɛ a ɛbue kɔ so, na ama obiara ne obiara atumi abɔ n’ankasa kokoamsɛm ho ban wɔ n’ankasa nsɛm so. Adeɛ titire a ɛwɔ kokoamsɛm mu ankasa ne sɛ wobɛtumi apaw deɛ ɛyɛ anaasɛ wɔmfa nkyɛ afoforo. Kokoamsɛm yɛ ahofadi ne pene a wɔpene so nyinaa; ɛho hia ma nnipa nidi ne ɔmanfo a wɔbɛkɔ so ayɛ adwuma yiye.

## Nnipa ne Akuw ahorow

Zcash Foundation no wɔ ~12 adwumayɛfoɔ a wɔkyekyɛ wɔn mu wɔ wiase nyinaa. Wɔde wɔn adwene si protocol nkɔsoɔ, protocol ahobanbɔ, protocol nnisoɔ, infrastructure nkɔsoɔ ne Zcash mpɔtam hɔ nhyiamu a wɔhwɛ so. Zcash Foundation no nnyɛ adwuma wɔ Zcash nkutoo so na enti ɛsan nso de ne ho hyɛ mfiridwuma a ɛkora kokoam nsɛm so ma [nnipa mmoa](https://zfnd.org/sai/) na ama ɔmanfo nyinaa yiyedi.

Zcash Foundation Grants ma abɔdeɛ a nkwa wom nhyehyɛeɛ no nya ɔkwan a wɔbɛfa so atua nnwuma a ɛboa kokoam nneɛma a nkwa wom te sɛ [Zebra](https://zebra.zfnd.org/) (node ​​dwumadie a ɛde ne ho ma Zcash) & [FROST](https://frost.zfnd.org/) saa ara nso na [ahorow ahorow](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2021/06/08/tor-project-receives-670k-from-zcash-open-major-grants-for-coding-language-upgrade/) nnwuma afoforo a ɛnam [Zcash Community Grants Committee so](https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/). 

Fapem no nso kura [Zcon](https://zfnd.org/zcon/), afe afe nhyiamu a wɔyɛ no ankasa a ɛtwe adwene si Zcash abɔdeɛ a nkwa wom so na ɛboa nnipa a wɔte aseɛ, mpɔtam hɔ nnwuma te sɛ [Zcon Vozes](https://zfnd.org/zcon-voices/), ɔmantam pɔtee bi [nhyiam ahorow](https://zfnd.org/zcash-dev-summits/), the Zcash Foundation A/V [Club](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/the-zero-knowledge-audiovisual-club/43733), mprempren Zero Knowledge Audio Visual [Klub](https://www.zkav.club/) ne mpɔtam [frɛ] afoforo ahorow a wɔabue ano](https://zfnd.org/arborist-calls/), mpɔtam hɔ [nsɛm a esisi](https://zfnd.org/zf-events/#:~:text=Zcomm) ne [AMA deɛ](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/community-call-with-the-zcg-candidates-december-2024/49707).

Zcash Foundation no de [Zcash Aguadi Agyiraehyɛde nhyehyɛe no di dwuma](https://zfnd.org/new-zcash-trademark-policy/) per the updated conditions of it's donation by the Electric Coin Co. which helps "Bɔ Zcash, ne nnwumakuo a ɛka ho, ne Zcash mpɔtam hɔfoɔ din ho ban" denam "kyerɛ(ing) aban amanneɛbɔ fibea, tew hokwan a ɛwɔ hɔ sɛ nnaadaa anaa nsɛm a ɛnteɛ so" ne "Identify(ing) official apps and services, reducing the likelihood of fraudulent activities. 

## Ahyehyɛde Nhyehyɛe

The Zcash Foundation is a [501(c)(3)](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/820707962) tow a wontua, a wonnya mfaso [adɔe adwuma](https://zfnd.org/#:~:text=Committed%20to%20Transparency). Wɔ wɔn [Team](https://zfnd.org/#:~:text=Our%20Team), wɔwɔ Board of [Akwankyerɛfo](https://zfnd.org/#:~:text=Board%20of%20Directors) na ama mpɔtam nnisoɔ abatoɔ ayɛ mmerɛ denam Zcash Community Advisory [Panel](https://zfnd.org/zcash-community-advisory-panel/).

## Sika a wɔde ma

Zcash Foundation no nnya sika mfi Zcash nkɔso [sikakorabea](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1014) as of November, 2024. Mprempren wɔde sika a wɔde bɛma The Foundation no nam atetesɛm, towtua a wotumi tew so [ntoboa](https://www.jotform.com/form/251874383638065). Yebetumi ahu sɛnea wɔde sikasɛm di dwuma wɔ wɔn asram abiɛsa biara [Transparency Reports](https://zfnd.org/category/transparency-reports/).

## Zcash Mpɔtam Hɔ Mmoa Dwumadie

[https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/](https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/)

Saa nhyehyeɛ yi ma akuo a wɔde wɔn ho no sika ma wɔyɛ nkɔsoɔ akɛseɛ a ɛkɔ so (anaa adwuma foforɔ) a ɛboa Zcash abɔdeɛ a nkwa wom. Nnipa baanum boayikuw a Zcash Mpɔtam Afotufo Kuw no paw wɔn na wɔpaw mmoa. Yɛhyɛ akuo ne ahyehyɛdeɛ a wɔn ani gye ho sɛ wɔbɛma Zcash dwumadie, ahobanbɔ, kokoamsɛm ne ne gye a wɔbɛgye atom no anya nkɔsoɔ denneennen sɛ wɔnbisa Zcash Community Grant!
